67 | 9 | 16 | Narrated `Aisha: I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Suppose you landed in a valley where there is a tree of which somethinghas been eaten and then you found trees of which nothing has been eaten, of which tree would you letyour camel graze?" He said, "(I will let my camel graze) of the one of which nothing has been eatenbefore." (The sub-narrator added: `Aisha meant that Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) had not married a virgin besidesherself .)
