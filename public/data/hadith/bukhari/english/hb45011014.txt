45 | 11 | 14 | Narrated Zaid bin Khalid: A bedouin asked the Prophet (ﷺ) about the Luqata. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Make public announcement about itfor one year and if then somebody comes and describes the container of the Luqata and the string itwas tied with, (give it to him); otherwise, spend it." He then asked the Prophet (ﷺ) about a lost camel. Theface of the Prophet (ﷺ) become red and he said, "You have o concern with it as it has its water reservoirand feet and it will reach water and drink and eat trees. Leave it till its owner finds it." He then askedthe Prophet (ﷺ) about a lost sheep. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "It is for you, for your brother, or for the wolf."
