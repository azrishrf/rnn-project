73 | 10 | 15 | Narrated `Aisha: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) entered upon me at Sarif while I was weeping (because I was afraid that I would notbe able to perform the ,Hajj). He said, "What is wrong with you? Have you got your period?" Ireplied, "Yes." He said, "This is a matter Allah has decreed for all the daughters of Adam, so performthe ceremonies of the Hajj as the pilgrims do, but do not perform the Tawaf around the Ka`ba."Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) slaughtered some cows as sacrifices on behalf of his wives.
