93 | 21 | 34 | Narrated `Ali bin Husain: Safiya bint (daughter of) Huyai came to the Prophet (in the mosque), and when she returned (home),the Prophet (ﷺ) accompanied her. It happened that two men from the Ansar passed by them and theProphet called them saying, "She is Safiya!" those two men said, "Subhan Allah!" The Prophet (ﷺ) said,"Satan circulates in the human body as blood does."
