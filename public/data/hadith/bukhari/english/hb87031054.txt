87 | 31 | 54 | Narrated Abu Juhaifa: I asked `Ali "Do you have anything Divine literature besides what is in the Qur'an?" Or, as Uyainaonce said, "Apart from what the people have?" `Ali said, "By Him Who made the grain split(germinate) and created the soul, we have nothing except what is in the Qur'an and the ability (gift) ofunderstanding Allah's Book which He may endow a man, with and what is written in this sheet ofpaper." I asked, "What is on this paper?" He replied, "The legal regulations of Diya (Blood-money)and the (ransom for) releasing of the captives, and the judgment that no Muslim should be killed inQisas (equality in punishment) for killing a Kafir (disbeliever).
