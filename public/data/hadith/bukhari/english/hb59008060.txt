59 | 8 | 60 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "The people of Paradise will look at the dwellers of the lofty mansions (i.e. asuperior place in Paradise) in the same way as one looks at a brilliant star far away in the East or in theWest on the horizon; all that is because of their superiority over one another (in rewards)." On that thepeople said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Are these lofty mansions for the prophets which nobody else canreach? The Prophet (ﷺ) replied," No! "By Allah in whose Hands my life is, these are for the men whobelieved in Allah and also believed in the Apostles."
