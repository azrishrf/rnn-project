30 | 24 | 37 | Narrated Zainab: (daughter of Um Salama) that her mother said, "While I was (lying) with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) underneatha woolen sheet, I got the menstruation, and then slipped away and put on the clothes (which I used towear) in menses. He asked, "What is the matter? Did you get your menses?" I replied in theaffirmative and then entered underneath that woolen sheet. I and Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) used to take a bathfrom one water pot and he used to kiss me while he was fasting."
