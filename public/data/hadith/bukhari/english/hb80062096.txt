80 | 62 | 96 | Narrated Ibn Abi Mulaika: `Aisha said, "The Jews came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and said to him, "As-Samu 'Alaika (i.e., Death be uponyou)." He replied, 'The same on you.' " `Aisha said to them, "Death be upon you, and may Allah curseyou and shower His wrath upon you!" Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) I said, "Be gentle and calm, O `Aisha! Begentle and beware of being harsh and of saying evil things." She said, "Didn't you hear what theysaid?" He said, "Didn't you hear what I replied (to them)? have returned their statement to them, andmy invocation against them will be accepted but theirs against me will not be accepted."
