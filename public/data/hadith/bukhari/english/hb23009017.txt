23 | 9 | 17 | Narrated Um 'Atiyya: Aiyub said that Hafsa narrated to him a narration similar to that of Muhammad inwhich it was said that the bath was to be given for an odd number of times, and the numbers 3, 5 or 7were mentioned. It was also said that they were to start with the right side and with the parts whichwere washed in ablution, and that Um 'Atiyya also mentioned, "We combed her hair and divided themin three braids."
