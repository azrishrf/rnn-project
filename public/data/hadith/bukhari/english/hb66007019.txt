66 | 7 | 19 | Narrated Abu-Huraira: Gabriel used to repeat the recitation of the Qur'an with the Prophet (ﷺ) once a year, but he repeated ittwice with him in the year he died. The Prophet (ﷺ) used to stay in I`tikaf for ten days every year (in themonth of Ramadan), but in the year of his death, he stayed in I`tikaf for twenty days.
