18 | 5 | 10 | Narrated `Aisha: "When the prayers were first enjoined they were of two rak`at each. Later the prayer in a journey waskept as it was but the prayers for non-travelers were completed." Az-Zuhri said, "I asked `Urwa whatmade Aisha pray the full prayers (in journey)." He replied, "She did the same as `Uthman did."
