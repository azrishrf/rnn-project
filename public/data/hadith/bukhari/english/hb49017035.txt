49 | 17 | 35 | Narrated Abu Huraira and Zaid bin Khalid: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "If a slave-girl (Ama) commits illegal sexual intercourse, scourge her; if she does itagain, scourge her again; if she repeats it, scourge her again." The narrator added that on the third orthe fourth offense, the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Sell her even for a hair rope."
