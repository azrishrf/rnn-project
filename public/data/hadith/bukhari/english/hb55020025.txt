55 | 20 | 25 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: That the mother of Sa`d bin Ubada the brother of Bani Saida died in Sa`d's absence, so he came to theProphet saying, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! My mother died in my absence, will it benefit her if I give incharity on her behalf?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Yes." Sa`d said, "I take you as my witness that I give mygarden Al-Makhraf in charity on her behalf."
