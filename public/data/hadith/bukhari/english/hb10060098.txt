10 | 60 | 98 | Narrated `Amr: Jabir bin `Abdullah said, "Mu`adh bin Jabal used to pray with the Prophet (ﷺ) and then go to lead hispeople in prayer Once he led the `Isha' prayer and recited Surat "Al-Baqara." Somebody left theprayer and Mu`adh criticized him. The news reached the Prophet (ﷺ) and he said to Mu`adh, 'You areputting the people to trial,' and repeated it thrice (or said something similar) and ordered him to recitetwo medium Suras of Mufassal." (`Amr said that he had forgotten the names of those Suras).
