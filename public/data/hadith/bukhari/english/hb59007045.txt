59 | 7 | 45 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "On the night of my Ascent to the Heaven, I saw Moses who was a tall brown curlyhairedman as if he was one of the men of Shan'awa tribe, and I saw Jesus, a man of medium heightand moderate complexion inclined to the red and white colors and of lank hair. I also saw Malik, thegate-keeper of the (Hell) Fire and Ad-Dajjal amongst the signs which Allah showed me." (TheProphet then recited the Holy Verse): "So be not you in doubt of meeting him' when you met Mosesduring the night of Mi'raj over the heavens" (32.23)Narrated Anas and Abu Bakra: "The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "The angels will guard Medina from Ad-Dajjal(who will not be able to enter the city of Medina).
