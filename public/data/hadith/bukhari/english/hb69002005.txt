69 | 2 | 5 | Narrated Abu Huraira: "The Prophet (ﷺ) said, 'The best alms is that which is given when one is rich, and a giving hand is betterthan a taking one, and you should start first to support your dependents.' A wife says, 'You shouldeither provide me with food or divorce me.' A slave says, 'Give me food and enjoy my service." A sonsays, "Give me food; to whom do you leave me?" The people said, "O Abu Huraira! Did you hear thatfrom Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ?" He said, "No, it is from my own self."
