23 | 25 | 38 | Narrated Sa`d from his father: Once the meal of `Abdur-Rahman bin `Auf was brought in front of him, and he said, "Mus`ab bin`Umair was martyred and he was better than I, and he had nothing except his Burd (a black squarenarrow dress) to be shrouded in. Hamza or another person was martyred and he was also better than Iand he had nothing to be shrouded in except his Burd. No doubt, I fear that the rewards of my deedsmight have been given early in this world." Then he started weeping.
