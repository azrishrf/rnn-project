77 | 58 | 99 | Narrated `Aisha: A necklace belonging to Asma' was lost, and the Prophet (ﷺ) sent men in its search. The time for theprayer became due and they were without ablution and they could not find water; therefore theyprayed without ablution, They mentioned that to the Prophet (ﷺ) . Then Allah revealed the Verse ofTayammum. (`Aisha added: that she had borrowed (the necklace) from Asma').
