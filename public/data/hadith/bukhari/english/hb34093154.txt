34 | 93 | 154 | Narrated Humaid: Anas said, "The Prophet (ﷺ) forbade the selling of dates till they were almost ripe." We asked Anas,"What does 'almost ripe' mean?" He replied, "They get red and yellow. The Prophet (ﷺ) added, 'If Allahdestroyed the fruits present on the trees, what right would the seller have to take the money of hisbrother (somebody else)?' "
