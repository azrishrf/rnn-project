92 | 18 | 53 | Narrated Abu Wail: Abu Musa and Abii Mas`ud went to `Ammar when `Ali had sent him to Kufa to exhort them to fight(on `Ali's side). They said to him, "Since you have become a Muslim, we have never seen you doing adeed more criticizable to us than your haste in this matter." `Ammar said, "Since you (both) becameMuslims, I have never seen you doing a deed more criticizable to me than your keeping away fromthis matter." Then Abu Mas`ud provided `Ammar and Abu Musa with two-piece outfits to wear, andone of them went to the mosque (of Kufa).
