77 | 8 | 13 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: A man asked, "O Allah s Apostle What kind of clothes should a Muhrim wear?" The Prophet, said, "AMuhrim should not wear a shirt, trousers a hooded cloak, or Khuffs (socks made from thick fabric orleather) unless he cannot get sandals, in which case he should cut the part (of the Khuff) that coversthe ankles."
