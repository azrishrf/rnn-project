96 | 14 | 45 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "You will follow the ways of those nations who were before you, span by span andcubit by cubit (i.e., inch by inch) so much so that even if they entered a hole of a mastigure, youwould follow them." We said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! (Do you mean) the Jews and the Christians?" Hesaid, "Whom else?"
