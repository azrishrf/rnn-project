86 | 11 | 16 | Narrated `Aisha: Usama approached the Prophet (ﷺ) on behalf of a woman (who had committed theft). The Prophet (ﷺ) said,"The people before you were destroyed because they used to inflict the legal punishments on the poorand forgive the rich. By Him in Whose Hand my soul is! If Fatima (the daughter of the Prophet (ﷺ) ) didthat (i.e. stole), I would cut off her hand."
