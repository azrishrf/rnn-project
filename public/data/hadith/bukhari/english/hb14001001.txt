14 | 1 | 1 | Narrated `A'isha: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) used to pray eleven rak`at at night and that was his night prayer and each of hisprostrations lasted for a period enough for one of you to recite fifty verses before Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)raised his head. He also used to pray two rak`at (Sunnah) before the (compulsory) Fajr prayer and thenlie down on his right side till the Mu'adh-dhin came to him for the prayer.
