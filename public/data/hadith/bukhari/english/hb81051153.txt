81 | 51 | 153 | Narrated Anas: Haritha was martyred on the day (of the battle) of Badr while he wasyoung. His mother came to the Prophet (ﷺ) saying, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Youknow the relation of Haritha to me (how fond of him I was); so, if heis in Paradise, I will remain patient and wish for Allah's reward, butif he is not there, then you will see what I will do." The Prophet (ﷺ)replied, "May Allah be merciful upon you! Have you gone mad? (Do youthink) it is one Paradise? There are many Paradises and he is in the(most superior) Paradise of Al-Firdaus."
