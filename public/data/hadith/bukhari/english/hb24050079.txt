24 | 50 | 79 | Narrated Az-Zubair bin Al-`Awwam: The Prophet (p.b.u.h) said, "It is better for anyone of you to take a rope (and cut) and bring a bundle ofwood (from the forest) over his back and sell it and Allah will save his face (from the Hell-Fire)because of that, rather than to ask the people who may give him or not."
