41 | 3 | 3 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Whoever keeps a dog, one Qirat of the reward of his good deeds is deducteddaily, unless the dog is used for guarding a farm or cattle." Abu Huraira (in another narration) saidfrom the Prophet, "unless it is used for guarding sheep or farms, or for hunting." Narrated Abu Hazimfrom Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "A dog for guarding cattle or for hunting."
