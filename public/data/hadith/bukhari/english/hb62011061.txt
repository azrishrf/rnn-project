62 | 11 | 61 | Narrated Anas: Whenever there was drought, `Umar bin Al-Khattab used to ask Allah for rain through Al-`Abbas bin`Abdul Muttalib, saying, "O Allah! We used to request our Prophet to ask You for rain, and Youwould give us. Now we request the uncle of our Prophet to ask You for rain, so give us rain." Andthey would be given rain."
