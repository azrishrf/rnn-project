46 | 4 | 5 | Narrated Anas: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Help your brother, whether he is an oppressor or he is an oppressed one. Peopleasked, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! It is all right to help him if he is oppressed, but how should we help him ifhe is an oppressor?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "By preventing him from oppressing others."
