93 | 30 | 46 | Narrated `Abdullah: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "If somebody on the demand of a judge takes an oath to grab (a Muslim's) propertyand he is liar in it, he will meet Allah Who will be angry with him". So Allah revealed,:--'Verily! those who purchase a small gain at the cost of Allah's Covenant and their oaths..' (3.77) 'Al-Ashath came while `Abdullah was narrating (this) to the people. Al-Ashath said, "This verse wasrevealed regarding me and another man with whom I had a quarrel about a well. The Prophet (ﷺ) said (tome), "Do you have any evidence?' I replied, 'No.' He said, 'Let your opponent take an oath.' I said: Iam sure he would take a (false) oath." Thereupon it was revealed: 'Verily! those who purchase a smallgain at the cost of Allah's Covenant....' (3.77)
