68 | 2 | 2 | Narrated Anas bin Seereen: Ibn `Umar said: "I divorced my wife while she was menstruating. `Umar mentioned that to theProphet . The Prophet (ﷺ) said, (to my father), "Let your son take her back." I asked (Ibn `Umar), "Is sucha divorce counted (i.e. as one legal divorce)?" Ibn `Umar said, "Of course." Narrated Yunus binJubair: Ibn `Umar said, "The Prophet (ﷺ) said to `Umar, 'Order him (Ibn `Umar) to take her back.' " Iasked, "Is such a divorce counted (as one legal divorce)?" Ibn `Umar said, "What do you think ifsomeone becomes helpless and foolish?"
