4 | 44 | 60 | Narrated Jabir: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) came to visit me while I was sick and unconscious. He performed ablution andsprinkled the remaining water on me and I became conscious and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! To whomwill my inheritance go as I have neither ascendants nor descendants?" Then the Divine versesregarding Fara'id (inheritance) were revealed.
