55 | 18 | 22 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: Some people claim that the order in the above Verse is cancelled, by Allah, it is not cancelled, but thepeople have stopped acting on it. There are two kinds of guardians (who are in charge of theinheritance): One is that who inherits; such a person should give (of what he inherits to the relatives,the orphans and the needy, etc.), the other is that who does not inherit (e.g. the guardian of theorphans): such a person should speak kindly and say (to those who are present at the time ofdistribution), "I can not give it to you (as the wealth belongs to the orphans).
