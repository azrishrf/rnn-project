70 | 57 | 90 | Narrated Abu Mas`ud Al-Ansari: There was an Ansari man nicknamed, Abu Shu'aib, who had a slave who was a butcher. He came tothe Prophet (ﷺ) while he was sitting with his companions and noticed the signs of hunger on the face ofthe Prophet (ﷺ) . So he went to his butcher slave and said, "Prepare for me a meal sufficient for fivepersons so that I may invite the Prophet (ﷺ) along with four other men." He had the meal prepared for himand invited him. A (sixth) man followed them. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "O Abu Shu'aib! Another man hasfollowed us. If you wish, you may invite him; and if you wish, you may refuse him." Abu Shu'aibsaid, "No, I will admit him."
