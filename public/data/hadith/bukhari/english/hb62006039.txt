62 | 6 | 39 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: The Prophet (ﷺ) ascended the mountain of Uhud and he was accompanied by Abu Bakr, `Umar and`Uthman. The mountain shook beneath them. The Prophet (ﷺ) hit it with his foot and said, "O Uhud ! Befirm, for on you there is none but a Prophet, a Siddiq and a martyr (i.e. and two martyrs).
