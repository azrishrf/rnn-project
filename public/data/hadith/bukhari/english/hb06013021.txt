6 | 13 | 21 | Narrated `Aisha: A woman asked the Prophet (ﷺ) about the bath which is taken after finishing from the menses. The Prophet (ﷺ)told her what to do and said, "Purify yourself with a piece of cloth scented with musk." The womanasked, "How shall I purify myself with it" He said, "Subhan Allah! Purify yourself (with it)." I pulledher to myself and said, "Rub the place soiled with blood with it."
