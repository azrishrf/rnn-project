63 | 3 | 5 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Ansar said (to the Prophet (ﷺ) ), "Please divide the date-palm trees between us and them (i.e.emigrants)." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "No." The Ansar said, "Let them (i.e. the emigrants) do the labor for usin the gardens and share the date-fruits with us." The emigrants said, "We accepted this."
