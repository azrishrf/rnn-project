61 | 25 | 126 | Narrated Ibn Mas`ud: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Soon others will be preferred to you, and there will be things which you will notlike." The companions of the Prophet (ﷺ) asked, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! What do you order us to do (in thiscase)? " He said, "(I order you) to give the rights that are on you and to ask your rights from Allah."
