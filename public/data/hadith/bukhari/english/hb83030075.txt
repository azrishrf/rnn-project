83 | 30 | 75 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: A man came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and said to him, "My sister vowed to perform the Hajj, but she died(before fulfilling it)." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Would you not have paid her debts if she had any?" The mansaid, "Yes." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "So pay Allah's Rights, as He is more entitled to receive His rights."
