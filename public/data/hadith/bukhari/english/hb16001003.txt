16 | 1 | 3 | Narrated Al-Mughira bin Shu`ba: "The sun eclipsed in the lifetime of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) on the day when (his son) Ibrahim died. So thepeople said that the sun had eclipsed because of the death of Ibrahim. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "The sunand the moon do not eclipse because of the death or life (i.e. birth) of someone. When you see theeclipse pray and invoke Allah."
