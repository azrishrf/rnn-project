58 | 9 | 16 | Narrated Um Hani: the daughter of Abu Talib: I went to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) on the day of the conquest of Mecca and foundhim taking a bath, and his daughter Fatima was screening him. I greeted him and he asked, "Who isthat?" I said, "I, Um Hani bint Abi Talib." He said, "Welcome, O Um Hani." When he had finished hisbath, he stood up and offered eight rak`at while dressed in one garment. I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)!My brother `Ali has declared that he will kill a man to whom I have granted asylum. The man is soand-so bin Hubaira." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "O Um Hani! We will grant asylum to the one whom youhave granted asylum." (Um Hani said, "That (visit) took place in the Duha (i.e. forenoon)).
