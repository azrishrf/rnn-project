34 | 35 | 51 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: `Ukaz, Majanna and Dhul-Majaz were markets in the Pre-Islamic period. When the people embracedIslam they considered it a sin to trade there. So, the following Holy Verse came:-- 'There is no harmfor you if you seek of the bounty of your Lord (Allah) in the Hajj season." (2.198) Ibn `Abbas recitedit like this.
