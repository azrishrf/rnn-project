77 | 92 | 174 | Narrated Abu Talha: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Angels (of mercy) do not enter a house where there are pictures.'" The subnarratorBusr added: "Then Zaid fell ill and we paid him a visit. Behold! There was, hanging at hisdoor, a curtain decorated with a picture. I said to 'Ubaidullah Al-Khaulani, the step son ofMaimuna, the wife of the Prophet (ﷺ) , "Didn't Zaid tell us about the picture the day before yesterday?"'Ubaidullah said, "Didn't you hear him saying: 'except a design in a garment'?"
