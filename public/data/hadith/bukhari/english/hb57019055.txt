57 | 19 | 55 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: While I was walking with the Prophet (ﷺ) who was wearing a Najrani outer garment with a thick hem, abedouin came upon the Prophet (ﷺ) and pulled his garment so violently that I could recognize the impressof the hem of the garment on his shoulder, caused by the violence of his pull. Then the bedouin said,"Order for me something from Allah's Fortune which you have." The Prophet (ﷺ) turned to him andsmiled, and ordered that a gift be given to him.
