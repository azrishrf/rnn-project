4 | 46 | 65 | Narrated `Amr bin Yahya: (on the authority of his father) My uncle used to perform ablution extravagantly and once he asked`Abdullah bin Zaid to tell him how he had seen the Prophet (ﷺ) performing ablution. He asked for anearthenware pot containing water, and poured water from it on his hands and washed them thrice, andthen put his hand in the earthenware pot and rinsed his mouth and washed his nose by putting water init and then blowing it out thrice with one handful of water; he again put his hand in the water andtook a handful of water and washed his face thrice, then washed his hands up to the elbows twice, andtook water with his hand, and passed it over his head from front to back and then from back to front,and then washed his feet (up to the ankles) and said, "I saw the Prophet (ﷺ) performing ablution in thatway."
