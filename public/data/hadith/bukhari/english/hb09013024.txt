9 | 13 | 24 | Narrated Abu Bakr bin `Uthman bin Sahl bin Hunaif: that he heard Abu Umama saying: We prayed the Zuhr prayer with `Umar bin `Abdul `Aziz and thenwent to Anas bin Malik and found him offering the `Asr prayer. I asked him, "O uncle! Which prayerhave you offered?" He said 'The `Asr and this is (the time of) the prayer of Allah s Apostle which weused to pray with him."
