19 | 16 | 29 | Narrated Abu Salma bin `Abdur Rahman: I asked `Aisha, "How is the prayer of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) during the month of Ramadan." She said,"Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) never exceeded eleven rak`at in Ramadan or in other months; he used to offer fourrak`at-- do not ask me about their beauty and length, then four rak`at, do not ask me about their beautyand length, and then three rak`at." Aisha further said, "I said, 'O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Do you sleep beforeoffering the witr prayer?' He replied, 'O `Aisha! My eyes sleep but my heart remains awake'!"
