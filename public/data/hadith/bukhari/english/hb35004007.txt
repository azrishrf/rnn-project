35 | 4 | 7 | Narrated Abu Al-Bakhtari: I asked Ibn `Umar about Salam (the fruits of) date-palms. He replied, "The Prophet (ﷺ) forbade the sale ofdates till their benefit becomes evident and fit for eating and also the sale of silver (for gold) oncredit." I asked Ibn `Abbas about Salam for dates and he replied, "The Prophet (ﷺ) forbade the sale ofdates till they were fit for eating and could be estimated."
