5 | 15 | 25 | Narrated Hisham bin `Urwa: (on the authority of his father) `Aisha said, "Whenever Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) took the bath of Janaba, hecleaned his hands and performed ablution like that for prayer and then took a bath and rubbed his hair,till he felt that the whole skin of the head had become wet, then he would pour water thrice and washthe rest of the body." `Aisha further said, "I and Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) used to take a bath from a singlewater container, from which we took water simultaneously."
