34 | 49 | 71 | Narrated `Aisha: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "An army will invade the Ka`ba and when the invaders reach Al-Baida', all theground will sink and swallow the whole army." I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! How will they sink into theground while amongst them will be their markets (the people who worked in business and notinvaders) and the people not belonging to them?" The Prophet (ﷺ) replied, "all of those people will sinkbut they will be resurrected and judged according to their intentions."
