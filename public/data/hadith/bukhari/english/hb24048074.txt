24 | 48 | 74 | Narrated Zainab: (the daughter of Um Salama) My mother said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Shall I receive a reward if I spendfor the sustenance of Abu Salama's offspring, and in fact they are also my sons?" The Prophet (ﷺ) replied,"Spend on them and you will get a reward for what you spend on them."
