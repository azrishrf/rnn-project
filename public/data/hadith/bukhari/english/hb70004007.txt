70 | 4 | 7 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: A tailor invited Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) to a meal which he had prepared. I went along with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)and saw him seeking to eat the pieces of gourd from the various sides of the dish. Since that day Ihave liked to eat gourd. `Umar bin Abi Salama said: The Prophet, said to me, "Eat with your righthand."
