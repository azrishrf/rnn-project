28 | 25 | 36 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Abbas: I came riding on my she-ass and had (just) then attained the age of puberty. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) waspraying at Mina. I passed in front of a part of the first row and then dismounted from it, and theanimal started grazing. I aligned with the people behind Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) (The sub-narrator added thathappened in Mina during the Prophet's Hajjat-al-Wada`.)
