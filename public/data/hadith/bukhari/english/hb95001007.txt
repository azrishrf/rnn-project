95 | 1 | 7 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) finished his prayer after offerings two rak`at only. Dhul-Yaddain asked him whetherthe prayer had been reduced, or you had forgotten?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Is Dhul-Yaddain speaking thetruth?" The people said, "Yes." Then Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) stood up and performed another two rak`at andthen finished prayer with Taslim, and then said the Takbir and performed a prostration similar to orlonger than his ordinary prostrations; then he raised his head, said Takbir and prostrated and thenraised his head (Sahu prostrations).
