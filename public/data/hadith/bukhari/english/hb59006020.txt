59 | 6 | 20 | Narrated Abu Dhar: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Gabriel said to me, 'Whoever amongst your followers die without havingworshipped others besides Allah, will enter Paradise (or will not enter the (Hell) Fire)." The Prophet (ﷺ)asked. "Even if he has committed illegal sexual intercourse or theft?" He replied, "Even then."
