15 | 3 | 6 | Narrated Anas: Whenever drought threatened them, `Umar bin Al-Khattab, used to ask Al-Abbas bin `Abdul Muttalibto invoke Allah for rain. He used to say, "O Allah! We used to ask our Prophet to invoke You for rain,and You would bless us with rain, and now we ask his uncle to invoke You for rain. O Allah ! Blessus with rain."(1) And so it would rain.
