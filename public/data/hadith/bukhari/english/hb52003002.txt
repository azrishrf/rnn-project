52 | 3 | 2 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and Ubai bin Ka`b Al-Ansari went to the garden where Ibn Saiyad used to live. WhenAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) entered (the garden), he (i.e. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ) started hiding himself behind the date108palms as he wanted to hear secretly the talk of Ibn Saiyad before the latter saw him. Ibn Saiyadwrapped with a soft decorated sheet was lying on his bed murmuring. Ibn Saiyad's mother saw theProphet hiding behind the stems of the date-palms. She addressed Ibn Saiyad saying, "O Saf, this isMuhammad." Hearing that Ibn Saiyad stopped murmuring (or got cautious), the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "If shehad left him undisturbed, he would have revealed his reality." (See Hadith No. 290, Vol 4 for details)
