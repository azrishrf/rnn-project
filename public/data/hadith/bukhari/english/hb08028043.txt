8 | 28 | 43 | Narrated Abu Aiyub Al-Ansari: Narrated Maimun bin Siyah that he asked Anas binMalik, "O Abu Hamza! What makes the life and property of a person sacred?" He replied, "Whoeversays, 'None has the right to be worshipped but Allah', faces our Qibla during the prayers, prays like usand eats our slaughtered animal, then he is a Muslim, and has got the same rights and obligations asother Muslims have."
