40 | 12 | 13 | Narrated `Amr: Concerning the Waqf of `Umar: It was not sinful of the trustee (of the Waqf) to eat or provide hisfriends from it, provided the trustee had no intention of collecting fortune (for himself). Ibn `Umarwas the manager of the trust of `Umar and he used to give presents from it to those with whom heused to stay at Mecca.
