62 | 1 | 3 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: "Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "A time will come upon the people, when a group of people will wage a holywar and it will be said, 'Is there amongst you anyone who has accompanied Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?' Theywill say, 'Yes.' And so victory will be bestowed on them. Then a time will come upon the people whena group of people will wage a holy war, and it will be said, "Is there amongst you anynone who hasaccompanied the companions of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?' They will say, 'Yes.' And so victory will bebestowed on them. Then a time will come upon the people when a group of people will wage a holywar, and it will be said, "Is there amongst you anyone who has been in the company of thecompanions of the companions of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ?' They will say, 'Yes.' And victory will bebestowed on them."
