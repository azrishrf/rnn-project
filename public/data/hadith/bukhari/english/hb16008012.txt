16 | 8 | 12 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Amr: When the sun eclipsed in the lifetime of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and an announcement was made that theprayer was to be held in congregation. The Prophet (ﷺ) performed two bowing in one rak`a. Then he stoodup and performed two bowing in one rak`a. Then he sat down and finished the prayer; and by then the(eclipse) had cleared `Aisha said, "I had never performed such a long prostration."
