24 | 61 | 100 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The Prophet (ﷺ) saw a dead sheep which had been given in charity to a freed slave-girl of Maimuna, thewife of the Prophet (ﷺ) . The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Why don't you get the benefit of its hide?" They said, "It isdead." He replied, "Only to eat (its meat) is illegal."
