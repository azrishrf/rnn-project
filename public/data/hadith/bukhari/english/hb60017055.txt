60 | 17 | 55 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: When Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) landed at Al-Hijr during the Ghazwa of Tabuk, he ordered his companions notto drink water from its well or reserve water from it. They said, "We have already kneaded the doughwith its water. and also filled our bags with its water.'' On that, the Prophet (ﷺ) ordered them to throwaway the dough and pour out the water.
