13 | 18 | 30 | Narrated `Abdur Rahman bin `Abis: Ibn `Abbas was asked whether he had joined the Prophet (ﷺ) in the `Id prayer. He said, "Yes. And I couldnot have joined him had I not been young. (The Prophet (ﷺ) came out) till he reached the mark which wasnear the house of Kathir bin As-Salt, offered the prayer, delivered the Khutba and then went towardsthe women. Bilal was accompanying him. He preached to them and advised them and ordered them togive alms. I saw the women putting their ornaments with their outstretched hands into Bilal's garment.Then the Prophet (ﷺ) along with Bilal returned home.
