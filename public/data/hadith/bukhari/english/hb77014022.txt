77 | 14 | 22 | Narrated `Abdullah: A man got up and said, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! What do you order us to wear when we assume the state ofIhram?" The Prophet (ﷺ) replied, "Do not wear shirts, trousers, turbans, hooded cloaks or Khuffs (socksmade from thick fabric or leather), but if a man has no sandals, he can wear Khuffs after cutting themshort below the ankles; and do not wear clothes touched with (perfumes) of saffron or wars."
