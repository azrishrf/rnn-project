75 | 5 | 11 | Narrated Jabir bin `Abdullah: Once I fell ill. The Prophet (ﷺ) and Abu Bakr came walking to pay me a visit and found me unconscious.The Prophet (ﷺ) performed ablution and then poured the remaining water on me, and I came to my sensesto see the Prophet. I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! What shall I do with my property? How shall I disposeof (distribute) my property?" He did not reply till the Verse of inheritance was revealed.
