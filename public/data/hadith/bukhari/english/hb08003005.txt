8 | 3 | 5 | Narrated Muhammad bin Al-Munkadir: Once Jabir prayed with his Izar tied to his back while his clothes were Lying beside him on a woodenpeg. Somebody asked him, "Do you offer your prayer in a single Izar?" He replied, "I did so to show itto a fool like you. Had anyone of us two garments in the lifetime of the Prophet?"
