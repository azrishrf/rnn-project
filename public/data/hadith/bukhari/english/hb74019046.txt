74 | 19 | 46 | Narrated Sahl bin Sa`d: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) was offered something to drink. He drank of it while on his right was a boy and on hisleft were some elderly people. He said to the boy, "May I give these (elderly) people first?" The boysaid, "By Allah, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I will not give up my share from you to somebody else." On thatAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) placed the cup in the hand of that boy.
