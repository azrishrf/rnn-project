80 | 53 | 81 | Narrated Anas: The Prophet (ﷺ) seeing a yellow mark (of perfume) on the clothes of `Abdur-Rahman bin `Auf, said,"What about you?" `Abdur-Rahman replied, "I have married a woman with a Mahr of gold equal to adate-stone." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "May Allah bestow His Blessing on you (in your marriage). Give awedding banquet, (Walima) even with one sheep."
