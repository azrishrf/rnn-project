67 | 72 | 112 | Narrated Sahl bin Sa`d: Abu Usaid As-Sa'di invited Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) to his wedding party and his wife who was the bride,served them on that day. Do you know what drink she gave Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)? She had soaked somedates for him (in water) overnight, and when he had finished his meal she gave him that drink (ofsoaked dates).
