76 | 23 | 32 | Narrated Um Qais: that she took to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) one of her sons whose palate and tonsils she had pressed because hehad throat trouble. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Why do you pain your children by getting the palate pressedlike that? Use the Ud Al-Hindi (certain Indian incense) for it cures seven diseases one of which ispleurisy."
