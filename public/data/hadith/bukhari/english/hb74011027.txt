74 | 11 | 27 | Narrated Anas: While I was serving Abu Talha. Abu Dujana and Abu Suhail bin Al-Baida' with a drink made from amixture of unripe and ripe dates, alcoholic drinks, were made unlawful, whereupon I threw it away,and I was their butler and the youngest of them, and we used to consider that drink as an alcoholicdrink in those days.
