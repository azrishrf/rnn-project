35 | 1 | 1 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) came to Medina and the people used to pay in advance the price of fruits to bedelivered within one or two years. (The sub-narrator is in doubt whether it was one to two years ortwo to three years.) The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Whoever pays money in advance for dates (to be deliveredlater) should pay it for known specified weight and measure (of the dates).
