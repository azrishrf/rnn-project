24 | 62 | 102 | Narrated Um 'Atiyya Al-Ansariya: The Prophet (ﷺ) went to `Aisha and asked her whether she had something (to eat). She replied that shehad nothing except the mutton (piece) which Nusaiba (Um 'Atiyya) had sent to us (Buraira) incharity." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "It has reached its place and now it is not a thing of charity but a gift forus."
