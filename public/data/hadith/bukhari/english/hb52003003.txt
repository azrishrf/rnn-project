52 | 3 | 3 | Narrated Aisha: The wife of Rifa`a Al-Qurazi came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and said, "I was Rifa`a's wife, but he divorced meand it was a final irrevocable divorce. Then I married `Abdur-Rahman bin Az-Zubair but he isimpotent." The Prophet (ﷺ) asked her 'Do you want to remarry Rifa`a? You cannot unless you had acomplete sexual relation with your present husband." Abu Bakr was sitting with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) andKhalid bin Sa`id bin Al-`As was at the door waiting to be admitted. He said, "O Abu Bakr! Do youhear what this (woman) is revealing frankly before the Prophet (ﷺ) ?"
