34 | 100 | 165 | Narrated `Urwa bin Az-Zubair: Hakim bin Hizam said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I used to do good deeds in the Pre-Islamic period ofIgnorance, e.g., keeping good relations with my Kith and kin, manumitting slaves and giving alms.Shall I receive a reward for all that?" Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) replied, "You embraced Islam with all the gooddeeds which you did in the past."
