13 | 25 | 41 | Narrated `Urwa on the authority of `Aisha: On the days of Mina, (11th, 12th, and 13th of Dhul-Hijjah) Abu Bakr came to her while two younggirls were beating the tambourine and the Prophet (ﷺ) was lying covered with his clothes. Abu Bakrscolded them and the Prophet (ﷺ) uncovered his face and said to Abu Bakr, "Leave them, for these daysare the days of `Id and the days of Mina."
