86 | 23 | 46 | Narrated `Aisha: Sa`d bin Abi Waqqas and `Abd bin Zam`a quarrelled with each other (regarding a child). The Prophet (ﷺ)said, "The boy is for you, O `Abd bin Zam`a, for the boy is for (the owner) of the bed. O Sauda !Screen yourself from the boy." The sub-narrator, Al-Laith added (that the Prophet (ﷺ) also said), "And thestone is for the person who commits an illegal sexual intercourse."
