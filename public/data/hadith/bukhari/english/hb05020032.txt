5 | 20 | 32 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "When the Prophet (ﷺ) Job (Aiyub) was taking a bath naked,golden locusts began to fall on him. Job started collecting them in his clothes. His Lord addressedhim, 'O Job! Haven't I given you enough so that you are not in need of them.' Job replied, 'Yes!' ByYour Honor (power)! But I cannot dispense with Your Blessings.' "
