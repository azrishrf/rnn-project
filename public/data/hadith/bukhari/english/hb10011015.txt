10 | 11 | 15 | Narrated Salim bin `Abdullah: My father said that Allah s Apostle said, "Bilal pronounces 'Adhan at night, so keep on eating anddrinking (Suhur) till Ibn Um Maktum pronounces Adhan." Salim added, "He was a blind man whowould not pronounce the Adhan unless he was told that the day had dawned."
