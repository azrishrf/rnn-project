43 | 7 | 9 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) owed a camel of a certain age to a man who came to demand it back. The Prophet (ﷺ)ordered his companions to give him. They looked for a camel of the same age but found nothing but acamel one year older. The Prophet (ﷺ) told them to give it to him. The man said, "You have paid me infull, and may Allah pay you in full." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "The best amongst you is he who pays hisdebts in the most handsome manner."
