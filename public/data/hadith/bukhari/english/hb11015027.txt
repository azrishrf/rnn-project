11 | 15 | 27 | Narrated Aisha: (the wife of the Prophet) The people used to come from their abodes and from Al-`Awali (i.e.outskirts of Medina up to a distance of four miles or more from Medina). They used to pass throughdust and used to be drenched with sweat and covered with dust; so sweat used to trickle from them.One of them came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) who was in my house. The Prophet (ﷺ) said to him, "I wish that youkeep yourself clean on this day of yours (i.e. take a bath)."
