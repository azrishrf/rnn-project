91 | 13 | 21 | Narrated Az-Zuhri: Regarding the above narration, The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I do not know what Allah will do to him (Uthmanbin Maz'un)." Um Al-`Ala said, "I felt very sorry for that, and then I slept and saw in a dream aflowing spring for `Uthman bin Maz'un, and told Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) of that, and he said, "That flowingspring symbolizes his good deeds."
