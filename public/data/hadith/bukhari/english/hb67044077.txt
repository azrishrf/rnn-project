67 | 44 | 77 | Narrated Sahl: A woman came to the Prophet,, and presented herself to him (for marriage). He said, "I am not in needof women these days." Then a man said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Marry her to me." The Prophet (ﷺ) askedhim, "What have you got?" He said, "I have got nothing." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Give her something,even an iron ring." He said, "I have got nothing." The Prophet (ﷺ) asked (him), "How much of the Qur'ando you know (by heart)?" He said, "So much and so much." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I have married her toyou for what you know of the Qur'an."
