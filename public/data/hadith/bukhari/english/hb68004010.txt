68 | 4 | 10 | Narrated `Aisha: The wife of Rifa`a Al-Qurazi came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Rifa`a divorcedme irrevocably. After him I married `Abdur-Rahman bin Az-Zubair Al-Qurazi who proved to beimpotent." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said to her, "Perhaps you want to return to Rifa`a? Nay (you cannot returnto Rifa`a) until you and `Abdur-Rahman consummate your marriage."
