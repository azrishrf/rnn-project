73 | 12 | 17 | Narrated Al-Bara': One day Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) offered the `Id prayer and said, "Whoever offers our prayer and faces ourQibla should not slaughter the sacrifice till he finishes the `Id prayer." Abu Burda bin Niyar got upand said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I have already done it. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "That is something you havedone before its due time." Abu Burda said, "I have a Jadha'a which is better than two old sheep; shall Islaughter it?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Yes, but it will not be sufficient for anyone after you."
