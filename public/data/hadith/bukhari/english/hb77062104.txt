77 | 62 | 104 | Narrated Um Salama: that once the Prophet (ﷺ) was in her house, and an effeminate man was there too. The effeminate mansaid to `Abdullah, (Um Salama's brother) "0 `Abdullah! If Ta'if should be conquered tomorrow, Irecommend you the daughter of Ghailan, for she is so fat that she has four curves in the front (of herbelly) and eight at the back." So the Prophet (ﷺ) said (to his wives) "These effeminate (men) should notenter upon you (your houses).
