48 | 1 | 1 | Narrated Anas: No doubt, the Prophet (ﷺ) mortgaged his armor for barley grams. Once I took barley bread with somedissolved fat on it to the Prophet (ﷺ) and I heard him saying, "The household of Muhammad did notpossess except a Sa (of food grain, barley, etc.) for both the morning and the evening meals althoughthey were nine houses."
