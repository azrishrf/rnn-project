8 | 76 | 111 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) sent some horsemen to Najd and they brought a man called Thumama bin Uthal fromBani Hanifa. They fastened him to one of the pillars of the mosque. The Prophet (ﷺ) came and orderedthem to release him. He went to a (garden of) date-palms near the mosque, took a bath and enteredthe mosque again and said, "None has the right to be worshipped but Allah and Muhammad is HisApostle" (i.e. he embraced Islam).
