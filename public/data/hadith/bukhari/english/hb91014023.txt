91 | 14 | 23 | Narrated Abu Qatada Al-Ansari: (a companion of the Prophet (ﷺ) and one of his cavalry men) "I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "A gooddream is from Allah, and a bad dream is from Satan; so, if anyone of you had a bad dream which hedisliked, then he should spit on his left and seek refuge with Allah from it, for it will not harm him."
