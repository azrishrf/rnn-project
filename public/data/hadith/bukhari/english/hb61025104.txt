61 | 25 | 104 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "A time will come when the people will wage holy war, and it will be asked, 'Isthere any amongst you who has enjoyed the company of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?' They will say: 'Yes.' Andthen victory will be bestowed upon them. They will wage holy war again, and it will be asked: 'Isthere any among you who has enjoyed the company of the companions of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ?' They willsay: 'Yes.' And then victory will be bestowed on them."
