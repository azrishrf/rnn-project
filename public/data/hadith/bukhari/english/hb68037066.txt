68 | 37 | 66 | Narrated `Aisha: Rifa`a Al-Qurazi married a lady and then divorced her whereupon she married another man. She cameto the Prophet (ﷺ) and said that her new husband did not approach her, and that he was completelyimpotent. The Prophet (ﷺ) said (to her), "No (you cannot remarry your first husband) till you taste thesecond husband and he tastes you (i.e. till he consummates his marriage with you).
