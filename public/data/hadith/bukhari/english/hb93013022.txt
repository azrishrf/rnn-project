93 | 13 | 22 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: That he had divorced his wife during her menses. `Umar mentioned that to the Prophet. Allah'sApostle became angry and said, "He must take her back (his wife) and keep her with him till shebecomes clean from her menses and then to wait till she gets her next period and becomes clean againfrom it and only then, if he wants to divorce her, he may do so."
