76 | 57 | 95 | Al-Laith said: Narrated Yunus: I asked Ibn Shihab, "May weperform the ablution with the milk of she-asses or drink it, or drink the bile of wild animals or urine ofcamels?" He replied, "The Muslims used to treat themselves with that and did not see any harm in it.As for the milk of she-asses, we have learnt that Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) forbade the eating of their meat, butwe have not received any information whether drinking of their milk is allowed or forbidden." As forthe bile of wild animals, Ibn Shihab said, "Abu Idris Al-Khaulani told me that Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) forbadethe eating of the flesh of every wild beast having fangs . "
