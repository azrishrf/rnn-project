63 | 52 | 168 | Narrated Abu Musa: When the Prophet (ﷺ) arrived at Medina, he noticed that some people among the Jews used to respectAshura' (i.e. 10th of Muharram) and fast on it. The Prophet (ﷺ) then said, "We have more right to observefast on this day." and ordered that fasting should be observed on it.
