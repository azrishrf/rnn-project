67 | 95 | 140 | Narrated Aisha: regarding the Verse: 'If a wife fears cruelty or desertion on her husband's part ...') (4.128) It concernsthe woman whose husband does not want to keep her with him any longer, but wants to divorce herand marry some other lady, so she says to him: 'Keep me and do not divorce me, and then marryanother woman, and you may neither spend on me, nor sleep with me.' This is indicated by theStatement of Allah: 'There is no blame on them if they arrange an amicable settlement between themboth, and (such) settlement is better." (4.128)
