"He did not leave anything except what is Betweenthe two bindings (of the Qur'an)." Then we visited Muhammad bin Al-Hanafiyya and asked him (thesame question). He replied, "The Prophet (ﷺ) did not leave except what is between the bindings (of theQur'an).
