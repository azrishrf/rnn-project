70 | 25 | 47 | Narrated Anas: I went along with the Prophet (ﷺ) to the house of a young tailor of his. The tailor presented a dish ofTharid to the Prophet (ﷺ) and resumed his work. The Prophet (ﷺ) started picking the pieces of gourd and I too,started picking them and putting it before him. Since then I have always loved (to eat) gourd.
