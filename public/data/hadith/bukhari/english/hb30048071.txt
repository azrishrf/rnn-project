30 | 48 | 71 | Narrated Anas: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Do not practice Al-Wisal (fasting continuously without breaking one's fast in theevening or eating before the following dawn)." The people said to the Prophet, "But you practice Al-Wisal?" The Prophet (ﷺ) replied, "I am not like any of you, for I am given food and drink (by Allah)during the night."
