74 | 12 | 31 | Narrated Al-Bara: The Prophet (ﷺ) came from Mecca with Abu Bakr. Abu Bakr said "We passed by a shepherd and at thattime Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) was thirsty. I milked a little milk in a bowl and Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) drank till I waspleased. Suraqa bin Ju'shum came to us riding a horse (chasing us). The Prophet (ﷺ) invoked evil uponhim, whereupon Suraqa requested him not to invoke evil upon him, in which case he would go back.The Prophet (ﷺ) agreed.
