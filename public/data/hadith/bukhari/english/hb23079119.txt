23 | 79 | 119 | Narrated Anas: A young Jewish boy used to serve the Prophet (ﷺ) and he became sick. So the Prophet (ﷺ) went to visit him.He sat near his head and asked him to embrace Islam. The boy looked at his father, who was sittingthere; the latter told him to obey Abul-Qasim and the boy embraced Islam. The Prophet (ﷺ) came outsaying: "Praises be to Allah Who saved the boy from the Hell-fire."
