67 | 4 | 5 | Narrated 'Ata: We presented ourselves along with Ibn `Abbas at the funeral procession of Maimuna at a place calledSarif. Ibn `Abbas said, "This is the wife of the Prophet (ﷺ) so when you lift her bier, do not Jerk it orshake it much, but walk smoothly because the Prophet (ﷺ) had nine wives and he used to observe thenight turns with eight of them, and for one of them there was no night turn."
