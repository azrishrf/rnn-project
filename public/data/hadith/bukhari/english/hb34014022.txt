34 | 14 | 22 | Narrated Qatada: Anas went to the Prophet (ﷺ) with barley bread having some dissolved fat on it. The Prophet (ﷺ) hadmortgaged his armor to a Jew in Medina and took from him some barley for his family. Anas heardhim saying, "The household of Muhammad did not possess even a single Sa of wheat or food grainsfor the evening meal, although he has nine wives to look after."
