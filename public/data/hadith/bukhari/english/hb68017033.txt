68 | 17 | 33 | Narrated Al-Aswad: Aisha intended to buy Barira, but her masters stipulated that her wala wound be for them. Aishamentioned that to the Prophet (ﷺ) who said (to `Aisha), "Buy and manumit her, for the wala is for the onewho manumits." Once some me; was brought to the Prophet (ﷺ) and was said, "This meat was given incharity to Barira. " The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "It an object of charity for Barira and present for us."Narrated Adam:Shu`ba relate the same Hadith and added: Barira was given the option regarding her husband.
