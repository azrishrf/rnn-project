51 | 22 | 36 | Narrated Sahl bin Sa`d: A drink (milk mixed with water) was brought to the Prophet (ﷺ) who drank some of it while a boy wassitting on his right and old men on his left. The Prophet (ﷺ) said to the boy, "If you permit me, I'll give(the rest of the drink to) these old men first." The boy said, "I will not give preference to any one overme as regards my share from you, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)!" The Prophet (ﷺ) then put that container in the boy'shand.
