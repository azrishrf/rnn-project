86 | 9 | 14 | Narrated `Abdullah: Allah Apostle said in Hajjat-al-Wada`, "Which month (of the year) do you think is most sacred?" Thepeople said, "This current month of ours (the month of Dhull-Hijja)." He said, "Which town (country)do you think is the most sacred?" They said, "This city of ours (Mecca)." He said, "Which day do youthink is the most sacred?" The people said, "This day of ours." He then said, "Allah, the Blessed, theSupreme, has made your blood, your property and your honor as sacred as this day of yours in thistown of yours, in this month of yours (and such protection cannot be slighted) except rightfully."
