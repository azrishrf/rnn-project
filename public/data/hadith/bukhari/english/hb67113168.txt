67 | 113 | 168 | Narrated Um Salama: that while the Prophet (ﷺ) was with her, there was an effeminate man in the house. The effeminate mansaid to Um Salama's brother, `Abdullah bin Abi Umaiyya, "If Allah should make you conquer Ta'iftomorrow, I recommend that you take the daughter of Ghailan (in marriage) for (she is so fat) that sheshows four folds of flesh when facing you and eight when she turns her back." Thereupon the Prophet (ﷺ)said (to us), "This (effeminate man) should not enter upon you (anymore).
