70 | 8 | 13 | Narrated Anas: The Prophet (ﷺ) halted to consummate his marriage with Safiyya. I invited the Muslims to his weddingbanquet. He ordered that leather dining sheets be spread. Then dates, dried yoghurt and butter wereput on those sheets. Anas added: The Prophet (ﷺ) consummated his marriage with Safiyya (during ajourney) whereupon Hais (sweet dish) was served on a leather dining sheet.
