88 | 6 | 13 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Amr bin Yasar: That they visited Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri and asked him about Al-Harauriyya, a special unorthodoxreligious sect, "Did you hear the Prophet (ﷺ) saying anything about them?" Abu Sa`id said, "I do notknow what Al-Harauriyya is, but I heard the Prophet (ﷺ) saying, "There will appear in this nation---- hedid not say: From this nation ---- a group of people so pious apparently that you will consider yourprayers inferior to their prayers, but they will recite the Qur'an, the teachings of which will not gobeyond their throats and will go out of their religion as an arrow darts through the game, whereuponthe archer may look at his arrow, its Nasl at its Risaf and its Fuqa to see whether it is blood-stained ornot (i.e. they will have not even a trace of Islam in them).
