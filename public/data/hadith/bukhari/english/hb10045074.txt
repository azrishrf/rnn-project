10 | 45 | 74 | Narrated Aiyub: Abu Qilaba said, "Malik bin Huwairith came to this Mosque of ours and said, 'I pray in front of youand my aim is not to lead the prayer but to show you the way in which the Prophet (ﷺ) used to pray.' " Iasked Abu Qilaba, "How did he use to pray?' " He replied, "(The Prophet (ﷺ) used to pray) like thisSheikh of ours and the Sheikh used to sit for a while after the prostration, before getting up after thefirst rak`a. "
