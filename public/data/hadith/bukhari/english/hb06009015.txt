6 | 9 | 15 | Narrated Asma' bint Abi Bakr: A woman asked Allah's Messenger (ﷺ), "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! What should we do, if the blood of menses fallson our clothes?" Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) replied, "If the blood of menses falls on the garment of anyone ofyou, she must take hold of the blood spot, rub it, and wash it with water and then pray in (with it).
