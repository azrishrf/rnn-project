60 | 40 | 98 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Solomon (the son of) David said, 'Tonight I will sleep with seventy ladies each ofwhom will conceive a child who will be a knight fighting for "Allah's Cause.' His companion said, 'IfAllah will.' But Solomon did not say so; therefore none of those women got pregnant except one whogave birth to a half child." The Prophet (ﷺ) further said, "If the Prophet (ﷺ) Solomon had said it (i.e. 'If Allahwill') he would have begotten children who would have fought in Allah's Cause." Shuaib and Ibn AbiAz-Zinad said, "Ninety (women) is more correct (than seventy).
