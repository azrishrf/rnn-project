63 | 2 | 4 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) or Abul-Qasim said, "If the Ansar took their way through a valley or a mountain pass, Iwould take Ansar's valley. And but for the migration, I would have been one of the Ansar." AbuHuraira used to say, "The Prophet (ﷺ) is not unjust (by saying so). May my parents be sacrificed for him,for the Ansar sheltered and helped him," or said a similar sentence.
