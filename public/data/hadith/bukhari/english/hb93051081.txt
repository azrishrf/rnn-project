93 | 51 | 81 | Narrated Jubair bin Mut`im: A woman came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and spoke to him about something and he told her to return to him. Shesaid, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! If I come and do not find you?" (As if she meant, "...if you die?") TheProphet said, "If you should not find me, then go to Abu Bakr."
