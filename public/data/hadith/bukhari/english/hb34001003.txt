34 | 1 | 3 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: `Ukaz, Majanna and Dhul-Majaz were marketplaces in the Pre-Islamic period of ignorance. WhenIslam came, Muslims felt that marketing there might be a sin. So, the Divine Inspiration came: "Thereis no harm for you to seek the bounty of your Lord (in the seasons of Hajj)." (2.198) Ibn `Abbasrecited the Verse in this way.
