23 | 85 | 132 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: A funeral procession passed and the people praised the deceased. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "It has beenaffirmed to him." Then another funeral procession passed and the people spoke badly of the deceased.The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "It has been affirmed to him". `Umar bin Al-Khattab asked (Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)(p.b.u.h) ), "What has been affirmed?" He replied, "You praised this, so Paradise has been affirmed tohim; and you spoke badly of this, so Hell has been affirmed to him. You people are Allah's witnesseson earth."
