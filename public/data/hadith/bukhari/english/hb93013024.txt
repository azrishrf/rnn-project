93 | 13 | 24 | Narrated Abu Mas`ud Al-Ansari: A man came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! By Allah, I fail to attend the morningcongregational prayer because so-and-so (i.e., Mu`adh bin Jabal) prolongs the prayer when he leads usfor it." I had never seen the Prophet (ﷺ) more furious in giving advice than he was on that day. He thensaid, "O people! some of you make others dislike (good deeds, i.e. prayers etc). So whoever amongyou leads the people in prayer, he should shorten it because among them there are the old, the weakand the busy (needy having some jobs to do).
