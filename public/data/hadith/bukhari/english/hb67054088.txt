67 | 54 | 88 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: `Abdur-Rahman bin `Auf came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and he had marks of Sufra (yellow perfume).Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) asked him (about those marks). `AbdurRahman bin `Auf told him that he had marrieda woman from the Ansar. The Prophet (ﷺ) asked, "How much Mahr did you pay her?" He said, "I paidgold equal to the weight of a date stone." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said to him, "Give a wedding banquet, evenif with one sheep."
