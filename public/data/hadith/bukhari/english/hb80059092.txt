80 | 59 | 92 | Narrated Abu Huraira: at-Tufail bin `Amr came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! The tribe of Daus hasdisobeyed (Allah and His Apostle) and refused (to embrace Islam), therefore, invoke Allah's wrath forthem." The people thought that the Prophet (ﷺ) would invoke Allah's wrath for them, but he said, "OAllah! Guide the tribe Of Daus and let them come to us."
