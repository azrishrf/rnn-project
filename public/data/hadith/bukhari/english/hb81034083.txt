81 | 34 | 83 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: A bedouin came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Who is the best of mankind!" TheProphet said, "A man who strives for Allah's Cause with his life and property, and also a man wholives (all alone) in a mountain path among the mountain paths to worship his Lord and save the peoplefrom his evil."
