86 | 26 | 51 | Narrated Abu Huraira: A person had sexual relation with his wife in the month of Ramadan(while he was fasting), and he came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) seeking hisverdict concerning that action. The Prophet (ﷺ) said (to him), "Can youafford to manumit a slave?" The man said, "No." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Canyou fast for two successive months?" He said, "No." The Prophet (ﷺ) said,"Then feed sixty poor persons."
