79 | 26 | 36 | Narrated Abu Sa`id: The people of (the tribe of) Quraiza agreed upon to accept the verdict of Sa`d. The Prophet (ﷺ) sent forhim (Sa`d) and he came. The Prophet (ﷺ) said (to those people), "Get up for your chief or the best amongyou!" Sa`d sat beside the Prophet (ﷺ) and the Prophet (ﷺ) said (to him), "These people have agreed to acceptyour verdict." Sa`d said, "So I give my judgment that their warriors should be killed and their womenand children should be taken as captives." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "You have judged according to the King's(Allah's) judgment."
