61 | 25 | 88 | Narrated `Aisha: The Prophet (ﷺ) in his fatal illness, called his daughter Fatima and told her a secret because of which shestarted weeping. Then he called her and told her another secret, and she started laughing. When Iasked her about that, she replied, The Prophet (ﷺ) told me that he would die in his fatal illness, and so Iwept, but then he secretly told me that from amongst his family, I would be the first to join him, andso I laughed."
