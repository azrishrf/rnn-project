23 | 79 | 121 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: (Ibn `Umar added): Later on Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) (p.b.u.h) once again went along with Ubai binKa`b to the date-palm trees (garden) where Ibn Saiyad was staying. The Prophet (p.b.u.h) wanted tohear something from Ibn Saiyad before Ibn Saiyad could see him, and the Prophet (p.b.u.h) saw himlying covered with a sheet and from where his murmurs were heard. Ibn Saiyad's mother saw Allah'sApostle while he was hiding himself behind the trunks of the date-palm trees. She addressed IbnSaiyad, "O Saf ! (and this was the name of Ibn Saiyad) Here is Muhammad." And with that Ibn Saiyadgot up. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Had this woman left him (Had she not disturbed him), then Ibn Saiyadwould have revealed the reality of his case.
