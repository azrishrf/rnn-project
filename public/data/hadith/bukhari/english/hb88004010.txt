88 | 4 | 10 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: A Jew passed by Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "As-Samu 'Alaika." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said in reply, "We'Alaika." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) then said to his companions, "Do you know what he (the Jew) has said? Hesaid, 'As-Samu 'Alaika.'" They said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Shall we kill him?" The Prophet, said, "No.When the people of the Book greet you, say: 'Wa 'Alaikum.'"
