77 | 85 | 155 | Narrated Asma': A woman asked the Prophet (ﷺ) saying, "0 Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! My daughter got measles and her hair fellout. Now that I got her married, may I let her use false hair?" He said (to her), "Allah has cursed thelady who lengthens hair artificially and the one who gets her hair lengthened artificially."
