59 | 7 | 44 | Narrated Busr bin Sa`id: That Zaid bin Khalid Al-Juhani narrated to him something in the presence of Sa`id bin 'Ubaidullah Al-Khaulani who was brought up in the house of Maimuna the wife of the Prophet. Zaid narrated to themthat Abu Talha said that the Prophet (ï·º) said, "The Angels (of Mercy) do not enter a house wherein thereis a picture." Busr said, "Later on Zaid bin Khalid fell ill and we called on him. To our surprise wesaw a curtain decorated with pictures in his house. I said to Ubaidullah Al-Khaulani, "Didn't he (i.e.Zaid) tell us about the (prohibition of) pictures?" He said, "But he excepted the embroidery ongarments. Didn't you hear him?" I said, "No." He said, "Yes, he did."
