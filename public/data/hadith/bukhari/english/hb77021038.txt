77 | 21 | 38 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) forbade two types of dresses: (A) To sit in an Ihtiba' posture in one garment nothing ofwhich covers his private parts. (B) to cover one side of his body with one garment and leave the otherside bare The Prophet (ﷺ) also forbade the Mulamasa and Munabadha.
