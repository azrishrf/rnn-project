77 | 13 | 20 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: A man said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ). What type of clothes should a Muhrim wear Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) replied,'Do not wear shirts, turbans trousers hooded cloaks or Khuffs (socks made from thick fabric orleather); but if someone cannot get sandals, then he can wear Khuffs after cutting them short belowthe ankles. Do not wear clothes touched by saffon or wars (two kinds of perfumes).
