91 | 18 | 27 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "While I was sleeping, I saw (in a dream) the people being displayedbefore me, wearing shirts, some of which (were so short that it) reached as far as their breasts andsome reached below that. Then `Umar bin Al-Khattab was shown to me and he was wearing a shirtwhich he was dragging (behind him)." They asked. What have you interpreted (about the dream)? OAllah's Messenger (ﷺ)?" He said, "The religion."
