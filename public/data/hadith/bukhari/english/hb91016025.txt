91 | 16 | 25 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "While I was sleeping, I was given a bowl full of milk (in the dream) and I drankfrom it (to my fill) till I noticed its wetness coming out of my limbs. Then I gave the rest of it to`Umar bin Al-Khattab." The persons sitting around him, asked, "What have you interpreted (about thedream) O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?" He said, "(It is religious) knowledge."
