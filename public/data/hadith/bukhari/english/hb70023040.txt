70 | 23 | 40 | Narrated Abu Hazim: I asked Sahl bin Sa`d, "Did Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ever eat white flour?" Sahl said, "Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) neversaw white flour since Allah sent him as an Apostle till He took him unto Him." I asked, "Did thepeople have (use) sieves during the lifetime of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?" Sahl said, "Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) never saw(used) a sieve since Allah sent him as an Apostle until He took him unto Him," I said, "How couldyou eat barley unsifted?" he said, "We used to grind it and then blow off its husk, and after the huskflew away, we used to prepare the dough (bake) and eat it."
