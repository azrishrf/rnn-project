73 | 16 | 29 | Narrated Salama bin Al-Aqua': The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Whoever has slaughtered a sacrifice should not keep anything of Its meat afterthree days." When it was the next year the people said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Shall we do as we did lastyear?" He said, ' Eat of it and feed of it to others and store of it for in that year the people were havinga hard time and I wanted you to help (the needy).
