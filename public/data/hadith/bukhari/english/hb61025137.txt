61 | 25 | 137 | Narrated Zainab bint Jahsh: That the Prophet (ﷺ) came to her in a state of fear saying, "None has the right to be worshiped but Allah!Woe to the Arabs because of evil that has come near. Today a hole has been made in the wall of Gogand Magog as large as this." pointing with two of his fingers making a circle. Zainab said, "I said, 'OAllah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Shall we be destroyed though amongst us there are pious people? ' He said, 'Yes, ifevil increases."
