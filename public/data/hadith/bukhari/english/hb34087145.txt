34 | 87 | 145 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) forbade the sale of fruits till they are almost ripe.He was asked what is meant by 'are almost ripe.' He replied, "Tillthey become red." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) further said, "If Allah spoiled thefruits, what right would one have to take the money of one's brother(i.e. other people)?"
