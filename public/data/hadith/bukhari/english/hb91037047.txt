91 | 37 | 47 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "While I was sleeping, I saw that a cup full of milk was brought to meand I drank of it and gave the remaining of it to `Umar bin Al-Khattab." They asked. What have youinterpreted (about the dream)? O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said. "(It is Religious) knowledge."
