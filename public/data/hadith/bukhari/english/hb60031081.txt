60 | 31 | 81 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Adam and Moses argued with each other. Moses said to Adam. 'You are Adamwhose mistake expelled you from Paradise.' Adam said to him, 'You are Moses whom Allah selectedas His Messenger and as the one to whom He spoke directly; yet you blame me for a thing which hadalready been written in my fate before my creation?"' Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said twice, "So, Adamoverpowered Moses."
