59 | 10 | 70 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Your (ordinary) fire is one of 70 parts of the (Hell) Fire." Someone asked, "OAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) This (ordinary) fire would have been sufficient (to torture the unbelievers)," Allah'sApostle said, "The (Hell) Fire has 69 parts more than the ordinary (worldly) fire, each part is as hot asthis (worldly) fire."
