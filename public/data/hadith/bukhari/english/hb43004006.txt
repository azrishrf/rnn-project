43 | 4 | 6 | Narrated Abu Huraira: A man demanded his debts from Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) in such a rude manner that the companions of theProphet intended to harm him, but the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Leave him, no doubt, for he (the creditor) has theright to demand it (harshly). Buy a camel and give it to him." They said, "The camel that is availableis older than the camel he demands. "The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Buy it and give it to him, for the best amongyou are those who repay their debts handsomely. "
