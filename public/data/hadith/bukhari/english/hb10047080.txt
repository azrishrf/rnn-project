10 | 47 | 80 | Narrated Hisham ibn `Urwa's father: `Aisha said, "Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ordered Abu Bakr to lead the people in the prayer during his illness andso he led them in prayer." `Urwa, a sub narrator, added, "Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) felt a bit relieved and cameout and Abu Bakr was leading the people. When Abu Bakr saw the Prophet (ﷺ) he retreated but theProphet beckoned him to remain there. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) sat beside Abu Bakr. Abu Bakr was followingthe prayer of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and the people were following the prayer of Abu Bakr."
