62 | 6 | 37 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "While I was sleeping, the people were presented to me (in a dream).They were wearing shirts, some of which were merely covering their (chests). and some were a bitlonger. `Umar was presented before me and his shirt was so long that he was dragging it." They asked,"How have you interpreted it, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?" He said, "Religion."
