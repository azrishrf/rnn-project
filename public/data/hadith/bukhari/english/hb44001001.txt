44 | 1 | 1 | Narrated `Abdullah: I heard a man reciting a verse (of the Holy Qur'an) but I had heard the Prophet (ﷺ) reciting it differently.So, I caught hold of the man by the hand and took him to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) who said, "Both of you areright." Shu`ba, the sub-narrator said, "I think he said to them, "Don't differ, for the nations before youdiffered and perished (because of their differences). "
