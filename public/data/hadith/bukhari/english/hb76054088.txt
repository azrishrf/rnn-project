76 | 54 | 88 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Abu Huraira also said: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "No 'Adha." A bedouin got upand said, "Don't you see how camels on the sand look like deer but when a mangy camel mixes withthem, they all get infected with mange?" On that the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Then who conveyed the (mange)disease to the first camel?"
