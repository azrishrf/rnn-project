34 | 30 | 45 | Narrated 'Is-haq bin `Abdullah bin Abu Talha: I heard Anas bin Malik saying, "A tailor invited Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) to a meal which he had prepared. "Anas bin Malik said, "I accompanied Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) to that meal. He served the Prophet (ﷺ) with breadand soup made with gourd and dried meat. I saw the Prophet (ﷺ) taking the pieces of gourd from thedish." Anas added, "Since that day I have continued to like gourd."
