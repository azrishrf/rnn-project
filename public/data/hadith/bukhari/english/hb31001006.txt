31 | 1 | 6 | Narrated Abu Salama bin `Abdur Rahman: that he asked `Aisha "How was the prayer of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) in Ramadan?" She replied, "He did notpray more than eleven rak`at in Ramadan or in any other month. He used to pray four rak`at ---- letalone their beauty and length----and then he would pray four ----let alone their beauty and length ----and then he would pray three rak`at (witr)." She added, "I asked, 'O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! Do you sleepbefore praying the witr?' He replied, 'O `Aisha! My eyes sleep but my heart does not sleep."
