8 | 10 | 20 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) forbade two kinds of sales i.e. Al-Limais and An-Nibadh (the former is a kind of sale inwhich the deal is completed if the buyer touches a thing, without seeing or checking it properly andthe latter is a kind of a sale in which the deal is completed when the seller throws a thing towards thebuyer giving him no opportunity to see, touch or check it) and (the Prophet (ﷺ) forbade) also Ishtimal-As-Samma' and Al-Ihtiba' in a single garment.
