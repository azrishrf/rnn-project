57 | 19 | 52 | Narrated `Amr bin Taghlib: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) gave (gifts) to some people to the exclusion of some others. The latter seemed to bedispleased by that. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I give to some people, lest they should deviate from True Faithor lose patience, while I refer other people to the goodness and contentment which Allah has put intheir hearts, and `Amr bin Taghlib is amongst them." `Amr bin Taghlib said, "The statement of Allah'sApostle is dearer to me than red camels."Narrated Al-Hasan: `Amr bin Taghlib told us that Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) got some property or some warprisoners and he distributed them in the above way (i.e. giving to some people to the exclusion ofothers) .
