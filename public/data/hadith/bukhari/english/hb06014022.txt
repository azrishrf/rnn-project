6 | 14 | 22 | Narrated `Aisha: An Ansari woman asked the Prophet (ﷺ) how to take a bath after finishing from the menses. He replied,"Take a piece a cloth perfumed with musk and clean the private parts with it thrice." The Prophet (ﷺ) feltshy and turned his face. So I pulled her to me and told her what the Prophet (ﷺ) meant.
