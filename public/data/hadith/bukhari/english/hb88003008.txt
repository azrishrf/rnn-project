88 | 3 | 8 | Narrated Abu Huraira: When the Prophet (ﷺ) died and Abu Bakr became his successor and some of the Arabs reverted todisbelief, `Umar said, "O Abu Bakr! How can you fight these people although Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, 'Ihave been ordered to fight the people till they say: 'None has the right to be worshipped but Allah,'and whoever said, 'None has the right to be worshipped but Allah', Allah will save his property andhis life from me, unless (he does something for which he receives legal punishment) justly, and hisaccount will be with Allah?'
