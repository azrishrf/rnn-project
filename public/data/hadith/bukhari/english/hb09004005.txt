9 | 4 | 5 | Narrated Ibn Mas`ud: A man kissed a woman (unlawfully) and then went to the Prophet (ﷺ) and informed him. Allah revealed:And offer prayers perfectly At the two ends of the day And in some hours of the night (i.e. the fivecompulsory prayers). Verily! good deeds remove (annul) the evil deeds (small sins) (11.114). Theman asked Allah's Messenger (ﷺ), "Is it for me?" He said, "It is for all my followers."
