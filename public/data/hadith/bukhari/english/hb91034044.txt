91 | 34 | 44 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "While I was sleeping, I saw a bowl full of milk was brought to meand I drank of it (to my fill) till I noticed its wetness flowing (in my body). Then I gave the remainingof it to `Umar." They asked, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! What have you interpreted (about the dream)? Hesaid, "(It is Religious) knowledge."
