13 | 2 | 3 | Narrated Aisha: It was the day of `Id, and the Black people were playingwith shields and spears; so either I requested the Prophet (p.b.u.h) or he asked me whether I wouldlike to see the display. I replied in the affirmative. Then the Prophet (p.b.u.h) made me stand behindhim and my cheek was touching his cheek and he was saying, "Carry on! O Bani Arfida," till I gottired. The Prophet (p.b.u.h) asked me, "Are you satisfied (Is that sufficient for you)?" I replied in theaffirmative and he told me to leave.
