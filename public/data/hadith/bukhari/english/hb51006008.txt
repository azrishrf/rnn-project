51 | 6 | 8 | Narrated As-Sa'b bin Jath-thama: An onager was presented to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) at the place called Al-Abwa' or Waddan, but Allah'sApostle rejected it. When the Prophet (ﷺ) noticed the signs of sorrow on the giver's face he said, "Wehave not rejected your gift, but we are in the state of Ihram." (i.e. if we were not in a state of Ihram wewould have accepted your gift
