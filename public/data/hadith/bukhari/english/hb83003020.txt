83 | 3 | 20 | Narrated Al-Bara 'bin `Azib: A piece of silken cloth was given to the Prophet (ﷺ) as a present and the people handed it over amongstthemselves and were astonished at its beauty and softness. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Are you astonishedat it?" They said, "Yes, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)!" He said, "By Him in Whose Hand my soul is, thehandkerchiefs of Sa`d in Paradise are better than it."
