51 | 25 | 42 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) took a camel of special age from somebody on credit. Its owner came and demanded itback (harshly). The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "No doubt, he who has a right, can demand it." Then the Prophet (ﷺ)gave him an older camel than his camel and said, "The best amongst you is he who repays his debts inthe most handsome way."
