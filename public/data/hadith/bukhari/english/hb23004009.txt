23 | 4 | 9 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Zaid took over the flag and was martyred. Then it was taken by Jafar who wasmartyred as well. Then `Abdullah bin Rawaha took the flag but he too was martyred and at that timethe eyes of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) were full of tears. Then Khalid bin Al-Walid took the flag without beingnominated as a chief (before hand) and was blessed with victory."
