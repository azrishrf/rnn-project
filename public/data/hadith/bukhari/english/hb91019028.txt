I said to `Abdullahbin Salam, "They said so-and-so." He replied, "Subhan Allah! They ought not to have said things ofwhich they have no knowledge, but I saw (in a dream) that a post was fixed in a green garden. At thetop of the post there was a handhold and below it there was a servant. I was asked to climb (the post).So I climbed it till I got hold of the handhold."
