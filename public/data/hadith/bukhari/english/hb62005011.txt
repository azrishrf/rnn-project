62 | 5 | 11 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: That Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Allah will not look on the Day of Judgment at him who drags his robe(behind him) out of pride." Abu Bakr said "One side of my robe slacks down unless I get verycautious about it." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "But you do not do that with a pride."
