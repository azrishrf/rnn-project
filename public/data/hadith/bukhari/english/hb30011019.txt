30 | 11 | 19 | Narrated Anas: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) vowed to keep aloof from his wives for one month, and he had dislocation of his leg.So, he stayed in a Mashruba for 29 nights and then came down. Some people said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)!You vowed to stay aloof for one month," He replied, "The month is of 29 days."
