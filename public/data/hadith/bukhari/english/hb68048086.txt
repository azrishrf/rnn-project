68 | 48 | 86 | Narrated Um 'Atiyya: We were forbidden to mourn for more than three days for a dead person, except for a husband, forwhom a wife should mourn for four months and ten days (while in the mourning period) we were notallowed to put kohl in our eyes, nor perfume our-selves, nor wear dyed clothes, except a garment of'Asb (special clothes made in Yemen). But it was permissible for us that when one of us became cleanfrom her menses and took a bath, she could use a piece of a certain kind of incense. And it wasforbidden for us to follow funeral processions.
