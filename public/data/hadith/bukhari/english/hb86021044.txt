86 | 21 | 44 | Narrated Jabir bin `Abdullah Al-Ansari: A man from the tribe of Bani Aslam came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and Informed him that he had committedillegal sexual intercourse and bore witness four times against himself. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) ordered him tobe stoned to death as he was a married Person.
