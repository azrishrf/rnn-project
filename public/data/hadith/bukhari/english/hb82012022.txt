82 | 12 | 22 | Narrated Warrad: (the freed slave of Al-Mughira bin Shu`ba) Muawiya wrote to Mughira. 'Write to me what you heardthe Prophet (ﷺ) saying after his prayer.' So Al-Mughira dictated to me and said, "I heard the Prophet (ﷺ)saying after the prayer, 'None has the right to be worshipped but Allah Alone Who has no partner. OAllah! No-one can withhold what You give, and none can give what You withhold, and the fortune ofa man of means is useless before You (i.e., only good deeds are of value).
