52 | 19 | 30 | Narrated `Abdullah: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "If somebody takes a false oath in order to get the property of a Muslim(unjustly) by that oath, then Allah will be angry with him when he will meet Him." Al-Ash'athinformed me, "By Allah! This was said regarding me. There was a dispute about a piece of landbetween me and a man from the Jews who denied my right. I took him to the Prophet. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)asked me, 'Do you have an evidence?' I replied in the negative. He said to the Jew, 'Take an oath.' Isaid, 'O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! He will surely take an oath and take my property unjustly." So, Allahrevealed: "Verily! Those who purchase a little gain at the cost of Allah's covenant and their oaths . . . "(3.77)
