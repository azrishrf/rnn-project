91 | 31 | 40 | Narrated Abu Huraira: We were sitting with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ), he said, "While I was sleeping, I saw myself in Paradise.Suddenly I saw a woman performing ablution beside a palace. I asked, "For whom is this palace?"They (the angels) replied, "It is for `Umar bin Al-Khattab." Then I remembered `Umar's ghira andwent back hurriedly." On hearing that, `Umar started weeping and said, " Let my father and mother besacrificed for you. O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! How dare I think of my Ghira being offended by you?
