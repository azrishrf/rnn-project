19 | 15 | 27 | Narrated Al-Aswad: I asked `Aisha "How is the night prayer of the Prophet?" She replied, "He used to sleep early at night,and get up in its last part to pray, and then return to his bed. When the Mu'adh-dhin pronounced theAdhan, he would get up. If he was in need of a bath he would take it; otherwise he would performablution and then go out (for the prayer)."
