30 | 16 | 27 | Narrated Sahl bin Saud: When the following verses were revealed: 'Eat and drink until the white thread appears to you, distinctfrom the black thread' and of dawn was not revealed, some people who intended to fast, tied black andwhite threads to their legs and went on eating till they differentiated between the two. Allah thenrevealed the words, 'of dawn', and it became clear that meant night and day.
