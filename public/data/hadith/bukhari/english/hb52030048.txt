52 | 30 | 48 | Narrated Aisha: Whenever Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) intended to go on a journey, he used to draw lots among his wives andwould take with him the one on whom the lot fell. He also used to fix for everyone of his wives a dayand a night, but Sauda bint Zam`a gave her day and night to `Aisha, the wife of the Prophet (ﷺ) intendingthereby to please Allah's Messenger (ﷺ).
