70 | 36 | 64 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: A tailor invited the Prophet (ﷺ) to a meal which he had prepared, and I went along with the Prophet (ﷺ) . Thetailor presented barley bread and soup containing gourd and cured meat. I saw the Prophet (ﷺ) picking thepieces of gourd from around the dish, and since then I have kept on liking gourd.
