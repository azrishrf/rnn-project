68 | 12 | 26 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The wife of Thabit bin Qais came toAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I do not blame Thabit for any defects in his character orhis religion, but I cannot endure to live with him." On that Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Will you return hisgarden to him?" She said, "Yes."
