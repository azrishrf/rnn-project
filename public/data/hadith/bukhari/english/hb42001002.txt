42 | 1 | 2 | Narrated Sahl bin Sa`d: A tumbler (full of milk or water) was brought to the Prophet (ﷺ) who drank from it, while on his right sidethere was sitting a boy who was the youngest of those who were present and on his left side there wereold men. The Prophet (ﷺ) asked, "O boy, will you allow me to give it (i.e. the rest of the drink) to the oldmen?" The boy said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I will not give preference to anyone over me to drink the restof it from which you have drunk." So, the Prophet (ﷺ) gave it to him.
