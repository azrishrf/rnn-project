61 | 25 | 110 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: I saw Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) at the 'time when the `Asr prayer was due. Then the people were searching forwater for ablution but they could not find any. Then some water was brought to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and heplaced his hand in the pot and ordered the people to perform the ablution with the water. I saw waterflowing from underneath his fingers and the people started performing the ablution till all of them didit.
