8 | 54 | 84 | Narrated `Aisha: Um Salama told Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) about a church which she had seen in Ethiopia and which was calledMariya. She told him about the pictures which she had seen in it. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "If anyrighteous pious man dies amongst them, they would build a place of worship at his grave and makethese pictures in it; they are the worst creatures in the sight of Allah."
