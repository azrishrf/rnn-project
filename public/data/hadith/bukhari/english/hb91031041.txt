91 | 31 | 41 | Narrated Jabir bin `Abdullah: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said: (I saw in a dream that) I entered Paradise, and behold, there was a palace built ofgold! I asked, 'For whom is this palace?' They (the angels) replied, 'For a man from the Quraish.' " TheProphet added, "O Ibn Al-Khattab! Nothing stopped me from entering it except your Ghira." `Umarsaid, "How dare I think of my Ghira being offended by you, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)?"
