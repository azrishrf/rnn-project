23 | 2 | 4 | Narrated Al-Bara' bin `Azib: Allah's Messenger (ï·º) ordered us to do seven things and forbade us to do other seven. He ordered us:to follow the funeral procession. to visit the sick, to accept invitations, to help the oppressed, to fulfillthe oaths, to return the greeting and to reply to the sneezer: (saying, "May Allah be merciful on you,"provided the sneezer says, "All the praises are for Allah,").
