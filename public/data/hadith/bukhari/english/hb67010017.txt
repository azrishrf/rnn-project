67 | 10 | 17 | Narrated Jabir bin `Abdullah: When I got married, Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said to me, "What type of lady have you married?" I replied, "Ihave married a matron' He said, "Why, don't you have a liking for the virgins and for fondling them?"Jabir also said: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Why didn't you marry a young girl so that you might play withher and she with you?'
