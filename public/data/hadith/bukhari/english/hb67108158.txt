67 | 108 | 158 | Narrated Anas: While the Prophet (ﷺ) was in the house of one of his wives, one of the mothers of the believers sent ameal in a dish. The wife at whose house the Prophet (ﷺ) was, struck the hand of the servant, causing thedish to fall and break. The Prophet (ﷺ) gathered the broken pieces of the dish and then started collectingon them the food which had been in the dish and said, "Your mother (my wife) felt jealous." Then hedetained the servant till a (sound) dish was brought from the wife at whose house he was. He gave thesound dish to the wife whose dish had been broken and kept the broken one at the house where it hadbeen broken.
