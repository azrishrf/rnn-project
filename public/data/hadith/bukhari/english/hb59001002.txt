59 | 1 | 2 | Narrated `Imran bin Husain: Some people of Bani Tamim came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and he said (to them), "O Bani Tamim! rejoice withglad tidings." They said, "You have given us glad tidings, now give us something." On hearing thatthe color of his face changed then the people of Yemen came to him and he said, "O people of Yemen! Accept the good tidings, as Bani Tamim has refused them." The Yemenites said, "We accept them.Then the Prophet (ﷺ) started taking about the beginning of creation and about Allah's Throne. In the meantime a man came saying, "O `Imran! Your she-camel has run away!'' (I got up and went away), but lwish I had not left that place (for I missed what Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) had said).
