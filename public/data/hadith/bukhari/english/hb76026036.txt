76 | 26 | 36 | Narrated Um Oais: that she took to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) one of her sons whose palate and tonsils she had pressed to treat athroat trouble. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Be afraid of Allah! Why do you pain your children by having theirtonsils pressed like that? Use the Ud Al-Hindi (a certain Indian incense) for it cures seven diseases,one of which is pleurisy."
