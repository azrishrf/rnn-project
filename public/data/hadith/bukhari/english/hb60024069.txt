60 | 24 | 69 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "One should not say that I am better than Jonah (i.e. Yunus) bin Matta." So, hementioned his father Matta. The Prophet (ﷺ) mentioned the night of his Ascension and said, "The prophetMoses was brown, a tall person as if from the people of the tribe of Shanu'a. Jesus was a curly-hairedman of moderate height." He also mentioned Malik, the gate-keeper of the (Hell) Fire, and Ad-Dajjal.
