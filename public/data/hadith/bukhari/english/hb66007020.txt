66 | 7 | 20 | Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The Prophet (ﷺ) was the most generous person, and he used to become more so (generous) particularly inthe month of Ramadan because Gabriel used to meet him every night of the month of Ramadan till itelapsed. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) used to recite the Qur'an for him. When Gabriel met him, he used to becomemore generous than the fast wind in doing good.
