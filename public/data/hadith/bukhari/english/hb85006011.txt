85 | 6 | 11 | Narrated Al-Aswad bin Yazid: Mu`adh bin Jabal came to us in Yemen as a tutor and a ruler, and we (the people of Yemen) asked himabout (the distribution of the property of ) a man who had died leaving a daughter and a sister. Mu`adhgave the daughter one-half of the property and gave the sister the other half.
