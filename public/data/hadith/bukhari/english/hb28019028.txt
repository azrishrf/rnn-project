28 | 19 | 28 | Narrated Ya'li: While I was with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) there came to him a man wearing a cloak having a trace of yellowishperfume or a similar thing on it. `Umar used to say to me, "Would you like to see the Prophet (ﷺ) at thetime when he is inspired divinely?" So, it happened that he was inspired (then) and when theinspiration was over the Prophet (ﷺ) said (to that man), "Do in your `Umra the same as you do in yourHajj."
