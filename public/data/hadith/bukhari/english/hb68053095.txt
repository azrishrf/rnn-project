68 | 53 | 95 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: The Prophet (ﷺ) said to those who were involved in a case of Lian, "Your accounts are with Allah. One ofyou two is a liar. You (husband) have right on her (wife)." The husband said, "My money, O Allah'sApostle!" The Prophet (ﷺ)said, "You are not entitled to take back any money. If you have told the truth, the Mahr that you paid,was for having sexual relations with her lawfully; and if you are a liar, then you are less entitled to getit back."
