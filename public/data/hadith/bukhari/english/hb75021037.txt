75 | 21 | 37 | Narrated Jabir bin `Abdullah: The Prophet (ﷺ) came to me while I was ill. He performed ablution and threw the remaining water on me(or said, "Pour it on him) " When I came to my senses I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I have no son orfather to be my heir, so how will be my inheritance?" Then the Verse of inheritance was revealed.
