84 | 3 | 3 | Narrated Abu Huraira: A man came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "I am ruined!" The Prophet (ﷺ) said to him, "What is thematter?" He said, "I have done a sexual relation with my wife (while fasting) in Ramadan." TheProphet said to him?" "Can you afford to manumit a slave?" He said, "No." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Canyou fast for two successive months?" He said, "No." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Can you feed sixty poorpersons?" He said, "No." Then an Ansari man came with an Irq (a big basket full of dates). TheProphet said (to the man), "Take this (basket) and give it in charity." That man said, "To poorer peoplethan we, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)? By Him Who has sent you with the Truth! There is no house in betweenthe two mountains (of the city of Medina) poorer than we." So the Prophet (ﷺ) said (to him), "Go and feedit to your family."
