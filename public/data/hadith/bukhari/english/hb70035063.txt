70 | 35 | 63 | Narrated Anas: I was a young boy when I once was walking with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) . Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) entered the houseof his slave tailor and the latter brought a dish filled with food covered with pieces of gourd. Allah'sApostle started picking and eating the gourd. When I saw that, I started collecting and placing thegourd before him. Then the slave returned to his work. Anas added: I have kept on loving gourd sinceI saw Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) doing what he was doing.
