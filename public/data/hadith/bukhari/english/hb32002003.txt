32 | 2 | 3 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: Some men amongst the companions of the Prophet (ﷺ) were shown in their dreams that the night of Qadrwas in the last seven nights of Ramadan. Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "It seems that all your dreams agreethat (the Night of Qadr) is in the last seven nights, and whoever wants to search for it (i.e. the Night ofQadr) should search in the last seven (nights of Ramadan).
