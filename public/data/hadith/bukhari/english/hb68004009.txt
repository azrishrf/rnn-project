68 | 4 | 9 | Narrated `Aisha: A man divorced his wife thrice (by expressing his decision to divorce her thrice), then she marriedanother man who also divorced her. The Prophet (ﷺ) was asked if she could legally marry the firsthusband (or not). The Prophet (ﷺ) replied, "No, she cannot marry the first husband unless the secondhusband consummates his marriage with her, just as the first husband had done."
