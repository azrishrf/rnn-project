I replied, "Labbaik, 0 Allah's Messenger (ﷺ), and Sa`daik!"he said, "Do you know what is Allah's right upon his slave?" I said, "Allah and His Apostle knowbest" He said "Allah's right upon his slaves is that they should worship Him alone and not worshipanything else besides Him." Then he proceeded for a while and then said, "O Mu`adh bin Jabal!" Ireplied, "Labbaik, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ):, Sa`daik!' He said, "Do you know what is the right of theslaves upon Allah if they do that?" I replied, "Allah and His Apostle know best." He said, "The rightof the slaves upon Allah is that He will not punish them (if they do that).
