96 | 12 | 40 | Narrated Abu Huraira: A bedouin came to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and said, "My wife has delivered a black boy, and I suspect that heis not my child." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said to him, "Have you got camels?" The bedouin said, "Yes." TheProphet said, "What color are they?" The bedouin said, "They are red." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Are any ofthem Grey?" He said, "There are Grey ones among them." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Whence do you thinkthis color came to them?" The bedouin said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! It resulted from hereditarydisposition." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "And this (i.e., your child) has inherited his color from his ancestors."The Prophet (ﷺ) did not allow him to deny his paternity of the child.
