87 | 4 | 16 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: A Jew crushed the head of a girl between two stones, and the girl was asked, "Who has done that toyou, so-and-so or so and so?" (Some names were mentioned for her) till the name of that Jew wasmentioned (whereupon she agreed). The Jew was brought to the Prophet (ﷺ) and the Prophet (ﷺ) kept onquestioning him till he confessed, whereupon his head was crushed with stones.
