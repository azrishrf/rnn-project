67 | 94 | 139 | Narrated `Aisha: An Ansari woman gave her daughter in marriage and the hair of the latter started falling out. TheAnsari women came to the Prophet (ﷺ) and mentioned that to him and said, "Her (my daughter's) husbandsuggested that I should let her wear false hair." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "No, (don't do that) for Allah sendsHis curses upon such ladies who lengthen their hair artificially."
