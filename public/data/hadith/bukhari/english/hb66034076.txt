66 | 34 | 76 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Amr: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said to me, "Recite the whole Qur'an in one month's time." I said, "But I have power(to do more than that)." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Then finish the recitation of the Qur'an in seven days,and do not finish it in less than this period."
