61 | 28 | 148 | Narrated Anas: Once two men from the companions of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) went out of the house of the Prophet (ﷺ) on a verydark night. They were accompanied by two things that resembled two lamps lighting the way in frontof them, and when they parted, each of them was accompanied by one of those two things (lamps) tillthey reached their homes.
