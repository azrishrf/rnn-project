94 | 4 | 6 | Narrated Aisha: One night the Prophet (ﷺ) was unable to sleep and said, "Would that a righteous man from mycompanions guarded me tonight." Suddenly we heard the clatter of arms, whereupon the Prophet (ﷺ) said,"Who is it?" It was said, "I am Sa`d, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! I have come to guard you." The Prophet (ﷺ) thenslept so soundly that we heard him snoring. Abu `Abdullah said: `Aisha said: Bilal said, "Would that Ibut stayed overnight in a valley with Idhkhir and Jalil (two kinds of grass) around me (i.e., inMecca)." Then I told that to the Prophet (ﷺ) .
