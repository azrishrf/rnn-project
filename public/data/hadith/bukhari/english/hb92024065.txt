92 | 24 | 65 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Soon the river "Euphrates" will disclose the treasure (the mountain) of gold, sowhoever will be present at that time should not take anything of it." Al-A'raj narrated from AbiiHuraira that the Prophet (ﷺ) said the same but he said, "It (Euphrates) will uncover a mountain of gold(under it).
