52 | 13 | 23 | Narrated `Uqba bin Al-Harith: That he had married Um Yahya bint Abu Ihab. He said. "A black slave-lady came and said, 'I suckledyou both.' I then mentioned that to the Prophet (ﷺ) who turned his face aside." `Uqba further said, "I wentto the other side and told the Prophet (ﷺ) about it. He said, 'How can you (keep her as your wife) when thelady has said that she suckled both of you (i.e. you and your wife?)" So, the Prophet (ﷺ) ordered him todivorce her.
