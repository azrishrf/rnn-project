67 | 34 | 60 | Narrated Zainab bint Salama: Um Habiba said to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) "We have heard that you want to marry Durra bint Abu-Salama."Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Can she be married along with Um Salama (her mother)? Even if I have notmarried Um Salama, she would not be lawful for me to marry, for her father is my foster brother."
