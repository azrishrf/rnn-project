96 | 16 | 60 | Narrated Hisham's father: `Aisha said to `Abdullah bin Az-Zubair, "Bury me with my female companions (i.e. the wives of theProphet) and do not bury me with the Prophet (ﷺ) in the house, for I do not like to be regarded assanctified (just for being buried there).''Narrated Hisham's father: `Umar sent a message to `Aisha, saying, "Will you allow me to be buriedwith my two companions (the Prophet (ﷺ) and Abu Bakr) ?" She said, "Yes, by Allah." though it was herhabit that if a man from among the companions (of the Prophet (ﷺ) ) sent her a message asking her toallow him to be buried there, she would say, "No, by Allah, I will never give permission to anyone tobe buried with them."
