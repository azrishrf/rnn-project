11 | 16 | 30 | Narrated Yahya bin Sa`id: I asked `Amra about taking a bath on Fridays. She replied, " Aisha said, 'The people used to work (fortheir livelihood) and whenever they went for the Jumua prayer, they used to go to the mosque in thesame shape as they had been in work. So they were asked to take a bath on Friday.' "
