10 | 24 | 36 | Narrated Abu Huraira: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) went out (of the mosque) when the Iqama had been pronounced and the rowsstraightened. The Prophet (ﷺ) stood at his Musalla (praying place) and we waited for the Prophet (ﷺ) to beginthe prayer with Takbir. He left and asked us to remain in our places. We kept on standing till theProphet returned and the water was trickling from his head for he had taken a bath (of Janaba).
