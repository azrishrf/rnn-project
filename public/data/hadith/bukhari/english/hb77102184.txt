77 | 102 | 184 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: We were coming from Khaibar along with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) while l was riding behind Abu Talhaand he was proceeding. While one of the wives of Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) was riding behind Allah's Messenger (ﷺ),suddenly the foot of the camel Slipped and I said, "The woman!" and alighted (hurriedly). Allah'sApostle said, "She is your mother." Sol resaddled the she-camel and Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) mounted it.When he approached or saw Medina, he said, "Ayibun, ta'ibun, 'abidun, li-Rabbina hami-dun."
