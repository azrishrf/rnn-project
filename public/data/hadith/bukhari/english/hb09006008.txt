9 | 6 | 8 | Narrated Abu Huraira: I heard Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) saying, "If there was a river at the door of anyone of you and he took a bath init five times a day would you notice any dirt on him?" They said, "Not a trace of dirt would be left."The Prophet (ﷺ) added, "That is the example of the five prayers with which Allah blots out (annuls) evildeeds."
