74 | 3 | 9 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: I was serving Abu 'Ubaida, Abu Talha and Ubai bin Ka`b with a drink prepared from ripe and unripedates. Then somebody came to them and said, "Alcoholic drinks have been prohibited." (On hearingthat) Abu Talha said, "Get up. O Anas, and pour (throw) it out! So I poured (threw) it out.
