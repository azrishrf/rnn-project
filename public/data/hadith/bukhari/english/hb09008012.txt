9 | 8 | 12 | Narrated Anas: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Whenever anyone of you offers his prayer he is speaking in private to his Lord. Sohe should not spit to his right but under his left foot." Qatada said, "He should not spit in front of himbut to his left or under his feet." And Shu`ba said, "He should not spit in front of him, nor to his rightbut to his left or under his foot." Anas said: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "He should neither spit in the directionof his Qibla nor to his right but to his left or under his foot."
