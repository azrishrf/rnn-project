34 | 64 | 102 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Don't keep camels and sheep unmilked for a long time, for whoever buys such ananimal has the option to milk it and then either to keep it or return it to the owner along with one Sa ofdates." Some narrated from Ibn Seereen (that the Prophet (ﷺ) had said), "One Sa of wheat, and he has theoption for three days." And some narrated from Ibn Seereen, " ... a Sa of dates," not mentioning theoption for three days. But a Sa of dates is mentioned in most narrations.
