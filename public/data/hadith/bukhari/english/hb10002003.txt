10 | 2 | 3 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: When the number of Muslims increased they discussed the question as to how to know the time forthe prayer by some familiar means. Some suggested that a fire be lit (at the time of the prayer) andothers put forward the proposal to ring the bell. Bilal was ordered to pronounce the wording of Adhantwice and of the Iqama once only.
