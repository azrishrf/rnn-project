76 | 10 | 15 | Narrated Um Qais bint Mihsan: I heard the Prophet (ﷺ) saying, "Treat with the Indian incense, for it has healing for seven diseases; it is tobe sniffed by one having throat trouble, and to be put into one side of the mouth of one suffering frompleurisy." Once I went to Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) with a son of mine who would not eat any food, and the boypassed urine on him whereupon he asked for some water and sprinkled it over the place of urine.
