6 | 24 | 32 | Narrated `Aisha: Fatima bint Abi Hubaish asked the Prophet, "I got persistent bleeding (in between the periods) and donot become clean. Shall I give up prayers?" He replied, "No, this is from a blood vessel. Give up theprayers only for the days on which you usually get the menses and then take a bath and offer yourprayers."
