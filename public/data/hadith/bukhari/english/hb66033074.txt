66 | 33 | 74 | Narrated `Abdullah bin Mas`ud: The Prophet (ﷺ) said to me, "Recite (the Qur'an) to me." I said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) Shall I recite (theQur'an) to you while it has been revealed to you?" He said, "Yes." So I recited Surat-An-Nisa' (TheWomen), but when I recited the Verse:'How (will it be) then when We bring from each nation a witness and We bring you (O Muhammad)as a witness against these people.' (4.41) He said, "Enough for the present," I looked at him andbehold! His eyes were overflowing with tears
