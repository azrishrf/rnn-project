95 | 1 | 5 | Narrated `Umar: There was a man from the Ansar (who was a friend of mine). If he was not present in the company ofAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) I used to be present with Allah's Messenger (ﷺ), I would tell him what I used to hear fromAllah's Messenger (ﷺ), and when I was absent from Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) he used to be present with him, and hewould tell me what he used to hear from Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) .
