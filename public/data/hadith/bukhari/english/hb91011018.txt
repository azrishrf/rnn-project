91 | 11 | 18 | Narrated Abu Huraira: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I have been given the keys of eloquent speech and given victory with awe (castinto the hearts of the enemy), and while I was sleeping last night, the keys of the treasures of the earthwere brought to me till they were put in my hand." Abu Huraira added: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) left (thisworld) and now you people are carrying those treasures from place to place.
