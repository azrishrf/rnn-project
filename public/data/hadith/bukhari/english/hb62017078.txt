62 | 17 | 78 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: The Prophet (ﷺ) sent an army under the command of Usama bin Zaid. When some people criticized hisleadership, the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "If you are criticizing Usama's leadership, you used to criticize hisfather's leadership before. By Allah! He was worthy of leadership and was one of the dearest personsto me, and (now) this (i.e. Usama) is one of the dearest to me after him (i.e. Zaid).
