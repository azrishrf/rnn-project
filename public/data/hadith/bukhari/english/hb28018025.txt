28 | 18 | 25 | Narrated Anas bin Malik: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) entered Mecca in the year of its Conquest wearing an Arabian helmet on his head andwhen the Prophet (ﷺ) took it off, a person came and said, "Ibn Khatal is holding the covering of the Ka`ba(taking refuge in the Ka`ba)." The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Kill him."
