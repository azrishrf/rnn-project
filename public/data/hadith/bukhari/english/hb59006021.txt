59 | 6 | 21 | Narrated Abu Huraira: I heard the Prophet (ﷺ) saying, "Who ever spends a couple (of objects) in Allah's cause, will be called bythe Gatekeepers of Paradise who will say, "O so-and-so, come on!" Abu Bakr said, "Such a personwill never perish or be miserable' The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I hope you will be among such person."
