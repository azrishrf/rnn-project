68 | 19 | 35 | Narrated Ibn 'Abbas: Qariba,The daughter of Abi Umaiyya, was the wife of 'Umar bin Al-Khattab.'Umar divorced her and then Mu'awiyya bin Abi Sufyan married her.Similarly, Um Al-Hakam, the daughter of Abi Sufyan was the wife of'Iyad bin Ghanm Al-Fihri. He divorced her and then 'Abdullah bin'Uthman Al-Thaqafi married her.
