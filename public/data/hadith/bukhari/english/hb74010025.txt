74 | 10 | 25 | Narrated Abu Al-Juwairiyya: I asked Ibn `Abbas about Al-Badhaq. He said, "Muhammad prohibited alcoholic drinks before It wascalled Al-Badhaq (by saying), 'Any drink that intoxicates is unlawful.' I said, 'What about good lawfuldrinks?' He said,'Apart from what is lawful and good, all other things are unlawful and not good(unclean Al-Khabith).
