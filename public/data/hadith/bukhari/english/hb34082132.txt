34 | 82 | 132 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Do not sell fruits of dates until they become free from all the dangers of beingspoilt or blighted; and do not sell fresh dates for dry dates."Narrated Salim and `Abdullah from Zaid bin Habit' "Later on Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) permitted the selling ofripe fruits on trees for fresh dates or dried dates in Bai'-al-'Araya, and did not allow it for any otherkind of sale."
