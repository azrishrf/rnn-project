52 | 11 | 21 | Narrated Al-Miswar bin Makhrama: Some outer garments were received the Prophet (ﷺ) and my father (Makhrama) said to me, "Let us go tothe Prophet (ﷺ) so that he may give us something from the garments." So, my father stood at the door andspoke. The Prophet (ﷺ) recognized his voice and came out carrying a garment and telling Makhrama thegood qualities of that garment, adding, "I have kept this for you, I have sent this for you."
