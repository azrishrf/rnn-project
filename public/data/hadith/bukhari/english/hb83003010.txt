83 | 3 | 10 | Narrated `Aisha: Hind bint `Utba bin Rabi`a said, "O Allah 's Apostle! (Before I embraced Islam), there was no familyon the surface of the earth, I wish to have degraded more than I did your family. But today there is nofamily whom I wish to have honored more than I did yours." Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "I thoughtsimilarly, by Him in Whose Hand Muhammad's soul is!" Hind said, "O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)! (Myhusband) Abu Sufyan is a miser. Is it sinful of me to feed my children from his property?" TheProphet said, "No, unless you take it for your needs what is just and reasonable."
