67 | 77 | 117 | Narrated Sahl: When Abu Usaid As-Sa`idi got married, he invited the Prophet (ﷺ) and his companions. None preparedthe food for them and brought it to them but his wife. She soaked some dates in water in a stone potovernight, and when the Prophet (ﷺ) had finished his food, she provided him with that drink (of soakeddates).
