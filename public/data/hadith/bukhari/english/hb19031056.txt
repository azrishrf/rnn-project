19 | 31 | 56 | Narrated `Abdur Rahman bin Abi Laila: Only Um Hani narrated to me that she had seen the Prophet (ﷺ) offering the Duha prayer. She said, "Onthe day of the conquest of Mecca, the Prophet (ﷺ) entered my house, took a bath and offered eight rak`at(of Duha prayers. I had never seen the Prophet (ﷺ) offering such a light prayer but he performed bowingand prostrations perfectly .
