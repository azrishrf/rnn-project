30 | 50 | 74 | Narrated Abu Sa`id Al-Khudri: Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "Do not fast continuously day and night (practice Al-Wisal) and if anyone of youintends to fast continuously day and night, he should continue till the Suhur time." They said, "Butyou practice Al-Wisal, O Allah's Messenger (ﷺ)!" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I am not similar to you;. during mysleep I have One Who makes me eat and drink."
