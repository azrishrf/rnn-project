37 | 4 | 5 | Narrated Aisha: (the wife of the Prophet) Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) and Abu Bakr hired a man from the tribe of Bani-Ad-Dil asan expert guide who was a pagan (follower of the religion of the pagans of Quraish). The Prophet (ﷺ) andAbu Bakr gave him their two riding camels and took a promise from him to bring their riding camelsin the morning of the third day to the Cave of Thaur.
