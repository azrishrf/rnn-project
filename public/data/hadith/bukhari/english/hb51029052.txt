51 | 29 | 52 | Narrated Ibn `Umar: `Umar saw a silken cloak over a man for sale and requested the Prophet (ﷺ) to buy it in order to wear it onFridays and while meeting delegates. The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "This is worn by the one who will have noshare in the Hereafter." Later on Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) got some silken cloaks similar to that one, and hesent one to `Umar. `Umar said to the Prophet (ﷺ) "How can I wear it, while you said about it what yousaid?" The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "I have not given it to you to wear, but to sell or to give to someone else." So,`Umar sent it to his brother at Mecca before he embraced Islam.
