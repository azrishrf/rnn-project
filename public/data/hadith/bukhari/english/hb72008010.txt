72 | 8 | 10 | Narrated `Adi bin Hatim: And it has also been narrated by `Adi bin Hatim that he asked the Prophet (ﷺ)"If a hunter throws an arrow at the game and after tracing it for two or three days he finds it dead butstill bearing his arrow, (can he eat of it)?" The Prophet (ﷺ) replied, "He can eat if he wishes."
