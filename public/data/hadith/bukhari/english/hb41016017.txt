41 | 16 | 17 | Narrated `Abdullah bin `Umar: While the Prophet (ﷺ) was passing the night at his place of rest in Dhul-Hulaifa in the bottom of the valley(of Aqiq), he saw a dream and it was said to him, "You are in a blessed valley." Musa said, "Salim letour camels kneel at the place where `Abdullah used to make his camel kneel, seeking the place whereAllah's Messenger (ﷺ) used to take a rest, which is situated below the mosque which is in the bottom of thevalley; it is midway between the mosque and the road."
