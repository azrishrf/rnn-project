3|644|  Chapter: Law of Shari’ah pertaining to the plaited hair of the woman who takes a bath.  This Hadith has been narrated by Amr al-Naqid, Yazid b. Harun, ‘Abd b. Humaid, Abd  al-Razzaq, Thauri, Ayyub b. Musa, with the same chain of transmitters. In Hadith  narrated by Abd al-Razzaq there is a mention of the menstruation and of the sexual  intercourse. The rest of the Hadith has been transmitted like that of Ibn ‘Uyaina.
