33|6396|  Chapter: The growth of a child in the womb of a mother and his destiny in regard to his  livelihood, his deeds. both good and evil.  Hadhaifa b. Usaid Ghifari, a Companion of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him), reported it directly from Allah’s Messenger (may peace upon him).as he said:  There is an angel who looks after the womb when Allah decides to create anything after  more than forty nights are over; the rest of the hadith is the same.
