4|1477|  Chapter: Compensation of the missed prayer and excellence of observing it promptly.  Salim b. ‘Abdullah (b. ‘Umar) reported on the authority of his father that Allah’s  Messenger (way peace be upon him) observed the prayer of a traveller, i.e. two rak’ahs  in Mina, and other places; so did Abu Bakr and ‘Umar, and ‘Uthman too observed two  rak’ahs at the beginning of his caliphate, but he then completed four.
