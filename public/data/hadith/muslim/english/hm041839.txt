4|1839|  Chapter: Jumu’a Prayer.  ‘Aisha reported: The people came for Jumu’a prayer from their houses in the  neighbouring villages dressed in woollen garments on which dust was settled and this  emitted a foal smell. A person among them (those who were dressed so) came to the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) while he was in my house. The  Messenger of Allah (may peace he upon him) said to him: Were you to cleanse  yourselves on this day.
