40|6822|  Chapter: The haughty and the proud would Set into the fire of hell and the humble and meek  would get into Paradise.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: The  Paradise and the Hell disputed with each other. The rest of the hadith is the same as  transmitted by Abu Huraira up to the words’. “It is essential for Me to fill up both of you.”           INTIMATES
