7|2874|  Chapter: Talbiya of the Apostle (may peace be upon him) and his sacrifice.  Abd Nadra reported: While I was in the company of Jibir, a person came and said:  There is difference of opinion among Ibn Abbas and Ibn Zubair about two Mut’as  (benefits, Tamattul in Hajj and temporary marriage with women), whereupon jibir said:  We have been doing this during the lifetime of Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon  him), and then ‘Umar forbade us to do so, and we never resorted to them.
