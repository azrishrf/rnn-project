4|1980|  Chapter: Mention of the torment of grave in eclipse prayer.  Abu Juraij narrated this Hadith with the same chain of transmitters (but with the addition  of these words): “It was for a long duration that he (the Holy Prophet) observed qiyam  and he would then observe ruku’. (The narrator also added) I (Asma’) looked at a  woman who was older than I, and at another who was weaker than I.
