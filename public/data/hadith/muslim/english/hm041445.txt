4|1445|  Chapter: Who deserves most to act as Imam.  Khufaf b. Ima’ al-Ghifari reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  aid in prayer: O Allah I curse the tribes of Lihyan, Ri’l, Dhakwan, and ‘Usayya for they  disobeyed Allah and His Messenger (may peace be upon him). Allah pardoned (the  tribe of) Ghifar and Allah granted protection to (the tribe of) Aslam
