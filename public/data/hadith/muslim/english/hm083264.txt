8|3264|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.  Malik narrated this hadith on the authority of the same chain of transmitters that ‘Ali b.  Abil Talib said to a person: You are a person led astray; Allah’s Messenger (May peace         be upon him) forbade us (to do Mut’a), as is stated in the hadith transmitted on the  authority of Yahya b. Malik.
