32|6190|  Chapter: The preference of benevolent treatment to parents over voluntary prayers, etc.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Let him  be humbled, let him be humbled. It was said: Allah’s Messenger, who is he? He said.  He who finds his parents in old age, either one or both of them, and does not enter  Paradise.  OF RELATIONSHIP
