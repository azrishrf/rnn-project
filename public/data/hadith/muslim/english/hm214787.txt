21|4787|  Chapter: Pertaining to the flesh of the Lizard.  A hadith pertaining to the eating of the lizard is transmitted from the Holy Prophet (may  peace be upon him) on the authority of Ibn ‘Umar, but in this very hadith narrated  through a different chain of transmitters there is a slight variation of wording (and the  words are): “A lizard was brought to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) but he  neither ate that nor declared it unlawful.” And in the hadith transmitted through Usama         (the words are): “The man (inquirer) was standing in the mosque and Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) was sitting on the pulpit.”
