6|2511|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  This Hadith has been narrated from Jarir on the authority of A’mash with the same chain  of transmitters and he said (these words with a little bit of variation from the previous  Hadith): When (fasting) in Ramadan was (made) obligatory, he abandoned it (the  practice of observing fast on Ashura).
