7|2902|  Chapter: Excellence of walking at a quick pace in Tawaf and Umra and on the occasion  of first Tawaf in Hajj.         Abu Tufail reported: I said to Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them): People are of the  view that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) moved quickly round the House  and between al-Safa and al-Marwa, and (thus) it is Sunnah. He said: They told the truth  and they told the lie.
