10|3765|  Chapter: Merit of giving respite to one who is in straitened circumstances.  Jabir (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him)  visited Umm Mubashshir al-Ansariya at her orchard of date-palms and said to her: Who  has planted these trees of dates-a Muslim or a non-Muslim? She said: A Muslim, of  course, whereupon he said: Never a Muslim plants, or cultivates a land, and it out of  that men eat, or the animals eat, or anything else eats, but that becomes charity on his  (planter’s) behalf.
