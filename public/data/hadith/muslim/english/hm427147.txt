42|7147|  Chapter: To state Hadith after Its verification and recording of “Knowledge”.  Abu Sa’id Khudri reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: Do  not take down anything from me, and he who took down anything from me except the  Qur’an, he should efface that and narrate from me, for there is no harm in it and he who  attributed any falsehood to me-and Hammam said: I think he also said: “deliberately”-he  should in fact find his abode in the Hell-Fire.
