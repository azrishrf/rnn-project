25|5364|  Chapter: Disapproval of saying: “It is I” In response to one’s inquiry while seeking permission to  get in the house.  Jabir b. Abdullah reported: I sought permission from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) to see him. He said: Who is it? I said: It is I. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) said: It is I, it is I (these words lead me to no conclusion).
