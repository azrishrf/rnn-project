41|6983|  Chapter: The Last Hour would not come until a person would pass by a grave and wish that he  should have been the occupant of that grave because of this calamity.  Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: You  and the Jews would fight against one another until a stone would say: Muslim, here is a  Jew behind me; come and kill him.
