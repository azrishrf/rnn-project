22|4838|  Chapter: Of what age the animal is to be sacrificed.  Uqba b. ‘Amir reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) gave the gifts  of goats to be distributed amongst his Companions. They sacrificed them, but a lamb of  one year of age was left. (Someone) made a mention of that to the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him), whereupon he said: You sacrifice it.
