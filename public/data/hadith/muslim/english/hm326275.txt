32|6275|  Chapter: Merit of benevolent treatment.  This hadith has been reported by Miqdam b. Shuraih b. Hani with the same chain of  transmitters but with this addition: ‘Aisha mounted upon a wild camel and she began to  make that go round and round. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  said: You should show kindness, and then he made a mention of this hadith.  OF RELATIONSHIP
