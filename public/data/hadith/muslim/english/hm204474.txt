20|4474|  Chapter: The people are subservient to the Quraish and the Caliphate is the right of the  Quraish.  It has been narrated on the authority of Hammam b. Munabbih who said: This is one of  the traditions narrated by Abu Huraira from the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) who said: People are subservient to the Quraish: the Muslims among them being  subservient to the Muslims among them, and the disbelievers among them being  subservient to the disbelievers among them.
