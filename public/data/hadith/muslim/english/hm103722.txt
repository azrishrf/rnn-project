10|3722|  Chapter: Leasing out land.  Jabir b. Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) say: He who has surplus of land should either cultivate it himself, or let his  brother cultivate it, an should not sell it. I (the narrator) said to Sa’id: What does his  statement” do not sell it” mean? Does it imply” rent”? He said: Yes.
