9|3578|  Chapter: Emancipating a slave.  Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger may peace be upon  him) as saying: If anyone emancipates his share in a slave and has enough money to  pay the full price for him, a fair price for the slave should be fixed, his partners given  their shares, and the slave be thus emancipated, otherwise he is emancipated only to  the extent of the first man’s share.
