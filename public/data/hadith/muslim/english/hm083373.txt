8|3373|  Chapter: Al-Azl (incomplete sexual intercourse): Coitus Interruptus.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported: We took women captives, and  we wanted to do ‘azl with them. We then asked Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon  him) about it and he said to us: Verily you do it, verily you do it, verily you do it, but the  soul which has to be born until the Day of judgment must be born.
