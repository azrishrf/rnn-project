30|5756|  Chapter: The hatred of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) against sin and the adopting  of a course of action which is easier to adopt from amongst the permissible acts).  ‘Aisha reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) never beat anyone  with his hand, neither a woman nor a servant, but only, in the case when he had been  fighting in the cause of Allah and he never took revenge for anything unless the things  made inviolable by Allah were made violable; he then took revenge for Allah, the  Exalted and Glorious.
