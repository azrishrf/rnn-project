7|2829|  Chapter: Permissibility of the Tamattu’ (form of pilgrimage).  ‘Imran b. al-Husain (Allah be pleased with him) said: Know well that Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) combined ‘Hajj and ‘Umra, and nothing was revealed in the  Book (to abrogate it), and the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) too did not  forbid us from (combining) them. And whatever a person said was out of his personal  opinion.
