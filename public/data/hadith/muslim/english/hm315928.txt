31|5928|  Chapter: Merits of Sa’d b. Abi Waqqis (Allah be pleased with him).         ‘Abdullah b. Shaddad reported that he heard saying: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) did not gather his parents except in case of Sa’d b. Malik that he said to him  on the Day of Ubud: Shoot an arrow, may my father and mother be taken as ransom for  you.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
