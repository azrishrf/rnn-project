4|958|  Chapter: Moderation in the articles of prayers and their shortening and perfection.  Al-Bara’ (b. ‘Azib), and he was no liar (but a truthful Companion of the Holy Prophet),  reported: They used to say prayer behind the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him). I never saw anyone bending his back at the time when he (the Holy Prophet)  raised his head, till the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) placed his  forehead on the ground. They then fell in prostration after him.
