4|1438|  Chapter: Who deserves most to act as Imam.         ‘Asim reported: I heard Anas saying: Never did I ace the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) so much grieved (at the loss of a) small army as I saw him grieved  at those seventy men who were called “reciters” (and were killed) at Bi’r Ma’una; and he  invoked curse for full one month upon their murderers.
