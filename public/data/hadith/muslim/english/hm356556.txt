35|6556|  Chapter: Supplication at the time of sleeping and going to bed.  Anas reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: When you go to  bed, say: “Praise is due to Allah Who fed us, provided us drink, sufficed us and provided  us with shelter, for many a people there is none to suffice and none to provide shelter.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
