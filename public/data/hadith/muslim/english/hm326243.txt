32|6243|  Chapter: Whenever a believer falls sick or is stricken with grief or something tike it,  there is a reward for him even if it is pricking of a thorn.  Abu Huraira reported that when this verse was revealed: “Whoever does evil will be  requited for it”, and when this was conveyed to the Muslims they were greatly perturbed.  Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: Be moderate and stand  firm in trouble that falls to the lot of a Muslim (as that) is an expiation for him; even  stumbling on the path or the pricking of a thorn (are an expiation for him).
