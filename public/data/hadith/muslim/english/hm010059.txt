1|59|  Chapter: Concerning the branches of Iman.  It is narrated on the authority of ‘Imran b. Husain that the Prophet (may peace and  blessings be upon him) said: Modesty brings forth nothing but goodness. Bushair b.  Ka’b said: It is recorded in the books of wisdom, there lies sobriety in it and calmness of  mind in it, Imran said: I am narrating to you the tradition of the Messenger of Allah (may  peace and blessings be upon him) and you talk of your books.
