7|3008|  Chapter: Excellence of making a halt at al Muha’ab, on the day of Nahr, and observing  prayer there.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported.: Halt at al-Abtah is not the Sunnah. Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) halted there simply because it was easier for him  to depart from there, when he left.
