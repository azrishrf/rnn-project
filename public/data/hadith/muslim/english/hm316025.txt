31|6025|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud and that of his mother (Allah be pleased with them).  Masruq reported: We were in the company of Abdullah b ‘Amr that we made a mention  of a hadith from Abdullah b. Mas’ud; thereupon he said: That is a person whose love  ever remains (fresh in my heart) after I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) as saying: Learn Qur’an from four persons: Ibn Umm ‘Abd, i.e. Abdullah b. Mas’ud  and he started from his name-then Ubayy b. Ka’b and Mu’adh b Jabal. Zuhri did not  make a mention of the words yaquluhu in his narration  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
