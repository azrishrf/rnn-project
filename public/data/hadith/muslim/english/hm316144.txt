31|6144|  Chapter: Fraternity established by Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) amongst the  companions (Allah be pleased with them).  It was said to Anas b. Malik: You must have heard this that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) said: There is no alliance (hilf) of brotherhood in Islam. Anas said:  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) established the bond of fraternity between  the Quraish and the Ansar in his home.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
