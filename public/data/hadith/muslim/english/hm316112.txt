31|6112|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked blessings upon the tribes of Ghifar  and Aslam.         Abu Dharr reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Go to your  people and say that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) says: Ghifar (is a  tribe) to whom Allah granted pardon, and Aslam (is the tribe) to whom Allah granted  safety.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
