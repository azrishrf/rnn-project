4|1426|  Chapter: Excellence of sitting at the place of worship after the dawn prayer and excellence of  the mosque.  Malik b Huwairith reported: I came to the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him)  along with a companion of mine, and when we intended to return from him, he said:  When there is time for prayer, announce prayer, pronounce Iqama, and the oldest  amongst you should lead the prayer.
