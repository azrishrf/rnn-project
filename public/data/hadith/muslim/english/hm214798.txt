21|4798|  Chapter: Pertaining to the flesh of the Lizard.  Abu Zubair reported: I asked Jabir about the eating) of the lizard, whereupon he said:  Don’t eat that as he (the Holy Prophet) felt disgust. He (the narrator) said that Umar b.  al-Khattab reminded: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) did not declare it to be  unlawful. Allah, the Exalted and Majestic, has (made it a source) of benefit for more  than one (persons). It is a common diet of the shepherds. Had it been with me, I would  have eaten that.
