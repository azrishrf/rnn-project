4|1646|  Chapter: Forenoon prayer should be offered when it is sufficiently hot.  Anas b. Sirin reported: I asked Ibn ‘Umar like this (as recorded in the previous Hadith)  and he made this addition: “And he (the Holy Prophet) made the end of the night prayer  as odd number by one rak’ah.” And there is also (this addition): “Stop, stop, you are  bulky.”
