4|1401|  Chapter: Merit of praying in congregation and waiting for prayer.  Abu Musa reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The most  eminent among human beings (as a recipient of) reward (is one) who lives farthest  away, and who has to walk the farthest distance, and he who waits for the prayer to  observe it along with the Imam, his reward is greater than one who prays (alone) and  then goes to sleep. In the narration of Abu Kuraib (the words are): “(He waits) till he  prays along with the Imam in congregation.”
