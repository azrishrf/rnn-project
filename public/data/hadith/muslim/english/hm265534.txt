26|5534|  Chapter: Kahanat (Divination) is prohibited and it is forbidden to go to Kahin.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Muâ€™awiya b. Hakam as-Sulami through  another chain of transmitters. The hadith transmitted on the authority of Yahya b. Abu  Kathir (there is an addition of these words): I said: Among us there are men who draw  lines and thus make divination. What about this? Thereupon he (the Holy Prophet) said:  There was a Prophet who drew lines, so whose lines agree with his line for him it is  allowable.
