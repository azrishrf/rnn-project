4|1202|  Chapter: Prostration while reciting the Qur’an.  ‘Abdullah b. Zubair narrated on the authority of his father that when the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) sat for supplication, i.e. tashahhud (blessing and  supplication), he placed his right hand on his right thigh and his left hand on his left  thigh, and pointed with his forefinger, and placed his thumb on his (middle) finger, and  covered his knee with the palm of his left hand..
