32|6239|  Chapter: Whenever a believer falls sick or is stricken with grief or something tike it,  there is a reward for him even if it is pricking of a thorn.  ‘Aisha reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: A believer does  not undergo (the trouble) of running a thorn or more than that when Allah effaces his  sins.
