10|3622|  Chapter: It is forbidden that one should enter into a transaction on which one’s brother  has already been negotiating, or one should purchase (in opposition) to one’s brother,  or one should cheat and retain milk in the under (in order to deceive the purchaser).         Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) forbade the outbidding (against another).
