30|5758|  Chapter: The fragrance of the sacred body of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its  agility and softness and the blessings of its touch.  Jabir b. Samura reported: I prayed along with Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) the first prayer. He then went to his family and I also went along with him when he  met some children (on the way). He began to pat the cheeks of each one of them. He  also patted my cheek and I experienced a coolness or a fragrance of his hand as if it  had been brought out from the scent bag of a perfumer.
