15|4038|  Chapter: It is forbidden to take the oaths in the name of anyone else besides Allah.  ‘Abdullah (b. Umar) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) found,  Umar b. al-Khattab amongst the riders and he was taking oath by his father Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) called them (saying); Our Allah, the Exalted and  Majestic, has forbidden you that you take oath by your father. He who bag to take an  oath, he must take it by Allah or keep quiet.
