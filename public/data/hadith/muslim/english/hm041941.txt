4|1941|  Chapter: Permission for sport on ‘Id days in which there in no sin.  ‘Aisha reported: BY Allah, I remember the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  standing on the door of my apartment screening me with his mantle enabling me to see  the sport of the Abyssinians as they played with their daggers in the mosque of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him). He (the Holy Prophet) kept standing for  my sake till I was satiated and then I went back; and thus you can well imagine how  long a girl tender of age who is fond of sports (could have watched it).
