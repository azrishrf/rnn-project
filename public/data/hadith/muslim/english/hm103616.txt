10|3616|  Chapter: Prohibition of Habal al-Habala transaction.  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported that the people of pre-Islamic days  used to sell the meat of the slaughtered camel up to habal al-habala. And habal al- habala implies that a she-camel should give birth and then the (born one should grow  young) and become pregnant. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) forbade  them that (this transaction).
