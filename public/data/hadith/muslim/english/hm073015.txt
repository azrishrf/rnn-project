7|3015|  Chapter: Excellence of making a halt at al Muha’ab, on the day of Nahr, and observing  prayer there.  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him) as saying: God willing, when Allah has granted us victory, our halt tomorrow will be  at Khaif, where they (the unbelievers of Mecca) had taken an oath on unbelief.
