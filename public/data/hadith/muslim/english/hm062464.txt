6|2464|  Chapter: Sexual Intercourse is completely forbidden during the day in the month of Ramadhan.  ‘Abbad b. Abdullah b. Zubair narrated that he heard ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her)  saying: A person came to the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), and he  then narrated the Hadith. But (neither these words are found):”Give charity, give charity”  (nor) his words: “during the day time”.
