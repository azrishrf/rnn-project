4|773|  Chapter: The reciting of Takbir at the time of bowing and rising in prayer except rising after  Ruku’ when it is said: Allah listened to him who praised Him.  Mahmud b. al-Rabi’, on whose face the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  squirted water from the well, reported on the authority of ‘Ubada b. as-Samit that the         Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: He who does not recite Umm Al- Qur’an is not credited with having observed prayer.
