6|2503|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that the Quraish used to observe fast on the  day of Ashura during the pre-Islamic days. The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) then commanded to fast on that day till (fasting) in Ramadan became obligatory.  Then the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: He who wished to fast  should do so and he who wished to break it may do so.
