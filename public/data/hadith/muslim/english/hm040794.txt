4|794|  Chapter: The placing of the right hand over the left hand after the first Takbir in prayer (Takbir-i- Tahrima) below the chest and above the navel and then placing them opposite the shoulders in  prostration.  Shu’ba has narrated this on the authority of Mansur with the same chain of transmitters,  but he made no mention of this: “Then he may choose any supplication which pleases  him.”
