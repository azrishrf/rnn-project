17|4226|  Chapter: Prescribed punishment for (drinking) wine.  Anas b. Malik reported that a person who had drink wine was brought to Allah’s Apostle  (may peace be upon him). He gave him forty stripes with two lashes. Abu Bakr also did  that, but when Umar (assumed the responsibilities) of the Caliphate, he consulted  people and Abd al-Rahman said: The mildest punishment (for drinking) is eighty  (stripes) and ‘Umar their prescribed this punishment.
