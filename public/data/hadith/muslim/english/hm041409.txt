4|1409|  Chapter: Merit of praying in congregation and waiting for prayer.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be’ upon him) said: He who  purified himself in his house, and then he walked to one of the houses of Allah for the  sake of performing a Fard (obligatory act) out of the Fara’id (obligatory acts) of Allah,  both his steps (would be significant) as one of them would obliterate his sin and the  second one would raise his status.
