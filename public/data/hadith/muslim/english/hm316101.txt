31|6101|  Chapter: The merits of the Ansar (Allah be pleased with them).  Anas reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) saw children and  women of the Ansar coming back from a wedding feast. Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) stood up motionless (as a mark of respect) and said: O Allah, (bear witness)  (and addressing the Ansar), said: You are dearest to me amongst people, (and said: O  Allah (bear witness) (and addressing the Ansar), said: You are dearest to me amongst  people. And he meant Ansar.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
