3|703|  Chapter: A man who is sure of his purification, but entertains doubt of anything  breaking it, can safely offer prayer without performing a new ablution.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: If any  one of you has pain in his abdomen, but is doubtful whether or not anything has issued         from him, be should not leave the mosque unless he hears a sound or perceives a  smell.
