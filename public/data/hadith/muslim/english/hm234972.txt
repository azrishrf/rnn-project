23|4972|  Chapter: Permissibility to use Nabidh which is not strong and has not turned into Intoxicant.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that Nabidh was prepared for Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) in the water-skin, Shu’ba said: It was the night of Monday. He drank it on  Monday and on Tuesday up to the afternoon, and If anything was left out of it he gave it  to his servant or poured it out.
