4|1969|  Chapter: Prayer at the time of solar eclipse.  ‘Aisha reported that there was a solar eclipse during the lifetime of the Messenger of  Allah (way peace be upon him) and he sent the announcer (to summon them) for  congregational prayer. The people gathered together and he pronounced takbir and he  observed four rak’ahs, in the form of two rak’ahs (i.e. he observed two qiyams and two  ruku’s in one rak’ah) and four prostrations.
