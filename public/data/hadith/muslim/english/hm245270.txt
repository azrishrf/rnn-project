24|5270|  Chapter: Angels do not enter a house in which there is a dog or a picture.  ‘Abdullah reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Verity the  most grievously tormented people on the Day of Resurrection would be the painters of  pictures. Ashajj (one of the narrators) in the hadith narrated by him did not make  mention of the word “verity”.
