2|550|  Chapter: Instructions pertaining to the licking of a dog.  Hammam b. Munabbih reported: Of the a hadith narrated by Abu Huraira from  Muhammad, the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), one is this: The  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: The purification of the utensil  belonging to one amongst you, after it is licked by a dog, lies in washing it seven times.
