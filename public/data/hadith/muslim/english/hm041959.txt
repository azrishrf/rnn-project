4|1959|  Chapter: Supplication in prayer for rain.  ‘Ubaidullah b. Anas b. Malik heard (his father) Anas b. Malik as saying: A bedouin came  to the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) on Friday as he was (delivering the  sermon on his) pulpit; and the rest of the Hadith is the same but with this addition: “I  saw the cloud clearing just as a sheet is folded.”
