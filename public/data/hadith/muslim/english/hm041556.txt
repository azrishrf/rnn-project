4|1556|  Chapter: Excellence of observing two Rak’ahs in the mosque for one who comes back from a  journey.  Abu Murra narrated on the authority of Umm Hani that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) on the day of the Conquest of Mecca observed in her house eight  rak’ahs of prayer in one cloth, its opposite corners having been tied from the opposite  sides.
