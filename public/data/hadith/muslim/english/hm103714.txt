10|3714|  Chapter: Forbiddance of al-Mubiqala, and al-Muzabana, and al-Mukhabara and the  sale of fruits before their good condition is clear, and al-Mu’awama, i.e., the sale for  some years.  Jabir b. Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) forbade leasing of land, and selling ahead for years and selling of  fruits before they become ripe.
