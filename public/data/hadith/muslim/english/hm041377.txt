4|1377|  Chapter: He who hears the call for prayer it to essential for him to come to the mosque.  Abu Sha’tha’ reported: While we were sitting with Abu Huraira in a mosque a man went  out of the mosque after the call to prayer had been announced. (A man stood up in the  mosque and set off.) Abu Huraira’s eyes followed him till he went out of the mosque.         Upon this Abu Huraira said: This man has disobeyed Abu’l-Qasim (Muhammad) (may  peace be upon him).
