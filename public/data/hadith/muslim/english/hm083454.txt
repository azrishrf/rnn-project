8|3454|  Chapter: Permissibility of bestowing the turn on one’s fellow-wife.  Hisham reported on the authority of his father that ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her)  used to say: Does the woman not feel shy of offering herself to a man? Then Allah the  Exalted and Glorious revealed this verse: “You may defer any of them you wish and  take to yourself any you wish.” I (‘Aisha said): It seems to me that your Lord hastens to  satisfy your desire.
