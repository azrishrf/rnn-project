10|3740|  Chapter: Leasing out land.  Nafi, reported that Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) used to rent the land, and that  he was conveyed the hadith transmitted on the authority of Rafi b. Khadij. He (the  narrator) said: He then went to him along with me. He (Rafi) narrated from some of his  uncles in which it was mentioned that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) forbade  the renting of land. Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) then abandoned this practice  of renting. This hadith has been narrated through another chain of transmitters.
