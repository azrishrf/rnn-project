4|890|  Chapter: The praying women have been commanded not to precede men in lifting their heads  from prostration.  Ibn ‘Umar reported: Grant permission to women for going to the mosque in the night.  His son who was called Waqid said: Then they would make mischief. He (the narrator)  said: He thumped his (son’s) chest and said: I am narrating to you the Hadith of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), and you say: No !
