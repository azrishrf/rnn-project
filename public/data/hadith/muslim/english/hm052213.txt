5|2213|  Chapter: Exhortation to Sadaqa even though it is half a date, or a good word. for they are  protection against fire.  This Hadith has been narrated through another chain of transmitters with a change of  words. In the Hadith transmitted Rauh (the words are): “Out of the honest earning and  its spending at what is its (due) right”; and in the Hadith transmitted by Sulaiman (the  words are): “And its spending at its proper place.”
