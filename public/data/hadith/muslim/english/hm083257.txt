8|3257|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.         ‘Abd al-Malik b. Rabi’ b. Sabraal-Juhanni reported on the authority of his father who  narrated it on the authority of his father (i.e. ‘Abd al-Malik’s grandfather, Sabura al- juhanni): Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon him) permitted us to contract  temporary marriage in the Year of Victory, as we entered Mecca, and we did come out  of it but he forbade us to do it.
