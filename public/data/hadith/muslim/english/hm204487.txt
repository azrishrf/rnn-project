20|4487|  Chapter: Prohibition of desire for a position of authority and covetousness thereof.  It has been reported on the authority of ‘Abd al-Rahman b. Samura who said: The  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said to me: ‘Abd al-Rahman, do not ask  for a position of authority, for if you are granted this position as a result of your asking  for it, you will be left alone (without God’s help to discharge the responsibilities  attendant thereon), and it you are granted it without making any request for it, you will  be helped (by God in the discharge of your duties).
