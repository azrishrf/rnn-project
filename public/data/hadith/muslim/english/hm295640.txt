29|5640|  Chapter: Do not inform the vain sport of devil in a dream.  Jabir reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: There came to  him (the Holy Prophet) a desert Arab and said: I saw in a dream that I had been  beheaded and I had been following it (the severed head). Allah’s Apostle (may peace  be upon him) reprimanded him saying: Do not inform about the vain sporting of devil  with you during the night.
