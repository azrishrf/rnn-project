4|1496|  Chapter: The prayer of travellers and its shortening.  A Hadith like this that Ibn ‘Abbas ordered his Mu’adhdhin (to summon people to prayer  and then make announcement to say prayer in their houses) on Friday which was a  rainy day, has been transmitted by ‘Abdullah b. Harith. Wuhaib, however, says that he  did not hear it from him.
