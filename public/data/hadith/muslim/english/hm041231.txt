4|1231|  Chapter: Desirability of seeking refuge from the torment of the grave.         A Hadith like this has been narrated by Mughira b. Shu’ba with another chain of  transmitters. Abu Bakr and Abu Kuraib narrated in their narration (that Warrad  reported): Mughira gave me dictation of it and I wrote it to Mu’awiya.
