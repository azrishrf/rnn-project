35|6514|  Chapter: The merit of pronouncing Tablil, Tasbib and supplication.  Abu Malik Ashaja’i reported on the authority of his father that whenever a person  embraced Islam, Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) instructed him to recite:  “O Allah, grant me pardon, have mercy upon me, direct me to the path of righteousness  and provide me sustenance.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
