10|3818|  Chapter: Riba (Usury).  Salim b. ‘Abdullah reported on the authority of his father that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) said: He who kept a dog other than one meant for watching the  herd or for hunting would lose every day two qirat of his good deeds. ‘Abdullah and Abu  Huraira also said: Or dog meant for watching the field.
