6|2600|  Chapter: Excellence of observing fast for three days during every month, and fasting on the day  of ‘Arafa, and ‘Ashura and Monday and Thursday.         Mu’adha al-‘Adawiyya reported that she asked ‘Aisha, the wife of the Apostle of Allah  (may peace be upon him), whether the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  observed fasts for three days during every month. She said: Yes I said to her: Which  were (the particular) days of the month on which he observed fast? She said: He was  not particular about the days of the month on which to observe fast.
