7|2675|  Chapter: Entering upon the state of Ihram as the ride proceeds towards (Mecca).  ‘Ubaid b. Juraij reported: I remained in the company of ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar b. al-Khattab  (Allah be pleased with them) its twelve Hajjs and ‘Umras and I said to him: I saw four  characteristics (peculiar in you), and the rest of the Hadith is the same except the case  of Talbiya. There he offered the narration given by al-Maqburi and he stated the facts  excepting the one given above.
