7|3042|  Chapter: It is meritorious for one who does intend to go himself to send the sacrificial  animal to al-Haram.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: We used to garland the goats and send  them (to Mecca), and Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) stayed back in  Medina as a non-Muhrim and nothing was forbidden for him (which is forbidden for a  Muhrim).
