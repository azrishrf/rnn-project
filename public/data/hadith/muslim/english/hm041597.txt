4|1597|  Chapter: The virtue of Sunan prayers before and after the Fard Rak’ahs and their number.  Hafsa reported: Never did I see the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  observing supererogatory prayer sitting till one year before his death when he would  observe Nafl prayer in a sitting position, and he would recite the Surah (of the Qur’an) in  such a slow-measured tone (that duration of its recital) became more lengthy than the  one longer than this.
