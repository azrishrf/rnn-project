2|570|  Chapter: Washing away of the semen from the garment and its scraping.  ‘Amr b. Maimun said: I asked Sulaiman b. Yasir whether the semen that gets on to the  garment of a person should be washed or not. He replied: ‘Aisha told me: The  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) washed the semen, and then went out for  prayer in that very garment and I saw the mark of washing on it.
