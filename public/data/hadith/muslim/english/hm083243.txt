8|3243|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.  Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported: We were on an expedition with Allah’s Messenger (May  peace be upon him) and we had no women with us. We said: Should we not have  ourselves castrated? He (the Holy Prophet) forbade us to do so He then granted us  permission that we should contract temporary marriage for a stipulated period giving her  a garment, and ‘Abdullah then recited this verse: “Those who believe do not make  unlawful the good things which Allah has made lawful for you, and do not transgress.  Allah does not like trangressers” (al-Qur’an, v. 87).
