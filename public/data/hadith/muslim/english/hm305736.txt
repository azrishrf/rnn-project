30|5736|  Chapter: The mercy-and tenderness shown by Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him)  towards children, members of his family. and his humility and his sublime qualities.  Abu Huraira reported that al-Aqra’ b. Habis saw Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him) kissing Hasan. He said: I have ten children, but I have never kissed any one of  them, whereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: He who does not  show mercy (towards his children), no mercy would be shown to him.
