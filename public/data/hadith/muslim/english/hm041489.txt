4|1489|  Chapter: The prayer of travellers and its shortening.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that he summoned (people) to prayer at a place (known as) Dajnan,  and the rest of the Hadith is the same, and then said: Pray in your dwellings, but he did  not repeat for the second time words of Ibn ‘Umar (Pray in your dwellings).
