30|5777|  Chapter: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had a white elegant face.  Jurairi reported: I said to Abu Tufail: Did you see Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him)? He said: Yes, he had a white handsome face. Muslim b. Hajjaj said: Abu  Tufail who died in 100 Hijra was the last of the Companions of Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him).
