7|3001|  Chapter: Regarding one who shaves before offering the sacrifice or offers sacrifice  before throwing (the stones at Jamra).  This Hadith has been narrated on the authority of Zuhri with the same chain of  transmitters (and the words are): I saw Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) on  the back of the camel at Mina, and a person came to him,” and the rest of the Hadith Is  like that transmitted by Ibn ‘Uyaina.
