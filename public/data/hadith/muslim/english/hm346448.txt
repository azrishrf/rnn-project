34|6448|  Chapter: Following the footsteps of the Jews and the Christians.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  You would tread the same path as was trodden by those before you inch by inch and  step by step so much so that if they had entered into the hole of the lizard, you would  follow them in this also. We said: Allah’s Messenger, do you mean Jews and Christians  (by your words) “those before you”? He said: Who else (than those two religious  groups)?
