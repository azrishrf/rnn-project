6|2467|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing the fast or not observing it in the month of Ramadan for a  traveller.  This Hadith is narrated on the authority of Zuhri with the same chain of transmitters.  Yahya (one of the narrators) said that Sufyan (the narrator) had stated: I do not know  whose statement it is: “It is the last word of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) which is accepted as (final as it abrogates the previous ones).”
