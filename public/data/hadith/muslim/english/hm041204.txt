4|1204|  Chapter: Prostration while reciting the Qur’an.  ‘Ali b. ‘Abual-Rahman al-Mu’awi reported: ‘Abdullah b. Umar saw me playing with  pebbles during prayer. After finishing the prayer he forbade me (to do it) and said: Do as  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) used to do. I said: How did Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) do? He said that he (the Messenger of Allah) sat  at tashahhud, placed his right palm on the right thigh and closed all his fingers and  pointed with the help of finger next to the thumb, and placed his left palm on his right  thigh.
