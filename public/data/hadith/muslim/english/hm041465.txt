4|1465|  Chapter: Compensation of the missed prayer and excellence of observing it promptly.  Musa b. Salama Hudhali said: I asked Ibn ‘Abbas: How should I say prayer when I am  in Mecca, and when I do not pray along with the Imam? He said: Two rak’ahs (of  prayer) is the Sunnah of Abu’l-Qasim (may peace be upon him).
