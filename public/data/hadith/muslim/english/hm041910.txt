4|1910|  Chapter: Hadith pertaining to giving instruction in Kbutba.  Ibn Abbas reported: The Apostle of Allah (way peace be upon him) used to recite in the  morning prayer on Friday Surah “Alif-Lam-Mim, Tanz’il ul-Sajda” (Surah xxxii.): Surely  there came over the man a time” (Surah lxxvii) and he used to recite in Jumu’a prayer  Surahs Jumu’a and al-Munafiqin.
