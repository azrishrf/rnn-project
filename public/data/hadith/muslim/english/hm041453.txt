4|1453|  Chapter: The excellence of Qunut in all the prayers when any calamity befalls the Muslims.  Abu Qatada reported that when the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) was  in a journey he got down for rest at night, and he used to lie down on his right side, and  when he lay down for rest before the dawn, he used to stretch his forearm and place his  head over his palm.
