4|1745|  Chapter: Excellence of the Hifz; (one who commits the Qur’an to memory) of the  Qur’an.  ‘Aisha reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) (as saying): One who is  proficient in the Qur’an is associated with the noble, upright, recording angels; and he  who falters in it, and finds it difficult for him, will have a double reward.
