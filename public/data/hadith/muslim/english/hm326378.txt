32|6378|  Chapter: A person would be along with him whom he loves.  Anas b. Malik reported that a desert Arab said to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him): When would be the Last Hour? Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  said: What preparation have you made for that? Thereupon he said: The love of Allah  and of His Messenger (that is my preparation for the Last Hour) (for the Day of  Resurrection). Thereupon he (the Holy Prophet) said: You would be along with one  whom you love.  OF RELATIONSHIP
