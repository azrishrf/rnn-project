6|2629|  Chapter: Excellence of Lailat-ul Qadar and exhortation to find it.  This Hadith has been reported on the authority of Yahya b. Abu Kathir with the same  chain of transmitters (with a slight variation of these words): I saw the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) after he had completed (the prayer) and there was a  trace of clay on his forehead and tip (of the nose).
