18|4281|  Chapter: Stray thing found by anyone.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Salama b. Kuhail through different  chains of transmitters. In their ahadith, it is three years, except in the hadith of Hammid  b. Salama it is two years or three years. In the hadith transmitted on the authority of  Sufyan and Zaid b. Abu Unaisa and Hammid b. Salama (the words are): “If someone  comes and informs you about the number (of articles) of the bag and the straps, then  give that to him.” Sufyan has made this addition in the narration of Waki’: “Otherwise it  is like your property.” And in the narration of Ibn Numair the words are: “Otherwise  make use of that.”
