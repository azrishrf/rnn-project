40|6828|  Chapter: The haughty and the proud would Set into the fire of hell and the humble and meek  would get into Paradise.  Abu Sa’id reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: When the  inmates of Paradise would be admitted to Paradise and the inmates of Hell would be  admitted to Hell, it would be said (to the inmates of Paradise): O inmates of Paradise.  The rest of the hadith is the same but with this variation (that he only) said. That is the  word of Allah, the Exalted. And he did not say: Then Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) recited, and he did not make a mention of his having pointed with his hand  towards the (material) world.               INTIMATES
