35|6587|  Chapter: The merit of Subhan Allah Wa Bi Hamdihi.  Abu Dharr reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: Should I not  inform you about the words liked most by Allah? I said: Allah’s Messenger, do inform  me about the words liked most by Allah. He said: Verily, the words liked most by Allah  are: “hallowed be Allah and praise is due to Him.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
