4|1577|  Chapter: Excellence of the forenoon prayer, two are its minimum Rak’ahs and eight are its  maximum Rak’ahs and average Rak’ahs are four or six, and exhortation for the observance of  the prayer.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) used to  recite in the two (supererogatory) rak’ahs of the dawn prayer: “Say: We believed in Allah  and what was revealed to us” and that which is found in Surah Al-i-‘lmran: “Come to that  word (creed) which is common between you and us”(iii. 64).
