6|2399|  Chapter: The timing of fast begins with the dawn.  ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud (Allah be pleased with him) reported that the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) had said: Bilal would pronounce Adhan (at the fag end of the  night in order to inform the people about the time of the Sahri). So you eat and drink till  you hear the Adhan of Ibn Umm Maktum (which was pronounced at the conclusion of  the Sahri and the commencement of the fast).
