3|586|  Chapter: The menstruating woman is permitted to wash the head of her husband, comb  his hair, and her left-over is clean, and one is permitted to recline in her lap and recite  the Qur’an.  Al-Aswad narrated it from ‘Aisha that she observed: I used to wash the head of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), while I was in a state of menstruation.
