20|4709|  Chapter: About the martyrs.  It has been narrated on the authority of Hafsa daughter of Sirin who said: Anas b. Malik  asked me the cause of death of Yahya b. ‘Abu ‘Amra. I said: (He died) of plague. He  said: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said that death by plague is  martyrdom for a Muslim.
