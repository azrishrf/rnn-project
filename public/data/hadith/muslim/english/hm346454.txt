34|6454|  Chapter: Knowledge would be taken away and ignorance would prevail upon people and the  turmoil at the end of the world.  Abu Wa’il reported: I was sitting with ‘Abdullah and Abu Musa that they reported Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) having said: Prior to the Last Hour, there would  be a time when knowledge would be taken away, and ignorance would take its place  and there would be bloodshed on a large scale.
