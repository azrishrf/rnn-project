23|4938|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that a person came to him and said: Did Allah’s Apostle (may peace  be upon him) forbid the preparation of Nabidh in a green pitcher (besmeared with pitch  and) in varnished jar? He said: Yes.
