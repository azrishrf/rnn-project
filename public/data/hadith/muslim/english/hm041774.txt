4|1774|  Chapter: Excellence of the recitation of “Say: He is Allah, the One”.  ‘Uqba b. ‘Amir reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: What  wonderful verses have been sent down today, the like of which has never been seen !  They are: “Say: I seek refuge with the Lord of the dawn,” and “Say: I seek refuge with  the Lord of men.”
