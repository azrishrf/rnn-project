7|2890|  Chapter: Excellence of spending the night at Dhi-Tuwa for entering Mecca (for Hajj)  and getting into it after a bath and entering during the day.  Nafi’ reported that Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) did not enter Mecca without  spending the night at Dhi Tawu until it was dawn, when he took a bath, and then  entered Mecca in the morning, and made a mention that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) did that.
