5|2152|  Chapter: Taking out of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr before observing Fitr prayer.  Abdullah b. Umar reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  ordered the (payment of) Zakat-ul-Fitr one sa’ of dates, or one sa’ of barley. Ibn ‘Umar  (‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar) further said: The people equalised it (then) with two mudds of fine  wheat.
