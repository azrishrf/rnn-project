43|7186|  Chapter: Pertaining to the prohibition of wine.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that Umar delivered a sermon on the pulpit of Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) and he praised Allah and lauded Him and then said: Now  coming to the point. Behold I when the command pertaining to the prohibition of wine  was revealed, it was prepared from five things: from wheat, barley, date, grape, honey;  and wine is that which clouds the intellect; and O people, I wish Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) could have explained to us in (more) detail the laws pertaining to  the inheritance of the grandfather, about one who dies leaving no issue, and some of  the problems pertaining to interest.
