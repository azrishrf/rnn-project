2|555|  Chapter: It is forbidden to urinate in stagnant water.  Hammam b. Munabbih said: Of the ahadith narrated to us by Abfi Huraira from  Muhammad the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) one is this: The  Messenger or Allah (may peace be upon him) said: You should not urinate in standing  water, that is not flowing, then wash in it.
