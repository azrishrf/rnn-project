7|3061|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.  This Hadith is narrated (from ‘Aisha) on the authority of Ibn Shihab with the same chain  of transmitters (and the words are): Safiyyah bint Huyayy, the wife of Allah’s Apostle  (may peace be upon him), entered the period of menses at the occasion of the Farewell  Pilgrimage after she had performed Tawaf Ifada in the state of cleanliness; the rest of  the Hadith is the same.
