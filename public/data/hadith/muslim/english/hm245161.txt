24|5161|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.  ‘Ali reported that Ukaidir of Duma presented to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him) a silk garment, and he presented it to ‘Ali, and said: Tear it to make head covering  for Fitimas out of it. This tradition is transmitted on the authority of Abu Bakr, and Abu  Kuraib said: Among the women.
