31|6146|  Chapter: Fraternity established by Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) amongst the  companions (Allah be pleased with them).  Jubair b. Mut’im reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: There  is no alliance (hilf) in Islam but (the hilf) established in the pre-Islamic days (for good).  Islam intensifies and strengthens it.           HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
