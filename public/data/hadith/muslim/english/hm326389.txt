32|6389|  Chapter: When a pious person is praised that is glad tidings for him.  This hadith has been narrated through another chain of transmitters also and the one  transmitted on the authority of Shu’ba (the words are): “People love him.” In the hadith  transmitted on the authority of ‘Abd-us-Samad (the words are): “People praise him as  stated by Hammad.”
