1|394|  Chapter: Pertaining to the words of the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him): “I  would be the first among people to Intercede in the Paradise and among the Apostles I  would have the largest following”.  Mis’ar transmitted it with the same chain of narrators from Qatada except that in the  hadith narrated by Waki’ (the Prophet) said: “He was endowed,” and in the hadith  reported by Abu Usama (the words are): “It is reported from the Apostle of Allah (may  peace be upon him).”
