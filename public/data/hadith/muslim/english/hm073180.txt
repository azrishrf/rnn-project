7|3180|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: He who patiently endures the hardships of it (of this city of  Medina), I would be an intercessor or a witness on his behalf on the Day of  Resurrection.
