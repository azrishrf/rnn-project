20|4498|  Chapter: The merits of a just ruler and the demerits of a tyrant ruler.  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of Ibn ‘Umar, but there is (a slight  change of wording) in the hadith transmitted through Zuhri that he said: “I think that he  (the narrator) said: The man is a custodian of the wealth of his father, and he would be  answerable for what is in his custody.”
