30|5821|  Chapter: Respect of Allah’s Messenger and abandoning of too many questions especially those  for which there is no genuine need.         Amir b. Sa’d reported on the authority of his father that Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) said: The greatest sinner amongst the Muslims is one who asked about a  thing (from Allah’s Apostle) which had not been forbidden for the Muslims and it was  forbidden for them because of his persistently asking about it.
