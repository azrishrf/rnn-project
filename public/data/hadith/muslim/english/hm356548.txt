35|6548|  Chapter: Supplication at the time of sleeping and going to bed.  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of al-Bara’ b. ‘Azib that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) commanded a person (in these words) and there  is no mention of this: “if you get up in the morning you would get up with a bliss.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
