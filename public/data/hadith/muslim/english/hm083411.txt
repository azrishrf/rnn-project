8|3411|  Chapter: Kitab Al-Rada’a-Book pertaining to fosterage.  Umm Salama (Allah be pleased with her), the wife of Allah’s Apostle (May peace be  upon him), said: It was said to the Messenger of Allah (May peace be upon him): Is not  the daughter of Hamza a suitable match for you? Or it was said: Why don’t you propose  to marry the daughter of Hamza, the son of Abd al-Muttalib? Thereupon he said: Hamza  is my brother by reason of fosterage.
