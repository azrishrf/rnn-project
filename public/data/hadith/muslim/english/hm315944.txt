31|5944|  Chapter: The merits of Zubair and Talha (Allah be pleased with both of them).  Hisham reported on the authority of his father (‘Urwa b. Zubair) that ‘Aisha said: BY  Allah, both fathers of yours are amongst those who have been mentioned in this verse:  “Those who responded to the call of Allah and the Messenger after the misfortune had  fallen upon them”.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
