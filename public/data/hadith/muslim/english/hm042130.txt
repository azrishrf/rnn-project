4|2130|  Chapter: The Apostle of Allah (way peace be upon him) seeking permission from the  Lord. the Exalted and High, for visiting the grave of his mother.  Abu Huraira reported: The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) visited the grave  of his mother and he wept, and moved others around him to tears, and said: I sought  permission from my Lord to beg forgiveness for her but it was not granted to me, and I         sought permission to visit her grave and it was granted to motel So visit the graves, for  that makes you mindful of death.
