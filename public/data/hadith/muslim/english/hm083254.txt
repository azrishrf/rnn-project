8|3254|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.  Rabi’ b. S’abra al-jahanni reported on the authority of his father. We went with Allah’s  Messenger (May peace be upon him) to Mecca during the year of Victory and he  narrated like this a hadith transmitted by Bishr (the previous one) but with this addition:  “She said: Can it be possible?” And it is also mentioned in it: “He said: The cloak of this  (man) is old and worn out.”
