7|3162|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Anas b. Malik (Allah he pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) said: O Allah, increase in Medina twice the blessings (Thou showered) on  Mecca.
