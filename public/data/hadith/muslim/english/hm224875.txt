22|4875|  Chapter: It is not permissible for one who intends to sacrifice the animal to get one’s  hair or nails cut after the beginning of Dhull-Hijja.         Amr b. Muslim al-Jundani reported that Ibn Musayyib had told him that it was Umm  Salama, the wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him), who had informed him of  that as narrated above.
