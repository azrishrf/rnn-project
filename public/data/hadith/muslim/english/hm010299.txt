1|299|  Chapter: Description of the period in which Iman would not be acceptable to Allah.  Abu Dharr reported: I entered the mosque and the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) was sitting there. When the sun disappeared (from the sight) he said: O Abu  Dharr! Do you know where it goes? He (the narrator) said: Allah and His Apostle know  best. He (the Holy Prophet) said: Verily it goes and begs permission, for prostration (to  Allah) and the permission is granted to it.
