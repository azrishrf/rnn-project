3|643|  Chapter: Law of Shariâ€™ah pertaining to the plaited hair of the woman who takes a bath.  Umm Salama reported: I said: Messenger of Allah, I am a woman who has closely  plaited hair on my head; should I undo it for taking a bath, because of sexual  intercourse? He (the Holy Prophet) said: No, it is enough for you to throw three handfuls  of water on your head and then pour water over yourself, and you shall be purified.
