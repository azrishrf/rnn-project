24|5162|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.         ‘Ali b. Abu Talib reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) gave me to  wear a garment in the form of silk cloak. I went out wearing it, but saw signs of anger on  his face, so I tore it and distributed it amongst my women.
