3|653|  Chapter: The woman who has a prolonged flow of blood, her bathing and prayer.  The Hadith narrated by Waki’ and with its chain of narrators has been transmitted on the  authority of Hisham b. ‘Urwa, but in the Hadith narrated by Qutaiba on the authority of  Jarir, the words are: “There came Fatimah b. Abu Hubaish, b. ‘Abd al-Muttalib b. Asad,  and she was a woman amongst us,” and in the Hadith of Hammid b. Zaid there is an  addition of these words: “We abandoned mentioning him.”
