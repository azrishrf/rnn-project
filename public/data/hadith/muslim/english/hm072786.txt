7|2786|  Chapter: Types of Ihram- It is permissible to enter into the state of Ihram singularly for  the sake of Hajj or for tamattu’ bi’l-‘umra ila’l-Hajj or for both hajj and ‘umra together.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that the Apostle of Allah (may peace be  upon him) came out (for Pilgrimage) on The 4th or 5th of Dhu’l Hjjja. The rest of the  Hadith is the same, but he (the narrator) made no mention of the doubt of Hakam about  his (the Prophet’s) words: “They were reluctant.”
