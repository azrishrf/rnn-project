32|6287|  Chapter: He upon whom Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked curse  whereas he in fact did not deserve it, it would be a source of reward and mercy for him.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: O Allah, I  am a human being and for any person amongst Muslims upon whom I hurl malediction  or invoke curse or give him whipping make it a source of purity and mercy.
