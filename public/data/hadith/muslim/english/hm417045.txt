41|7045|  Chapter: Approach of the Last Hour.  Anas b. Malik reported that Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon him) said: I and the  Last Hour have been sent like this. Shu’ba said: I heard Qatada as saying in his  narration: The excellence of one over the other. And I do not know whether he narrated  it from Anas or Qatada himself said so.
