Barb reported: I heard Nu’man deliver an address in which he said that  (Hadrat) Umar made a mention of what had fallen to the lot of people out of the material  world and he said: I saw Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) spend the whole  day being upset because of hunger and he could not get even an interior quality of  dates with which he could fill his belly.
