4|1236|  Chapter: Desirability of seeking refuge from the torment of the grave.  Abu Zubair reported: Abdullah b Zubair used to say La ilaha il-Allah at the end of every  prayer like the Hadith narrated by Ibn Numair and he reported it in the end, and then  reported Ibn Zubair saying: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) uttered  La ilaha il-Allah at the end of every prayer.
