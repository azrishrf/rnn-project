4|1886|  Chapter: Warning on neglecting Jumu’a.  Ja’far b. Muhammad said on the authority of his father: I heard Jabir b. ‘Abdullah saying  that in the sermon of the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) he praised Allah,  lauded Him (and subsequently said [other words] and raised his voice, and the rest of  the Hadith is the same).
