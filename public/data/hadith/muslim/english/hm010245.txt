1|245|  Chapter: Pertaining to evil suggestion or prompting in faith and what should be said  when it occurs to the mind of a man.  This hadith is transmitted by Urwa b. Zubair on the authority of Abu Huraira (and the  words are):The Satan comes to the bondsman (of Allah) and says: Who created this  and that? The remaining part of the hadith is the same.
