5|2244|  Chapter: Exhortation to give charity even though it is small; (the one who gives) small amount  should neither be discouraged not looked down upon.  Asma’ reported Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon him) as saying (to her): Spend  and do not calculate, (for) Allah would calculate in your case; and do not hoard,  otherwise Allah would be withholding from you.
