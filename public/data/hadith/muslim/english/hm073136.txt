7|3136|  Chapter: The Muhajir is permitted to stay for three days in Mecca after Hajj and Umra  and not more than this.  Al-‘Ala’ b. al-Hadrami reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  It is only for three nights that a Muhajir should stay at Mecca after the completion of the  rituals of Hajj.
