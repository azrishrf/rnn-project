4|1082|  Chapter: Change of Qibla from Bait-u’l-Manqdis to Ka’ba.  ‘Aisha and Abdullah reported: As the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) was  about to breathe his last, he drew his sheet upon his face and when he felt uneasy, he  uncovered his face and said in that very state: Let there be curse upon the Jews and  the Christians that they have taken the graves of their apostles as places of worship. He  in fact warned (his men) against what they (the Jews and the Christians) did.
