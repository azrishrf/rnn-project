10|3906|  Chapter: How much land is to be spared when there is disagreement over the breadth of path.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported that when Allah’s Prophet (may peace  be upon him) came to Medina, they were paying one and two years in advance for  fruits, so he said: Those who pay in advance for anything must do so for a specified  weight and for a definite time.
