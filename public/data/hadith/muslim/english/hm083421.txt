8|3421|  Chapter: One suckling or two do not make marriage unlawful.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with, her) reported that it had been revealed in the Holy Qur’an  that ten clear sucklings make the marriage unlawful, then it was abrogated (and  substituted) by five sucklings and Allah’s Apostle (May peace be upon him) died and it  was before that time (found) in the Holy Qur’an (and recited by the Muslims).
