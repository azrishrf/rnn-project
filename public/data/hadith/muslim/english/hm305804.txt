30|5804|  Chapter: How long did the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) stay in Medina and  Mecca.  Jabir reported that he heard Mu’awiya say in his address that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) died at the age of sixty-three, so was the case with Abu Bakr and  ‘Umar, and I (am now) sixty-three.
