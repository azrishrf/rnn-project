2|568|  Chapter: Washing away of the semen from the garment and its scraping.         Qutaiba b. Sa’id, Ishaq b. Ibrahim, Ibn Abi ‘Aruba, Abu Ma’shar, Abu Bakr b. Abu  Shaiba, Mansur and Mughira have all transmitted from Ibrahim, who transmitted it on  the authority of ‘Aisha’s narration pertaining to the scraping off of the (drop) of semen  from the garment of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) like the hadith of  Khalid on the authority of Abu Ma’shar.
