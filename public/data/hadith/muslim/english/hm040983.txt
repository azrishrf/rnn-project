4|983|  Chapter: Following the Imam and acting after him.  ‘Aisha reported: Never did I ,see the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) after the  revelation (of these verses): “When Allah’s help and victory came.” observing his prayer  without making (this supplication) or he said in it (supplication): Hallowed be Thee, my  Lord, and with Thy praise, O Allah, forgive me.
