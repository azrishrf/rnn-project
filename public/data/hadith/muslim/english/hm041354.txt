4|1354|  Chapter: Desirability of observing the morning prayer at earlier hour and that is the time when  there is darkness before dawn and the exposition about the length of recitation in it.  Abu Dharr reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said to me: O  Abu Dharr, you would soon find after me rulers who would make their prayers dead.         You should say prayer at its prescribed time. If you say prayer at its prescribed time that  would be a supererogatory prayer for you, otherwise you saved your prayer.
