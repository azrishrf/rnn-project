4|1890|  Chapter: Warning on neglecting Jumu’a.  ‘Adi b. Hatim reported that a person recited a sermon before the Apostle of Allah (may  peace be upon him) thus: He who obeys Allay and His Apostle, he in fact follows the  right path, and he who disobeys both of them, he goes astray. Upon this the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: What a bad speaker you are; say: He who  disobeys Allah and His Apostle. Ibn Numair added: He in fact went astray.
