4|2024|  Chapter: The dead body is punished for the lamentation of his family.  ‘Amr reported on the authority of Ibn Abu Mulaika: We were with the bier of Umm Aban,  daughter of ‘Uthman, and the rest of the Hadith is the same, but he did not narrate it as  a marfu’ Hadith on the authority of ‘Umar from the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon  him) as it was narrated by Ayyub and Ibn Juraij, and the Hadith narrated by them  (Ayyub and Ibn Juraij) is more complete than that of ‘Amr.
