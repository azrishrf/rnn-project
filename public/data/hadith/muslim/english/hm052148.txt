5|2148|  Chapter: Zakat (Sadaqa) Fitr is binding on every Muslim.  Abu Huraira reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)sent Umar  for (collecting) Sadaqa (zakat), and it was said that Ibn Jamil, Khalid b. Walid and  ‘Abbas the uncle of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), refused (to pay  it). Upon this the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Ibn Jamil is taking  revenge but for this that he was destitute and Allah made him rich. As regards Khalid,  you are unjust to Khalid, for be reserved his armours and weapons for the sake of Allah,         and as for ‘Abbas, I shall be responsible for it and an equal amount along with it. And he  again said: ‘Umar, bear this in mind, the uncle of a person is like his father.
