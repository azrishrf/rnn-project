23|4924|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that there came to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) a  group of people from the tribe of ‘Abd al-Qais. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) said to them: I forbid you to prepare Nabidh in gourd, in pitcher besmeared with  pitch, in hollow stump and in water-skin (meant for preserving wine). In the hadith  transmitted on the authority of Hammad the word. “gourd” has been used in place of  “water-skin”.
