18|4254|  Chapter: Judgment is to be pronounced on evidence, and one who is eloquent in his plea (may  win the case).  ‘Aisha reported that Hind, daughter of Utba h. Rabi’, came to Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) and said: Allah’s Messenger, by Allah, there was no household  upon the surface of the earth than your household about which I cherished that it should  be disgraced. But today there is no household on the surface of the earth than your  household about which I cherish that it be honoured Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) said. It will increase, by Him in Whose Hand is my life. She then  said: Messenger of Allah, Abu Sufyan is a niggardly person; is there any harm for me if I  spend out of that which belongs to him on our children? He said to her: No, but only that  what is reasonable.
