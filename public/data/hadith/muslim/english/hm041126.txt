4|1126|  Chapter: It is forbidden to remove pebbles and smooth the ground while engaged in  prayer.         Abu Dharr reported: The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: The deeds of  my people, good and bad, were presented before me, and I found the removal of  something objectionable from the road among their good deeds, and the sputum mucus  left unburied in the mosque among their evil deeds.
