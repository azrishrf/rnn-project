16|4145|  Chapter: When anyone attacks the life of a person, or his limb and the victim wards off the  attack and in self-defence either the life of the assailant is lost or his limb broken, there would be  no penalty on the victim.  ‘Imran b. Husain reported that a person bit the arm of another person; he pulled it out  and his fore-tooth fell down. This matter was taken to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him), and he turned it down saying: Did you want to eat his flesh?  MURDERS, FIGHTING, REQUITAL
