32|6294|  Chapter: He upon whom Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked curse  whereas he in fact did not deserve it, it would be a source of reward and mercy for him.  Abu Huraira reported that he heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as  saying: O Allah, for any believing servant whom I curse make that as a source of  nearness to Thee on the Day of Resurrection.
