26|5381|  Chapter: Prohibition of saying first as-Salam-u-Alaikum to the people of the book, and how their  salutations should be responded.  Anas reported that the Companions of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said to  him: The people of the Book offer us salutations (by saying as-Salamu-‘Alaikum). How  should we reciprocate them? Thereupon he said: Say: Wa ‘Alaikum (and upon you too).
