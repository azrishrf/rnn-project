7|2956|  Chapter: Excellence of observing the dawn prayer at the earliest part of the dawn on  the 10th of Dhu’l-Hijja at Muzdalifa.  A’bdullah (b. ‘Umar) reported: I have never seen Allah’s Messenger, (may peace be  upon him) but observing the prayers at their appointed times except two players, sunset  and ‘Isha, at Muzdalifa (where he deferred the sunset prayer to combine it with ‘Isha  and he observed the dawn prayer before its stipulated time on that day (10th of Dhu’l- Hijja).
