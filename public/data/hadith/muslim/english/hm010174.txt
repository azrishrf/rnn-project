1|174|  Chapter: Prohibition of killing of an infidel after he say’s: “There is no god but Allah”.  The same hadith has been transmitted by the same chain of narrators. The hadith  transmitted by Auza’i and Ibn Juraij contains these words: I embraced Islam for Allah’s  sake, and in the hadith narrated by Ma’mar the words are: I knelt down to kill him, that  he said; There is no god but Allah.
