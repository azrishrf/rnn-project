8|3400|  Chapter: Kitab Al-Rada’a-Book pertaining to fosterage.  This ha0dith has been narrated on the authority of Zuhri with the same chain of  transmitters (that ‘Aisha said): There came Aflah, the brother of Abu’l Qulais (Allah be  pleased with him), and sought permission from her, the rest of the hadith is the same  (except for the words that the Holy Prophet) said: “He is your uncle. Let your hand be  besmeared with dust. Abu’l Qulais was the husband of the woman who had suckled  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her).
