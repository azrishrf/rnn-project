1|417|  Chapter: Friendship with believers and dissociation with non-believers and severance  from the.         ‘Amr b. ‘As reported: I heard it from the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  quite audibly and not secretly: Behold! the posterity of my fathers, that is, so and so, are  not my friends. Verily Allah and the pious believers are my friends.
