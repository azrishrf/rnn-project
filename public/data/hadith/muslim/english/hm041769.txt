4|1769|  Chapter: Excellence of surat al-Kahf and Ayat al-Kursi.  Abu Darda’ reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: Is any one of  you incapable of reciting a third of the Qur’an in a night? They (the Companions) asked:  How could one recite a third of the Qur’an (in a night)? Upon this he (the Holy Prophet)  said: “He is Allah, One” (Qur’an. cxii.) is equivalent to a third of the Qur’an.
