27|5601|  Chapter: The use of Musk and that is the best scent, and the disapproval of rejecting the gift of  scent and flower.  Nafi’ reported that when Ibn Umar wanted fumigation he got it from aloeswood without  mixing anything with it, or he put camphor along with aloeswood and then said: This is  how Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) fumigated.
