1|347|  Chapter: In proof of the fact that the believers would see their Lord hallowed be He and  exalted) on the Last Day.  Suhaib reported the Apostle (may peace be upon him) saying: When those deserving of  Paradise would enter Paradise, the Blessed and the Exalted would ask: Do you wish  Me to give you anything more? They would say: Hast Thou not brightened our faces?  Hast Thou not made us enter Paradise and saved us from Fire? He (the narrator) said:  He (God) would lift the veil, and of things given to them nothing would he dearer to them  than the sight of their Lord, the Mighty and the Glorious.
