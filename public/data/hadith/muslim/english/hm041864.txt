4|1864|  Chapter: Excellence of Friday.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: When it is  Friday, the angels stand at every door of the mosque and record the people in the order  of their arrival, and when the Imam sits (on the pulpit for delivering the sermon) they fold  up their sheets (manuscripts of the Qur’an) and listen to the mention (of Allah). And he  who comes early is like one who offers a she-camel as a sacrifice, the next like one who  offers a cow, the next a ram, the next a hen, the next an egg.
