7|2917|  Chapter: It is permissible to observe Tawaf on the back of a camel or any other riding  beast, and to touch the black stone with a stick.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) circumambulated the House on the occasion of the Farewell Pilgrimage on  the back of his camel and touched the Corner (of Black Stone) with a stick.
