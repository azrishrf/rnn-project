24|5244|  Chapter: Prohibition of using clothes dyed in saffron for men.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah reported that Abu Qubafa was led (to the audience of the Holy  Prophet) on the day of the Conquest of Mecca and his head and beard were white like  hyssop, whereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: Change it with  something but avoid black.
