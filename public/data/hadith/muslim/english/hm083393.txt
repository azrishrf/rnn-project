8|3393|  Chapter: Permissibility of intercourse with a suckling lady and disapproval of ‘Azl.  Judama bint Wahb al-Asadiyya (Allah be pleased with her) reported: I heard Allah’s  Messenger (May peace be upon him) saying this. The rest of the hadith is the same  concerning ‘azl and ghila (cohabitating with a suckling woman), but with a slight  variation of words.
