6|2552|  Chapter: Permissibility of completing the fasts of Ramadhan before the commencement of the  coming Ramadhan.  ‘Aisha reported: If one amongst us had to break fasts (of Ramadan due to natural  reasons, i.e. menses) during the life of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) she could not find it possible to complete them so long she had been in the  presence of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) till Sha’ban commenced.
