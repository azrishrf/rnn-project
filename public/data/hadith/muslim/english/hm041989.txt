4|1989|  Chapter: Mention of the torment of grave in eclipse prayer.  This Hadith has been narrated on the authority of Isma’il with the same chain of  transmitters and in the Hadith narrated by Sufyan and Waki’ (the words are): “The sun  eclipsed on the day when Ibrahim died, and the people said: It has eclipsed on the  death of Ibrahim.”
