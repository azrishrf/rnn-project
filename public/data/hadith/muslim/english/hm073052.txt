7|3052|  Chapter: It is permissible to ride the sacrificial animal (camel) for one who is in need of  it.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported that he was asked about riding  on a sacrificial animal, and he said: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) as saying: Ride on it gently, when you have need for it, until you find (another)  mount.
