7|3010|  Chapter: Excellence of making a halt at al Muha’ab, on the day of Nahr, and observing  prayer there.  Salim reported that Abu Bakr, ‘Umar and Ibn Umar used to halt at Abtah. ‘Urwa narrated  from ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) that he did not observe this practice and said:  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) halted there, for it is a place from where it  was easy to depart.
