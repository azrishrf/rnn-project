4|1255|  Chapter: Desirability of going to prayer with dignity and tranquillity and forbiddance of going to it  in hot haste.  Abu Qatada reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: When  the Iqama is pronounced do not get up till you see me Ibn Hatim was in doubt whether it  was said: “When the Iqama is pronounced” or “When call is made”.
