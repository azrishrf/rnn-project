20|4584|  Chapter: It is good on the part of a leader to take the oath of allegiance from his forces  when intending to fight, and an account of the allegiance of God’s pleasure under the  tree.  It has been narrated on the authority of ‘Abdullah b. Abu Aufa who said: The  Companions of the Tree (i.e. those who swore fealty under the tree) were one thousand  and three hundred, and the people of Aslam tribe were one-eighth of the Muhajirs.
