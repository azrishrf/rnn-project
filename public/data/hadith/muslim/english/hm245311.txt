24|5311|  Chapter: Prohibition of wearing the garment of falsehood.  ‘Aisha reported that a woman said: Allah’s Messenger, may I say to my (co-wife) that  my husband has given me (such and such) a thing but which he has not in fact gives  me? ‘Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: The one who  makes a false statement of that which one has not been given is like one who wears a  garment of falsehood.
