24|5243|  Chapter: Prohibition of using clothes dyed in saffron for men.         Jabir reported that when Abu Qubafa (father of Abu Bakr) came in the Victory or on the  Day of Victory (to the Holy Prophet to pledge his allegiance to him) his head and his  beard were white like hyssop. He (the Holy Prophet) commanded or the women were  commanded by him that they should change this with something (that the colour of his  hair should be changed).
