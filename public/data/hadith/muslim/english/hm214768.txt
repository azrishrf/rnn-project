Abu Aufa about (the lawfulness or unlawfulness  of) the flesh of the domestic asses. He said: We experienced hunger on the Day of  Khaibar as we were with the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him). We found  domestic asses in the exterior of Medina. We slaughtered them and our earthen pots  were boiling when the announcer of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  made an announcement that the earthen pots should be turned upside down and  nothing of the flesh of the domestic asses should be eaten. I said: What kind of  prohibition is it that he (the Holy Prophet) has made? He said: We discussed it amongst  -ourselves. Some of us aaid that it has been declared unlawful for ever, (whereas others  said) it has been declared unlawful since one-fifth (of the booty) has not been given (to  the treasury, as is legally required).
