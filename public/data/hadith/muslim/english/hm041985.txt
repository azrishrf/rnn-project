4|1985|  Chapter: Mention of the torment of grave in eclipse prayer.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed prayer  while it was (solar) eclipse. He recited (the Qur’an in qiyam) and then bowed. He again  recited and again bowed. He again recited and again bowed and again recited and  again bowed, and then prostrated; and the second (rak’ah) was like this.
