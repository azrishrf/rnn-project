7|3135|  Chapter: The Muhajir is permitted to stay for three days in Mecca after Hajj and Umra  and not more than this.  Al-‘Ali,’ b. al-Hadrami reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  The Muhijir should stay at Mecca after performing the rituals (of Hajj) but for three  (days) only.
