24|5156|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.  Suwaid b. Ghafala said: ‘Umar addressed us at a place known as Jabiya (Syria) and he  said: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) forbade us the wearing of silk but to the  extent of two or three fingers or four fingers.
