7|3065|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) inclined to do with Safiyyah what a man feels inclined to do with his wife. They  said: Messenger of Allah, she has entered the state of menses, whereupon he said:  (Well) she is going to detain us. They (his wives) said: Messenger of Allah, she  performed Tawaf Ziyara (Tawaf Ifada) on the Day of Nahr. Thereupon he said: Then  she should proceed along with you
