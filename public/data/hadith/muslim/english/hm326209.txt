32|6209|  Chapter: Forbiddance of nursing mutual jealousy mutual hatred and mutual hostility.  Anas reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: Nurse no grudge,  nurse no aversion and do not sever ties of kinship and live like fellow-brothers as  servants of Allah. This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Shu’ba with the  same chain of transmitters but with this addition: “As Allah has commanded you.”  OF RELATIONSHIP
