24|5150|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.  Khalifa b. Ka’b AbCi Dhubyan reported: I heard ‘Abdullah b. Zubair addressing the  people and saying: Behold! do not dress your women with silk clothes for I heard ‘Umar  b. Khattab as saying that he had heard Allah’s messenger(may peace be upon him) as  saying: Do not wear silk, for one who wear it in this world will not wear it in the  Hereafter.
