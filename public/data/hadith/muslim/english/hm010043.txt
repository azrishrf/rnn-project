1|43|  Chapter: He who meets his Lord with implicit faith would enter Heaven and fire would  be forbidden to harm him.  It is narrated on the authority of Ubadah b. Samit that the messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) observed: He who said: “There is no god but Allah, He is One and  there is no associate with Him, that Muhammad is his servant and His messenger, that  Christ is servant and the son of His slave-girl and he (Christ) His word which He  communicated to Mary and is His Spirit, that Paradise is a fact and Hell is a fact,” Allah  would make him (he who affirms these truths enter Paradise through any one of its eight  doors which he would like.
