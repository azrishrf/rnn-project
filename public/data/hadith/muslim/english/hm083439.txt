8|3439|  Chapter: Tracing of relationship from physical features.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon  him) visited me looking pleased as if his face was glistening and said: Did you see that  Mujazziz cast a glance at Zaid b. Haritha and Usama b. Zaid, and (then) said: Some (of  the features) of their feet are found in the others?
