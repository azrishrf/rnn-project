1|124|  Chapter: Don’t become unbelievers after me by striking the necks of one another.  It is narrated on the authority of Jarir b. ‘Abdullah that the Apostle of Allah (may peace  and blessings be upon him) asked him on the occasion of the Farewell Pilgrimage to  make the people silent and then said: Do not return to unbelief after me by striking the  necks of one another.
