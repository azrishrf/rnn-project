1|114|  Chapter: Concerning the peculiarities of a hypocrite.  ‘Uqba b. Mukarram al-‘Ami reported that he heard ‘Ala’ b. ‘Abdur-Rahman narrating this  hadith with this chain of transmitters and he said: Three are the signs of a hypocrite,  even if he observed fast and prayed and asserted that he was a Muslim.
