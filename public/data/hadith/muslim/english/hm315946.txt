31|5946|  Chapter: The merits of Zubair and Talha (Allah be pleased with both of them).  ‘Urwa reported: ‘Aisha said to me: Your fathers (Zubair and Abu Bakr) were amongst  those about whom (it has been revealed): “Those who responded to the call of Allah  and His Messenger after the misfortune had fallen upon them.”  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
