Kathir narrated as Na5r reported that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) entered into the state of Ihram for Hajj. And in the narration of Abu  Shihab (the words are): We went out with the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) pronouncing Talbiya for Hajj, And in an the ahadith (narrated in this connection the  words are): He led the morning prayer at al-Bathaâ€™, except al-jahdami who did not make  mention of it.
