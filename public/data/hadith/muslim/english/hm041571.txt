4|1571|  Chapter: Excellence of the forenoon prayer, two are its minimum Rak’ahs and eight are its  maximum Rak’ahs and average Rak’ahs are four or six, and exhortation for the observance of  the prayer.  ‘Aisha reported that the Apostle (may peace be upon him) was not so much particular  about observing supererogatory rak’ahs as in case of the two rak’ahs of the dawn  prayer.
