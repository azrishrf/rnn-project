6|2479|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing the fast or not observing it in the month of Ramadan for a  traveller.  Abu Saâ€™id al-Khudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported: We went out on an expedition  with the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) during Ramadan and neither the  observer of the fast was found fault with for his fasting, nor the breaker of the fast for  breaking it.
