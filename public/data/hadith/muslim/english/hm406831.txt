40|6831|  Chapter: The haughty and the proud would Set into the fire of hell and the humble and meek  would get into Paradise.  It is transmitted on the authority of Abu Huraira that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) said: The molar tooth of an unbeliever or the canine teeth of an unbeliever  will be like Ubud and the thickness of his skin a three night’s journey.
