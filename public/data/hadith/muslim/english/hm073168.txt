7|3168|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported: If I were to see deer grazing in  Medina, I would have never molested them, for Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) has stated: There is between the two lava mountains a sacred territory.
