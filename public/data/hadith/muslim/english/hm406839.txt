40|6839|  Chapter: The haughty and the proud would Set into the fire of hell and the humble and meek  would get into Paradise.  Sa’id b. Musayyib explained “al-bahira” as that animal which is not milked but for the  idols and none amongst the people milks them, and “as-sa’iba” as that animal which is  let loose for the deities. Nothing is loaded over it, and Ibn Musayyib narrated that Abu  Huraira stated that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: I saw ‘Amr b.  ‘Amir al-Khuzili dragging his intestines in fire and he was the first who devoted animals  to deity.
