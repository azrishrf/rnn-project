37|6660|  Chapter: The words of Allah “The good deeds take away the evil deeds”.  Anas reported that a person came to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said:  Allah’s Messenger, I have committed an offence which deserves imposition of haad, so  impose it upon me according to the Book of Allah. Thereupon he said: Were you not  present with us at the time of prayer? He said: Yes. Thereupon he said: You have been  granted pardon.
