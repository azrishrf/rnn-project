7|2871|  Chapter: Clipping of hair in ‘Umra.  Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Mu’awiya b. Abu Safyin had told  him: I clipped the hair (from the head of) Allah’s Messenger (may peace he upon him)  with a clipper while he was at al-Marwa, or I saw him getting his hair clipped with a  clipper as he was at al-Marwa.1722
