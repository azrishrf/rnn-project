30|5788|  Chapter: Pertaining to the old age of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).         Jabir b. Samura was asked about the old age of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him). He said: When he oiled his head nothing was seen (as a mark of old age) and  when he did not apply oil something (of the old age) became visible.  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
