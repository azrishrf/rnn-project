7|3122|  Chapter: Staying at Dhu’l-Halaifa and observing of prayer there, while returning from  Hajj or Umra.  Nafi’ reported that when ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar returned from Hajj or ‘Umra he made his  camel kneel down (i.e. halted) in the stony ground of Dhu’l-Hulaifa where Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) had made his camel halt.
