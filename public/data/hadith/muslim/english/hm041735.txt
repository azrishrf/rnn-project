4|1735|  Chapter: Concerning the careful remembering of the Qur’an.  Abu Burda narrated on the authority of Abu Musa that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) had said to Abu Musa: If you were to see me, as I was listening to  your recitation (of the Qur’an) yester-night (you would have felt delighted). You are in  fact endowed with a sweet voice like that of David himself.
