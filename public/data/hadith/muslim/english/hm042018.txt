4|2018|  Chapter: The dead body is punished for the lamentation of his family.         Ibn ‘Umar reported: When ‘Umar was wounded he fainted, and there was a loud  lamentation over him. When he regained consciousness he said: Didn’t you know that  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: “The dead is punished because  of the weeping of the living”?
