7|2940|  Chapter: Pronouncing of Talbiya and takbir while going from Mina to ‘Arafat on the day  of ‘Arafa.  Muhammad b. Abu Bakr reported: I said to Anas b. Malik in the morning of ‘Arafa: What  do you say as to pronouncing Talbiya on this day? He said: I travelled with Allah’s  Apostle (may peace he upon him) and his Companions in this journey. Some of us  pronounced Takbir and some of us pronounced Tahlil, and none of us found fault with  his companion.
