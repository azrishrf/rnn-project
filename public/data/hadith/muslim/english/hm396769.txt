39|6769|  Chapter: None would attain salvation because of his deeds but it is through Lord’s Mercy (that  he would attain that).  Jabir reported: I heard Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: None of you  would get into Paradise because of his good deeds alone, and he would not be rescued  from Fire, not even I, but because of the Mercy of Allah.
