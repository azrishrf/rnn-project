7|2866|  Chapter: Garlanding the sacrificial animals, and marking them.  This Hadith has been narrated on the authority of Qatada with the same chain of  transmitters but with this variation (of words): “When Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) came to Dhu’l-Hulaifa” and he made no mention (of the fact) that he led the  Zuhr prayer.
