4|916|  Chapter: Recitation of the Qur’an loudly in the dawn prayer.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri reported: The noon prayer would start and one would go to al-Baqi’  and after having relieved himself he would perform ablution and then come, while the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) would be in the first rak’ah, because he  would prolong it so much.
