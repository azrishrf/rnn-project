31|6104|  Chapter: The merits of the settlements of the Ansar).  Abu Usaid reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The  worthiest clans of the Ansar are Banu Najjar, thereafter Banu al-Ashhal; thereafter Banu  Harith b. Banu Khazraj; thereafter Banu Sa’idah and there is goodness in all clans of the  Ansar. Sa’d said: I see that he (the Holy Prophet) has placed others above us. It was  said to (him): He has placed you above many others.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
