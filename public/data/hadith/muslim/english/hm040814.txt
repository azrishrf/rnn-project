4|814|  Chapter: Blessing on the Prophet (may peace be upon him) after Tashahhud.         Abu Harare reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: When  anyone amongst you utters Amin and the angels In the heaven also utter Amin and (the  Amin) of the one synchronises with (that of) the other, all his previous sins are  pardoned.
