10|3694|  Chapter: It is forbidden to sell fresh dates against dry dates, except in case of al-Araya.  ‘Abdullah (b. Umar) reported that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) forbade  Muzabana, i.e. buying of fresh dates (on) the trees for dry dates by measure, and the  buying of grapes for raisins by measure and the selling of field of corn for corn by  measure.
