4|1647|  Chapter: Forenoon prayer should be offered when it is sufficiently hot.  Ibn ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The night  prayer consists of pairs and when you see the approach of dawn, make this number  odd by one rak’ah. It was said to Ibn ‘Umar: What does the (word) pair imply? He said:  (It means) that salutation is uttered after every two rak’ahs.
