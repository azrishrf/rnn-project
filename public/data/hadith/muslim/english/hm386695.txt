38|6695|  Chapter: Not known.  Iyas reported on the authority of his father: We went along with Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) to visit a person suffering from fever. When I placed my hand upon  him, I said: By Allah, I have never seen, till this day, a person running higher  temperature than he. Thereupon Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him), turning his  face to his companions, said: May I not inform you of a severer temperature than this  which these two persons would run on the Day of Resurrection? And they were two  hypocrites riding upon the camel turning their back towards (the Muslims).  COMMAND CONCERNING THEM
