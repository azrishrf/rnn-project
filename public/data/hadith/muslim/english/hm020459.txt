2|459|  Chapter: While cleaning the nose and using of pebbles in the toilet, the odd number is  preferable.  Hammam b. Munabbih reported: This is what Abu Huraira transmitted to us from  Muhammad, the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), and he mentioned a  number of a Hadith, of which this is one: that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) said: When anyone amongst you (performs ablution) he must snuff his  nostrils with water and then clean them.
