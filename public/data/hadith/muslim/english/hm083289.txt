8|3289|  Chapter: Prohibition of one’s making the proposal of marriage when it has already been made  by one’s brother until he permits or gives her up.  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Apostle (May peace be upon  him) as having forbidden a dweller of the town selling the merchandise of a villager or  outbidding in a sale (in order that another might fall into a snare), or a person making  the proposal of marriage when his brother has already made such a proposal, or  entering into a transaction when his brother has already entered; and a woman asking  the divorce of her sister in order to deprive her of what belongs to her. ‘Amr made this  addition: “The person should not purchase in opposition to his brother.”
