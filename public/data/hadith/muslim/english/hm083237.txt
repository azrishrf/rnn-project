8|3237|  Chapter: One who can support a wife should marry.  Sa’d b. Abi Waqqas (Allah be pleased with him) reported: The Messenger of Allah (May  peace be upon him) rejected (the idea) of Uthman b. Muz’ un-living in celibacy (saying):  And if he (the Holy Prophet) had given me permission We would have got ourselves  castrated.
