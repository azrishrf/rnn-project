32|6240|  Chapter: Whenever a believer falls sick or is stricken with grief or something like it,  there is a reward for him even if it is pricking of a thorn.         ‘Aisha said: No trouble comes to a believer even if it is the pricking of a thorn that it  becomes (the means) whereby his sins are effaced or his sins are obliterated.
