10|3813|  Chapter: Riba (Usury).  Abu Zubair heard Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with him) saying: Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) ordered us to kill dogs, and we carried out this  order so much so that we also kill the dog coming with a woman from the desert. Then  Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) forbade their killing. He (the Holy Prophet  further) said: It is your duty the jet-black (dog) having two spots (on the eyes), for it is a  devil.
