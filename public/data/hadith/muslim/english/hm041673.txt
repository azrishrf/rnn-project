4|1673|  Chapter: Encouragement to observe prayers during Ramadhan and that is Tarawih.  Makhrama b. Sulaiman narrated it with the same chain of narrators and he made this  addition: “He then went to the water-skin and brushed his teeth and performed ablution  well.
