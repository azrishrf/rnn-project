3|699|  Chapter: Abrogation of the Hadith that ablution is obligatory for him who takes  something cooked with the help of fire.  This Hadith is narrated by Muhammad b. ‘Amr b. Ata’ with these words: I was with Ibn  ‘Abbas, and Ibn ‘Abbas saw the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) doing like  this, and it is also said that the words are: He (the Holy Prophet) offered prayer; and the  word “people” is not mentioned.
