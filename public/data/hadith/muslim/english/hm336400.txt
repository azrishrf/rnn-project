33|6400|  Chapter: The growth of a child in the womb of a mother and his destiny in regard to his  livelihood, his deeds. both good and evil.  ‘Ali reported that one day Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) was sitting with  a wood in his hand and he was scratching the ground. He raised his head and said:  There is not one amongst you who has not been allotted his seat in Paradise or Hell.  They said: Allah’s Messenger, then, why should we perform good deeds, why not  depend upon our destiny? Thereupon he said: No, do perform good deeds, for  everyone is facilitated in that for which he has been created; then he recited this verse:  “Then, who gives to the needy and guards against evil and accepts the excellent (the  truth of Islam and the path of righteousness it prescribes), We shall make easy for him  the easy end...” (xcii. 5-10).
