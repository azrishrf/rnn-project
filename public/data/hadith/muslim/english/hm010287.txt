1|287|  Chapter: The descent of Jesus son of Mary, and he will judge according to the Shariâ€™ah  of our Apostle, Muhammad(may peace be upon him).  Abu Huraira reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: By  Him in Whose hand is my life, the son of Mary (may peace be upon him) will soon  descend among you as a just judge. He will break crosses, kill swine and abolish Jizya  and the wealth will pour forth to such an extent that no one will accept it.
