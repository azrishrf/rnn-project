31|6134|  Chapter: The merits of the tribes of Ghifir, Ashia’, Muzaina, Tamim, Daws and Tayyi.  Abu Huraira reported: There are some distinguishing features of Banu Tamim which I  heard from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and my love for them is never  on the decline after that and the words are: They are the bravest amongst people in the  battlefield and there is no mention of (the word) “Dajjal”.         HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
