7|3066|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: When Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him) decided to march (for return journey), he found Safiyyah at the door of her tent,  sad and downcast. He remarked. Barren, shaven-head, you are going to detain us, and  then said: Did you perform Tawaf Ifada on the Day of Nahr? She replied in the  affirmative, whereupon he said: Then march on.
