The Caliph is a shepherd over the people and shall be questioned  about his subjects (as to how he conducted their affairs). A man is a guardian over the  members of his family and shall be questioned about them (as to how he looked after  their physical and moral well-being). A woman is a guardian over the household of her  husband and his children and shall be questioned about them (as to how she managed  the household and brought up the children). A slave is a guardian over the property of  his master and shall be questioned about it (as to how he safeguarded his trust).  Beware, every one of you is a guardian and every one of you shall be questioned with  regard to his trust.
