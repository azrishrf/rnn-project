32|6330|  Chapter: Grim warning to one who torments people without any valid justification.  ‘Urwa b. Zubair reported that Hisham b. Hakim found a person (the ruler of Hims) who  had been detaining some Nabateans in connection with the dues of Jizya. He said:  What is this? I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Allah  would torment those persons who torment people in the world.  OF RELATIONSHIP
