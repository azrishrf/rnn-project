4|1432|  Chapter: Who deserves most to act as Imam.  Abu Salama b. ‘Abd al-Rahman is reported to have said that he had heard Abu Huraira  saying: I would say prayer along with you which is near to the prayer of the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) and Abu Huraira recited Qunut in the noon and in the  ‘Isha and in the morning prayer, and invoked blessing (of Allah) upon Muslims-and  curse upon the unbelievers.
