4|1521|  Chapter: Permissibility of combining two prayers on a journey.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: I observed with the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) eight  (rak’ahs) in combination, and seven rak’ahs in combination. I (one of the narrators) said:  O Abd Sha’tha’, I think that he (the Holy Prophet) had delayed the noon prayer and  hastened the afternoon prayer, and he delayed the sunset prayer and hastened the  ‘Isha prayer. He said: I also think so.
