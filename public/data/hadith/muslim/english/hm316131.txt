31|6131|  Chapter: The merits of the tribes of Ghifir, Ashia’, Muzaina, Tamim, Daws and Tayyi.         ‘Adi b. Hatim reported: I came to Umar b. Khattab and he said to me: The first  consignment of Sadaqa brought to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) which  brightened the face of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and the faces of his  Companions was that of Tayyi.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
