4|1454|  Chapter: The excellence of Qunut in all the prayers when any calamity befalls the Muslims.  Qatada reported from Anas b. Malik that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) said: He who forgets the prayer should say it when he remembers it, there is no  explanation for it, except this. Qatada said: (Allah says) “And observe prayer for  remembrance of Me “
