32|6276|  Chapter: It is forbidden to curse the beasts.  ‘Imran b. Husain reported: We were with Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) in  some of his journeys and there was a woman from the Ansar riding a she-camel that it  shied and she invoked curse upon that. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)         heard it and said: Unload that and set it free for it is accursed. ‘Imran said: I still  perceive that (dromedary) walking amongst people and none taking any notice of that.
