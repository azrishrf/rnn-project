4|904|  Chapter: Listening to the recitation of the Qur’an.  This Hadith has been reported by Dawud with the same chain of transmitters up to the  word(s): “The traces of their embers.” Sha’bi said: They (the Jinn) asked about their  provision, and they were the Jinn of al-jazira, up to the end of the Hadith, and the words  of Sha’bi have been directly transmitted from the Hadith of Abdullah.
