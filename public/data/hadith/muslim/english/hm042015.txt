4|2015|  Chapter: The dead body is punished for the lamentation of his family.  ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported that Hafsa wept for ‘Umar (when he was about to due). He  (‘Umar) said: Be quiet, my daughter. Don’t you know that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) had said: “The dead is punished because of his family’s weeping  over it”?
