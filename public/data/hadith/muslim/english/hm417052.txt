41|7052|  Chapter: Approach of the Last Hour.  Anas b. Malik reported that a person asked Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him):  When would the Last Hour come? Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon  him) kept quiet for a while. then looked at a young boy in his presence belonging to the  tribe of Azd Shanilwa and he said: If this boy lives he would not grow very old till the  Last Hour would come to you. Anas said that this young boy was of our age daring  those days.
