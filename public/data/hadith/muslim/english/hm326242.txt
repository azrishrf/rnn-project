32|6242|  Chapter: Whenever a believer falls sick or is stricken with grief or something tike it,  there is a reward for him even if it is pricking of a thorn.  Abu Sa’id and abu Huraira reported that they heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: Never a believer is stricken with discomfort, hardship or illness,  grief or even with mental worry that his sins are not expiated for him.
