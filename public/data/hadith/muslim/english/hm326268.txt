32|6268|  Chapter: According of benevolent treatment to one from whom one expects  transgression.  ‘Aisha reported that a person sought permission from Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) to see him. He said: Grant him permission. (and also added:) He is a bad son  of his tribe or he is a bad person of his tribe. When he came in he used kind words for  him. ‘Aisha reported that she said: Allah’s Messenger, you said about him what you had  to say and then you treated him with kindness. He said: ‘Aisha, verily in the eye of Allah,         worst amongst the person in rank on the Day of Resurrection is one whom the people  abandon or desert out of the fear of indecency.
