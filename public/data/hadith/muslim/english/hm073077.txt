7|3077|  Chapter: The merit of entering the ka’ba for a pilgrim and observing of prayer in it.  Isma’il b. Abu Khalid reported: I asked Abdullah b. Abu Aufa (Allah be pleased with  him), a Companion of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him), whether Allah’s  Apostle (may peace be upon him) had entered the House, while performing ‘Umra, He  said: NO.
