6|2549|  Chapter: Permissibility of completing the fasts of Ramadhan before the commencement of the  coming Ramadhan.         Abu Salama reported: I heard ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) as saying: I had to  complete some of the fasts of Ramadan, but I could not do it but during the month of  Sha’ban due to my duties to the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) or with  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him).
