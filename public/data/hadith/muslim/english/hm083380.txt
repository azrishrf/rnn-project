8|3380|  Chapter: Al-Azl (incomplete sexual intercourse): Coitus Interruptus.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported: Mention was made about al- ‘azl in the presence of Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon him), whereupon he  said: Why any one of you practises it? (He did not say: One of you should not do it), for  there is no created soul, whose creator is not Allah.
