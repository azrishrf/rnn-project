20|4628|  Chapter: The merit of Jihad and campaigning in the way of Allah.  It has been narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira who said: Allah has undertaken to  provide for one who leaves his home (only) to fight for His cause and to affirm the truth  of His word; Allah will either admit him to Paradise or will bring him back home from  where he had come out, with his reward and booty.
