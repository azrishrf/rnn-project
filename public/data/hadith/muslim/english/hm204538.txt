20|4538|  Chapter: Obedience to the ruler is forbidden in matters sinful, but is otherwise  obligatory.  It has been narrated on the authority of “Ubida who learnt the tradition from his father  who, in turn, learnt it from his own father. ‘Ubada’s grandfather said: The Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) took an oath of allegiance from us on our listening to  and obeying the orders of our commander in adversity and prosperity, in pleasure and  displeasure (and even) when somebody is given preference over us, on our avoiding to  dispute the delegation of powers to a person deemed to be a fit recipient thereof (in the  eye of one who delegates it) and on our telling the truth in whatever position we be  without fearing in the matter of Allah the reproach of the reproacher.
