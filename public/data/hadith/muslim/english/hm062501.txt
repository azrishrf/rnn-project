6|2501|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported. In the Pre-Islamic days fast was observed  on the day of Ashura, but with the advent of Islam (its position was ascertained as that  of a voluntary fast). Then he who wished to fast fasted, and he who liked to abandon it  abandoned it.
