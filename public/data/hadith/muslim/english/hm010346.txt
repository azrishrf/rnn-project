1|346|  Chapter: In proof of the fact that the believers would see their Lord hallowed be He and  exalted) on the Last Day.         ‘Abdullah b. Qais transmitted on the authority of his father (Abu Musa Ash’ari) that the  Apostle (may peace be upon him) said: There would be two gardens (in Paradise) the  vessels and contents of which would be of silver, and two gardens whose vessels and  contents would be of gold. The only thing intervening to hinder the people from looking  at their Lord will be the mantle of Grandeur over His face in the Garden of Eden.
