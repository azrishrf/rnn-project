7|2807|  Chapter: Staying and the significance of the verse: “Then hasten op from where the  people hasten on” (ii 199).  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that the Quraish (of the pre-Islamic days)  and those who followed their religions practices stayed at Muzdalifa, and they named  themselves as Hums, whereas all other Arabs stayed at ‘Arafa. With the advent of  Islam, Allah, the Exalted and Glorious, commanded His Apostle (may peace be upon  him) to come to ‘Arafat and stay there, and then hurry from there, and this is the  significance of the words of Allah: “Then hasten on from where the people hasten on.”
