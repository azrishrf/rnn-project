27|5593|  Chapter: The proper use of the words: Bondman, Slave-girl, Al-Maula and As-Sayyid.  This hadith has been reported on the authority of al-A’mash with the same chain of  transmitters, and the words are that the servant should not say to his chief: My Lord,  and Abu Mu’awiya made an addition: “For it is Allah, the Exalted and Glorious, Who is  your Lord.”
