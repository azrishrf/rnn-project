7|2792|  Chapter: Types of Ihram- It is permissible to enter into the state of Ihram singularly for  the sake of Hajj or for tamattu’ bi’l-‘umra ila’l-Hajj or for both hajj and ‘umra together.  Jabir b. Abdullah is reported to have said that the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon  him) came to ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) and she was weeping. The rest of the  Hadith is the same.
