18|4267|  Chapter: Rejecting of the wrong things and the innovations (in religion).  Sa’d b. Ibrahim reported: I asked Qasim b. Muhammad about a person who had three  dwelling houses and he willed away the third part of every one of these houses; he  (Qasim b. Muhammad) said: All of them could be combined in one house; and then  said: ‘Aisha informed me that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: He  who did any act for which there is no sanction from our behalf, that is to be rejected.
