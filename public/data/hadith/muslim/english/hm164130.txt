16|4130|  Chapter: Pertaining to the combatants and apostates.  Anas b. Malik reported that some people belonging (to the tribe) of ‘Uraina came to  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) at Medina, but they found its climate  uncongenial. So Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to them: If you so  like, you may go to the camels of Sadaqa and drink their milk and urine. They did so  and were all right. They then fell upon the shepherds and killed them and turned  apostates from Islam and drove off the camels of the Prophet (may peace be upon him).  This news reached Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) and he sent (people) on  their track and they were (brought) and handed over to him. He (the Holy Prophet) got  their hands cut off, and their feet, and put out their eyes, and threw them on the stony  ground until they died.  MURDERS, FIGHTING, REQUITAL
