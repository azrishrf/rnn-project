32|6273|  Chapter: Merit of benevolent treatment.  ‘Aisha, the wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him), reported that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: ‘Aisha, verily Allah is kind and He loves  kindness and confers upon kindness which he does not confer upon severity and does  not confer upon anything else besides it (kindness).
