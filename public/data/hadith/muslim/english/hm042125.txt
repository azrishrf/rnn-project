4|2125|  Chapter: Funeral prayer in the mosque.  Abu Salama b. ‘Abd al-Rahman reported on the authority of ‘Aisha that when Sa’d b.  Abu Waqqas died she said: Bring it (the bier) into the mosque so that I offer prayer for  him. But, this act of hers was disapproved. She said: By Allah, the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) offered prayer in the mosque for the two sons of Baida’, viz,  for Suhail and his brother.
