2|521|  Chapter: Wiping over the socks.  This hadith is narrated on the same authority from A’mash by another chain of  transmitters like one transmitted by Abu Mu’awyia. The hadith reported by ‘Isa and  Sufyan has these words also: “This hadith surprised the friends of Abdullab'” for Jarir  had embraced Islam after the revelation of al-Ma’ida.
