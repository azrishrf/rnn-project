9|3492|  Chapter: Pronouncement of three divorces.  Abu Sahba’ said to Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them): Do you know that three  (divorces) were treated as one during the lifetime of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him), and that of Abu Bakr, and during three (years) of the caliphate of Umar  (Allah be pleased with him)? Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) said: Yes.
