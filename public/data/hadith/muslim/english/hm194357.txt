19|4357|  Chapter: The saying of the Prophet (may peace be upon him): “We (Prophets) do not  have any heirs; what we leave behind is (to be given in) charity”.  It his been narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him)said: “We do not have any heirs; what we leave behind is a  charitable endowment.”
