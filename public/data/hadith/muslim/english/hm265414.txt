26|5414|  Chapter: If a person goes away from the place occupied by him, he has a better right to occupy  it after coming back.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: When  anyone amongst you stands up, and in the hadith transmitted on the authority of Abu  ‘Awina, the words are: “He who stands in his place and (goes away) and then comes  back to it, he his the greatest right (to occupy that).
