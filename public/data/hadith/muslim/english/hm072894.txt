7|2894|  Chapter: Excellence of walking at a quick pace in Tawaf and Umra and on the occasion  of first Tawaf in Hajj.  Ibn’Umar(Allah be pleased with them) reported that when Allah’s messenger (may  peace be upon him) circumambulated in Hajj and Umra he walked swiftly in the first  three circuit about the House, and then walked in four circuits, and then observed two  rak’ahs of prayer, and then ran between al-Safa and al-Marwa.
