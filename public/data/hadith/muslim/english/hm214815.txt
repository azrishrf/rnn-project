21|4815|  Chapter: It is forbidden to tie the animal and make it a target of arrows.  Sa’id b. Jubair reported that Ibn ‘Umar happened to pass by a party of men who had  tied a hen and were shooting arrows at it. As soon as they saw Ibn ‘Umar, they  scattered from it. Thereupon Ibn Umar said: Who has done this? Verily Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) has invoked curse upon him who does this.
