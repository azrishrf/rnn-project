1|110|  Chapter: Concerning diminution of Belief due to sins and its separation from the sinner;  negation of the excellence of Islam at the time of committing sin.  Muhammad b. Rafi’, Abdur-Razzaq, Sufyan, A’mash narrated this hadith like one  narrated by Shu’ba, on the authority of Abu Huraira tracing, it (right to the Holy Prophet).
