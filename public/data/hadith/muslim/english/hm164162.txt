16|4162|  Chapter: The (cases of) bloodshed would be decided first of all on the day of judgment.  Abu Bakra reported that when it was the day of (Dhu’l-Hijja) Allah’s Apostle (may peace  be upon him) mounted the camel and addressed and a person had been holding its  nose-string.
