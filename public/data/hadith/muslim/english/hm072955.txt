7|2955|  Chapter: Returning from ‘Arafat to Muzdalifa and excellence of observing sunset and  ‘Isha prayers together at Muzdalifa.  Sa’id b. Jubair reported: We came back along with Ibn ‘Umar till we reached Muzdalifa.  There he led us in the sunset and ‘Isha prayers with one iqama and we then proceeded  and he said: This is how Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) led us in prayer  at this place.
