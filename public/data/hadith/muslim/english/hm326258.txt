32|6258|  Chapter: There should be mutual fellow-feeling and love and the will to help each other  amongst the believers.  Nu’man b. Bashir reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The  similitude of believers in regard to mutual love, affection, fellow-feeling is that of one  body; when any limb of it aches, the whole body aches, because of sleeplessness and  fever.
