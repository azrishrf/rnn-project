24|5298|  Chapter: It Is forbidden to add false hair to one’s head or to pluck the eyebrow or to  separate the teeth.  ‘Aisha reported that a girl of the Ansar who had fallen ill and had lost the hair was  married. They (her relatives) thought of adding false hair (to her head), so they asked         Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) about it, whereupon he cursed the woman  who adds false hair and the woman who asks for it.
