3|691|  Chapter: Abrogation of the Hadith that ablution is obligatory for him who takes  something cooked with the help of fire.  Ja’far b. Amr b. Umayya al-Damari reported on the authority of his father who said: I  saw the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) take slices from goat’s shoulder,  and then eat them, and then offer prayer without having performed ablution.
