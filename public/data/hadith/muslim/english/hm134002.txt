13|4002|  Chapter: The deceased is entitled to reward for the sadaqa given after his death.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that a man said to Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him): My mother died all of a sudden, and I think if she (could have the  opportunity) to speak she would have (made a will) regarding Sadaqa’. Will I be entitled  to reward if I give charity on her behalf? He (the Holy Prophet) said: Yes.
