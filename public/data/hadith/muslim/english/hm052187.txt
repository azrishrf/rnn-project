5|2187|  Chapter: Getting of reward to Sadaqa for the dead given on his or her behalf.  Maimuna bint Harith reported that she set free a slave-girl during the lifetime of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) and she made a mention of that to the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) and he said: Had you gives her to your  maternal uncles, you would have a greater reward.
