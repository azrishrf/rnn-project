1|103|  Chapter: Din is sincerity and well-wishing.         It is narrated on the authority of Jarir that he observed: I owed allegiance to the Apostle  of Allah (may peace and blessings be upon him) on hearing (is commands) and obeying  (them) and the Prophet) instructed me (to act) as lay in my power, and sincerity and  goodwill for every Muslim.
