13|4010|  Chapter: He who has not anything with him to will away should not do it.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Malik b. Mighwal with the same chain  of transmitters but with a slight variation of words. In the hadith related by Waki (the  words are) “I said: How the people have been ordered about the will”; and in the hadith  of Ibn Numair (the words are): “How the will has been prescribed for the Muslims,’.
