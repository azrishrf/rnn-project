20|4614|  Chapter: Great benefit in the forelocks of the horses.  It has been narrated on the authority of Jarir b. Abdullah who said: I saw that the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) was twisting the forelock of a horse with  his fingers and he was saying: (A great) benefit. i.e. reward (for rearing them for Jihad)  and spoils of war, has been tied to the forelocks of horses until the Day of Judgment.
