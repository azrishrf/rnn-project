4|1814|  Chapter: How Amr b. Abasa embraced Islam.  ‘Aisha said that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) did not abandon  observing two rak’ahs after ‘Asr, but she reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: Do not get used to observe prayer at the time of the rising sun and  at the time of its setting and (exhorted the Muslims) to pray at their times.
