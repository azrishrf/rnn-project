7|2878|  Chapter: Talbiya of the Apostle (may peace be upon him) and his sacrifice.  Hanzala b. ‘Ali al-Aslaml reported that he had heard Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with  him) as saying that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) bed said: By Him In  Whose Hand is my life; the rest of the Hadith is the same.
