7|2912|  Chapter: Excellence of kissing the black stone ;while circumambulating.  Salim narrated on the authority of his father (Allah be pleased with him) that ‘Umar b. al- Khattab (Allah be pleased with him) kissed (the Black Stone) and then said: By Allah, I  know that you are a stone and if I were not to see Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) kissing you, I would not have kissed you. Harun said in his narration: A  Hadith like this has been transmitted to me by Zaid b. Aslam on the authority of his  father Aslam.
