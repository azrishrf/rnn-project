7|2942|  Chapter: Returning from ‘Arafat to Muzdalifa and excellence of observing sunset and  ‘Isha prayers together at Muzdalifa.  Usama b. Zaid (Allah be pleased with him) reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) on his way back from ‘Arafat got down in one of these creeks (to answer the  call of nature), and after he had done that I poured water (over his hands) and said: Are  you going to pray? Thereupon he said: The place of prayer is ahead of you.
