4|1766|  Chapter: Excellence of sura Fatiha and concluding verses of surat al-Baqara and  exhortation to recite the last two verses of surat al-Baqara.  Abu Darda’ reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: If anyone  learns by heart the first ten verses of the Surah Al-Kahf, he will be protected from the  Dajjal.
