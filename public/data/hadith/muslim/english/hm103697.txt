10|3697|  Chapter: It is forbidden to sell fresh dates against dry dates, except in case of al-Araya.  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) having forbidden Muzabana, and Muzabana implies the selling of dry dates  for fresh dates on the tree with a definite measure (making it clear) that in case it  increases, it belongs to me and if it is less, it is my responsibility.
