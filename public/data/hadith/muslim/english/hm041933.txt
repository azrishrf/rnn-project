4|1933|  Chapter: Permissibility of women’s going out on ‘Id days towards the place of worship  and their presence in the Khutba (sitting) at a distance from men.  Umm Atiyya reported: We were commanded to bring out on old days purdah-observing  ladies and those unmarried, and menstruating women came out but remained behind  people and pronounced takbir (Allah-o-Akbar) along with them.
