7|3033|  Chapter: It is meritorious for one who does intend to go himself to send the sacrificial  animal to al-Haram.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) sent the sacrificial animals from Medina. I wove garlands for his sacrificial animals  (and then he hung them round their necks), and he would not avoid doing anything  which the Muhrim avoids A Hadith like this has been transmitted on the authority of Ibn  Shihab.
