4|1566|  Chapter: Excellence of the forenoon prayer, two are its minimum Rak’ahs and eight are its  maximum Rak’ahs and average Rak’ahs are four or six, and exhortation for the observance of  the prayer.  Hafsa reported: When the dawn appeared, the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon  him) observed two rak’ahs (of Sunnah prayers).
