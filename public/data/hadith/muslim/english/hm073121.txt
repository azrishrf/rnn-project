7|3121|  Chapter: Staying at Dhu’l-Halaifa and observing of prayer there, while returning from  Hajj or Umra.  Nafi’ reported that ‘Abdullah b. Umar (Allah be pleased with them) used to halt his  camel in the stony ground at Dhu’l-Hulaifa, where Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) used to make a halt (and pray).
