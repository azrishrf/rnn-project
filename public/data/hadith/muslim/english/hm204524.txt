20|4524|  Chapter: Obedience to the ruler is forbidden in matters sinful, but is otherwise  obligatory.  It has been narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) said: It is obligatory for you to listen to the ruler and obey him in  adversity and prosperity, in pleasure and displeasure, and even when another person is  given (rather undue) preference over you.
