7|3011|  Chapter: Excellence of making a halt at al Muha’ab, on the day of Nahr, and observing  prayer there.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported: Halt at Muhassab is not something  (significant from the point of view of the Shari’ah). It is a place of halt where Allah’s  Messenger (way peace be upon him) halted.
