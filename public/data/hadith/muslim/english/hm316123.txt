31|6123|  Chapter: The merits of the tribes of Ghifir, Ashia’, Muzaina, Tamim, Daws and Tayyi.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The tribes  of Ashja’, Ghifar and Muzaina and from the tribe of Juhaina they are better than Banu  Tamim, Banu Amir and the allies of Asad and Ghatfan.               HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
