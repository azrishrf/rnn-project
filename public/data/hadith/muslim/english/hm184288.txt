18|4288|  Chapter: Entertainment of the Guest.  Sa’id al-Maqburi reported: I heard Abu Shuraih al-Khuzill saying: My ears heard and my  eyes saw and my mind retained it, when Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  spoke this, and he then narrated the hadith and made mention of this: “It is not  permissible for any one of you to stay with his brother until he makes him sinful.”
