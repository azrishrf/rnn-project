25|5365|  Chapter: Disapproval of saying: “It is I” In response to one’s inquiry while seeking  permission to get in the house.  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of Shu’ba with a slight variation of  wording and that is: The Holy Prophet (may peace be upon him) uttered these words: It  is I, it is I.” in the manner as if he disapproved of this.
