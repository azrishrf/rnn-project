8|3326|  Chapter: Excellence of emancipating a slave-girl and then marrying her.  This hadith has been narrated through another chain of transmitters on the authority of  Anas that Allah’s Apostle (May peace be upon him) emancipated Safiyya, and her  emancipation was treated as her wedding gift, and in the hadith transmitted by Mu’adh  on the authority of his father (the words are): “He (the Holy Prophet) married Safiyya  and bestowed her emancipation as her wedding gift.”
