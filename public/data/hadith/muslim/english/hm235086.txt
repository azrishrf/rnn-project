23|5086|  Chapter: Excellence of truffles and their use as a medicine for the eyes.  Sa’id b. Zaid b. ‘Amr b. Nufail reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as  saying: Truffles are a kind of ‘Manna’ which Allah the Glorious and Exalted, sent down  upon the people of Israil, and its juice is a medicine for the eyes.
