4|1210|  Chapter: Taslim at the completion of the prayer.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: We knew the finishing of the prayer of the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) through takbir. ‘Amr (b. Dinar) said: I made a mention of it to  Abu Mas’ud, he rejected it and said: I never narrated it to you. ‘Amr said: He did narrate  it before this.
