4|1036|  Chapter: The excellence of the prayer and the way it is begun and the excellence of  Ruku’ and moderation in it and prostration and moderation in it, etc.         ‘Aisha reported: The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) said his whole prayer  (Tahajjud prayer) during the night while I lay between him and the Qibla. When he  intended to say Witr (prayer) he awakened me and I too said witr (prayer).
