32|6306|  Chapter: Serious prohibition of tale-carrying.  ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud reported that Muhammad (may peace be upon him) said: Should I  inform you that slandering, that is in fact a tale-carrying which creates dissension  amongst people, (and) he (further) said: The person tells the truth until he is recorded  as truthful, and lie tells a lie until lie is recorded as a liar.
