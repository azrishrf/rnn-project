10|3738|  Chapter: Leasing out land.  Nafi reported: I went to Rafi b. Khadij in the company of Ibn ‘Umar (All be pleased with  them) until he (Ibn ‘Umar) came to him at Balat (a place near Prophet’s Mosque at  Medina) and he (Rafi b. Khadij) informed him that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) had forbidden the renting of land.
