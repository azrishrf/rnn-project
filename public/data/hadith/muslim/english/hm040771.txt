4|771|  Chapter: The reciting of Takbir at the time of bowing and rising in prayer except rising after  Ruku’ when it is said: Allah listened to him who praised Him.  ‘Ubada b. as-Samit reported from the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him ): He  who does not recite Fatihat al-Kitab is not credited with having observed the prayer.
