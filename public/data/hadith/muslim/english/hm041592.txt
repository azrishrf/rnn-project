4|1592|  Chapter: The virtue of Sunan prayers before and after the Fard Rak’ahs and their number.  Alqama b. Waqqas reported: I asked ‘Aisha how the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) did in the two rak’ahs as he (observed them) sitting. She said: He would  recite (the Qur’an) in them, and when he intended to bow, he would stand up and then  bowed.
