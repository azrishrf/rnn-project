31|6165|  Chapter: Meaning of the saying of the Prophet (may peace be upon him): “No person would  survive after a century who is living by this time of mine”.  Abu Sa’id reported that when Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) came back from  Tabuk they (his Companions) asked about the Last Hour. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) said: There would be none amongst the created beings living  on the earth (who would survive this century).  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
