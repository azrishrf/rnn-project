7|2726|  Chapter: Which animal the Muhrim and the non-Muhrim are permitted to kill in the state  of Ihram at the conclusion of it (within the precincts of the Ka’ba).  Zaid b. Jubair reported: A person asked Ibn Umar which beast a Muhrim could kill.  Thereupon he said: One of the wives of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) told  me: He (the Holy Prophet) commanded to kill rat, scorpion, kite, voracious dog and  crow.
