7|3110|  Chapter: Travelling of a woman with her Mahram for Hajj, etc.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) delivering a sermon and making this observation: “No person  should be alone with a woman except when there is a Mahram with her, and the woman  should not undertake journey except with a Mahram.” A person stood up and said:  Allah’s Messenger, my wife has set out for pilgrimage, whereas I am enlisted to fight in  such and such battle, whereupon he said: “You go and perform Hajj with your wife.”
