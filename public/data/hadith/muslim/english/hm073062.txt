7|3062|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.  Abd al-Rahman b. al Qasim narrated on the authority of ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with  her) that she made a mention to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) that  Safiyyah had entered the period of menses. The rest of the Hadith is the same.
