23|5109|  Chapter: Excellence of sharing the small food.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as  saying: Food for one person suffices two persons and food for two persons suffices four  persons, and food for four persons suffices eight persons; and in the tradition  transmitted on the authority of Ishaq there is no mention of the fact that he heard it  directly (from the Holy Prophet).
