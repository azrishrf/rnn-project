4|1435|  Chapter: Who deserves most to act as Imam.  Anas b. Malik reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed  Qunut for a month in the dawn prayer after ruku’ and invoked curse upon Ri’l, Dhakwan,  and said that ‘Usayya had disobeyed Allah and His Apostle (may peace be upon him).
