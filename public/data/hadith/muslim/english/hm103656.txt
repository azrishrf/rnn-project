10|3656|  Chapter: Both the buyers and the sellers have the option to withdraw the transaction  before leaving the meeting (where the bargain is struck).  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: Both parties in a business transaction have the right to annul it so  long as they have not separated; except in transactions which have been made subject  to the right of parties to annul them.
