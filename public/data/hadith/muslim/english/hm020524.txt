2|524|  Chapter: Wiping over the socks.  The son of Mughira b. Shu’ba reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) went out for relieving himself. Mughira went with him carrying a jug full of water.  When he (the Holy Prophet) came back after relieving himself, he poured water over  him and he performed ablution and wiped over his socks; and in the narration of Ibn  Rumh there is “till” instead of “when”.
