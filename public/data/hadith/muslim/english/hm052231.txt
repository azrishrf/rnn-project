5|2231|  Chapter: Concerning the collection of Sadaqa and doing of good deeds.  Abu Musa reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: The honest  Muslim trustee who spends (sometimes he said “who gives”) what he is commanded to  do and he gives that in full with his heart overflowing with cheerfulness and he gives it to  one to whom he is ordered, he is one of the givers of charity.
