1|63|  Chapter: Concerning the eminence of Islam and of the affairs which are excellent.         It is narrated on the authority of ‘Abdullah b. ‘Amr that a man asked the Messenger of  Allah (may peace and blessings be upon him) which of the merits (is superior) in Islam.  He (the Holy Prophet) remarked: That you provide food and extend greetings to one  whom you know or do not know.
