7|3160|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  ‘Asim reported: I asked Anas (Allah be pleased with him) whether Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) had declared Medina as sacred. He said: Yes, it is sacred, so  its tree is not to be cut; and he who did that let the curse of Allah and that of the angels  and of all people be upon him.
