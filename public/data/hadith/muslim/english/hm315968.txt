31|5968|  Chapter: The merits of Hadrat Khadija, mother of the faithful (Allah be pleased with her).  Ismail reported: I said to ‘Abdullah b. Abi Aufa: Did Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) give glad tidings of Paradise to Khadija? He said: Yes. He did give glad  tidings to her of a palace of jewels in Paradise wherein there would be no noise and no  toil.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
