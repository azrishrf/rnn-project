21|4773|  Chapter: The eating of the flesh of domestic asses is unlawful.  Bara’ b. ‘Azib reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) commanded us to  throw away the flesh of domestic asses whether uncooked or cooked; he then never  commanded us to eat that. This hadith has been narrated on the authority of ‘Asim with  the same chain of transmitters.
