37|6666|  Chapter: Throwing of non-believers in hell-fire for believers as Divine Grace and Mercy.  Abu Burda reported on the authority of his father that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) said: No Muslim would die but Allah would admit in his stead a Jew or a  Christian in Hell-Fire. ‘Umar b. Abd al-‘Aziz took an oath: By One besides Whom there  is no god but He, thrice that his father had narrated that to him from Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him).
