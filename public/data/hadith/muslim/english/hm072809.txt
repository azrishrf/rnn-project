7|2809|  Chapter: Staying and the significance of the verse: “Then hasten op from where the  people hasten on” (ii 199).  Jubair. b. Mut’im reported: I lost my camel and went in search of it on the day of ‘Arafa,  and I saw the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) staying along with people in  ‘Arafat. Thereupon I said: By Allah, he is among the Hums (Quraish) ; what has  happened to him that he has come to this (place)? The Quraish were counted among  Hums.
