8|3443|  Chapter: How long after marrying one should stay with a virgin or a woman previously married.  ‘Abd al-Malik b. Abu Bakr b. Abd al-Rahman b. al-Harith b. Hisham reported on the  authority of his father from Umm Salama (Allah be pleased with her) that when Allah’s  Messenger (May peace be upon him) married Umm Salama, he stayed with her for  three nights, and said: There is no lack of estimation on the part of your husband for  you. If you wish I can stay with you for a week, but in case I stay with you for a week,  then I shall have to stay for a week with all my wives.
