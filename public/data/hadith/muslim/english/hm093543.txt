9|3543|  Chapter: It is obligatory to abstain from adornment during the ‘Idda period, but  mourning beyond three days is prohibited.  Zainab bint Abu Salama reported: Umm Salama and Umm Habiba (Allah be pleased  with them) were talking with each other (and saying) that a woman came to Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) and mentioned to him that her daughter had lost  her husband, and her eyes were sore and she wanted to use collyrium, whereupon  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: One among you used to throw dung  at the end of a year, and now (this abstinence from adornment) is only for four months  and ten days.
