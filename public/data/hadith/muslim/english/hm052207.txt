5|2207|  Chapter: Acceptance of Sadaqa by honest work and its growth.  Abu Musa reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: There  would come a time for the people when a person would roam about with Sadaqa of  gold, but he would find no one to accept it from him. And a man would be seen followed  by forty women seeking refuge with him on account of the scarcity of males and  abundance of females.”
