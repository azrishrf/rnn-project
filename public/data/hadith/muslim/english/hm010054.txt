1|54|  Chapter: He relished the flavour of Iman who was pleased with Allah as his Lord.  It is narrated on the authority of ‘Abbas b. ‘Abdul-Muttalib that he heard the Messenger  of Allah saying: He relished the flavour of faith (Iman) who became the book of faith  pleased with Allah as Lord, with al-Islam as the code of life and with Muhammad as the  Messenger (of Allah).
