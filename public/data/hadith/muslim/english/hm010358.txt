1|358|  Chapter: Affirmation of intercession and rescue from Fire of the believers in Oneness of  Allah.  Abu Nadra narrated it from Abu Sa’id al-Khudri who reported it from the Apostle (may  peace be upon him) a similar (hadith) up to the words: “in the mud of the flood,” and he  did not mention (the words narrated) after it.
