4|762|  Chapter: The excellence of Adhin and running away of the Satan on hearing it.  Malik b. Huwairith reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) raised  his hands apposite his ears at the time of reciting the takbir (i.e. at the time of beginning  the prayer) and then again raised his hands apposite the ears at the time of bowing and  when he lifted his head after bowing he said: Allah listened to him who praised Him, and  did like it (raised his hands up to the ears).
