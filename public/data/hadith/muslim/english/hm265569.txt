26|5569|  Chapter: Prohibition of killing the ants.  Abu Huraira reported so many ahadith and one of them was this that Allah’ Messenger  (may peace be upon him) said: When an Apostle from amongst the Apostles of Allah  came to sit under a tree an ant bit him. He commanded his luggage to be removed from  under the tree and he commanded it to be burnt in the fire and Allah revealed to him:  “Why one ant (which had bitten you) was not killed (and why did you burn the others)?
