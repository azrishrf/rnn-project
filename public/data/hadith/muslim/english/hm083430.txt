8|3430|  Chapter: Suckling of a young (boy).  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon  him) visited me when a man was sitting near me, and he seemed to disapprove of that.  And I saw signs of anger on his face and I said: Messenger of Allah, he is my brother by  fosterage, whereupon he said: Consider who your brothers are because of fosterage  since fosterage is through hunger (i.e. in infancy).
