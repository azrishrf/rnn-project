7|2712|  Chapter: Hunting is forbidden for one who is in the state of Ihram.  This Hadith is narrated on the authority of ‘Uthman b. ‘Abdullah b. Mauhab with the  same chain of transmitters. And in the narration transmitted on the authority of Shaiban  (the words are): “The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Did any one of  you command him to attack it or point towards it?” And in the narration transmitted by  Shu’ba (the words are): “Did you point out or did you help or did you hunt?” Shu’ba said:  I do not know whether he said: “Did you help or did you hunt?”
