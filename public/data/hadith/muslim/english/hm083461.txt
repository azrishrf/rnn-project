8|3461|  Chapter: Excellence of marrying a virgin.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (May peace  be upon him) said to me: Jabir, have you married? The rest of the hadith is the same up  to (the words): “The woman would look after them and comb them.” He (Allah’s  Messenger), said: You did well. But no mention is made of the subsequent portion.
