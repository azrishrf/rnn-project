4|1104|  Chapter: Forbiddance of talking in prayer and abrogation of what was permissible.  Abu Huraira reported that he heard the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  saying: A highly wicked one amongst the Jinn escaped yester-night to interrupt my  prayer, but Allah gave me power over him, so I seized him and intended to tie him to  one of the pillars of the mosque in order that you, all together or all, might look at him,  but I remembered the supplication of my brother Sulaiman: “My Lord, forgive me, give  me such a kingdom as will not be possible for anyone after me” (Al-Qur’an, xxxvii. 35).
