25|5327|  Chapter: Disapproval of giving ugly names and such names as can be treated as ill omens.  Samura b. Jundub reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) forbade us to  give names to our servants as these four names: Aflah (Successful), Rabdh (Profit),  Yasar (Wealth), and Nafi’ (Beneficial).
