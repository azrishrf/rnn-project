34|6444|  Chapter: Pertaining to a disputationist.  Jundub b. ‘Abdullah al-Bajali reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as  saying: Recite the Qur’an as long as your hearts agree to do so, and when you feel  variance between them (between your hearts and tongues), then get up (and leave its  recital for the time being).
