2|504|  Chapter: How to cleanse oneself after relieving oneself.  Salman reported that it was said to him: Your Apostle (may peace be upon him) teaches  you about everything, even about excrement. He replied: Yes, he has forbidden us to  face the Qibla at the time of excretion or urination, or cleansing with right hand or with  less than three pebbles, or with dung or bone.
