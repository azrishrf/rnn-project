32|6336|  Chapter: The prohibition of pointing a weapon towards a Muslim.  Abu Huraira reported: Abu’l-Qasim (the kunya of Allah’s Messenger, may peace be  upon him), said: He who pointed a weapon towards his brother the angels invoke curse  upon him even if he is his real brother so long as he does not abandon it (the pointing of  weapon towards one’s brother Muslim).
