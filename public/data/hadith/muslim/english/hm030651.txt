3|651|  Chapter: The desirability of using musk at the spot of blood while bathing after  menstruation.  ‘Aisha reported: Asma’ b. Shakal came to the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) and said: Messenger of Allah, how one amongst us should take a bath after the         menstruation, and the rest of the Hadith is the same and there is no mention of bathing  because of sexual intercourse.
