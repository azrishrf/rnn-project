5|2153|  Chapter: Taking out of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr before observing Fitr prayer.  Ibn Umar reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) prescribed  Zakat-ul-Fitr of Ramadan one sa’ of dates or one sa’ of barley for every individual  among the Muslims (whether) free man or slave, male or female, young or old.
