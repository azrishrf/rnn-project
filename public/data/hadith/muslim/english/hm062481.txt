6|2481|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing the fast or not observing it in the month of Ramadan for a  traveller.  Abu Nadra reported Abu Saâ€™id al. Khudri and Jabir b. Abdullah as saying: We travelled  with the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him). The observer of the fast  observed it, and the breaker of the fast broke it, but none of them found fault with each  other.
