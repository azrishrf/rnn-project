20|4640|  Chapter: Merit of leaving for Jihad in the morning and evening.         It has been narrated on the authority of Sahl b. Sa’d as-Sa’idi that the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) said: The journey undertaken by a person in the  morning (for Jihad) in the way of Allah (will merit a reward) better than the world and all  that is in it.
