4|1609|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing Nafl (voluntary prayer) standing or sitting and observing  some part of it standing or sitting.  Abu Salama reported that he asked ‘Aisha about the prayer of the Messenger of Allah  (may peace he upon him) (during the night). The rest of the Hadith is the same but with  this exception that he (the Holy Prophet) observed nine rak’ahs including Witr.
