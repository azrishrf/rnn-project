30|5802|  Chapter: How long did the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) stay in Medina and  Mecca.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) stayed in Mecca  for thirteen years (after he had received revelation) and stayed in Medina for ten years,  and he was sixty-three when he died.
