2|443|  Chapter: The merit of Wudu and that of prayer after it.  Abu Anas reported that Uthman performed ablution at Maqi’aid and said: Should I not  show you the ablution performed by Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)? And  he then washed (the different parts of the body) three times.4′′ Qutaiba has added in his  narration the words: “There were with him (with Uthman) Companions of Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him).”
