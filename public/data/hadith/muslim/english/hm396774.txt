39|6774|  Chapter: The doing of good deeds very frequently and putting one’s best efforts in devotion to  Allah.  ‘Aisha reported that when Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) occupied  himself in prayer, he observed such a (long) qiylm (posture of standing in prayer) that  his feet were swollen. ‘Aisha said: Allah’s Messenger you do this (in spite of the fact)  that your earlier and later sins have been pardoned for you? Thereupon, he said. ‘Aisha  should I not prove myself to be a thanksgiving servant (of Allah)?  PARADISE AND HELL
