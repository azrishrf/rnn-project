4|1892|  Chapter: Warning on neglecting Jumu’a.  ‘Amra daughter of Abd al-Rahman reported on the authority of the sister of Amra, I  memorised (surah) Qaf – l.): “By the glorious Qur’an” from the mouth of the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) on Friday for he recited it on the pulpit on-every  Friday.
