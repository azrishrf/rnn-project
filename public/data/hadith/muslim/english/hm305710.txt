30|5710|  Chapter: The cistern of our Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its characteristics.  Anas reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: You would be  shown in it jugs of gold and silver (as numerous) as the number of stars in the sky. This  hadith has been transmitted on the authority of Anas b. Malik with this addition: “More  numerous than stars in the sky.”  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
