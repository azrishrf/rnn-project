31|6117|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked blessings upon the tribes of Ghifar  and Aslam.  Khufaf b. Jura’ reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said in  prayer: O Allah, hurl curse upon the tribe of Lihyan and Ri’l aid Dhakwan and Usayya  for they disobeyed Allah and His Messenger, (and for) Ghifar Allah has granted pardon  and for the tribe of Aslam Allah has granted safety.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
