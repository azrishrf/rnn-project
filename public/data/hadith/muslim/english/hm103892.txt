10|3892|  Chapter: Hoarding of foodstuff is forbidden.  Abd Mutawakkil al-Najl reported from Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them)  who said: I accompanied Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) in one of his  journeys (the narrator says, he said in Jihad), and he narrated the rest of the hadith, and  made this addition: He (the Holy Prophet) said: Jabir, have you received the price? I  said: Yes, whereupon he said: Yours is the price as well as the camel; yours is the price  as well as the camel.
