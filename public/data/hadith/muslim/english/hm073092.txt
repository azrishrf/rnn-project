7|3092|  Chapter: The validity of the Hajj performed by a boy and reward for one who enables  him to perform it (by accompanying him and bearing expenses for him).  Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported: A woman lifted up her child and said:  Messenger of Allah, would the child be credited with having performed the Hajj?  Thereupon he said: Yes, and there would be a reward for you.
