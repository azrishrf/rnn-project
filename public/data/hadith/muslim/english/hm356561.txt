35|6561|  Chapter: Refuge from the evil of what one has done, and what one has not done.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) used to say: “O  Allah, it is unto Thee that I surrender myself. I affirm my faith in Thee and repose my  trust in Thee and turn to Thee in repentance and with Thy help fought my adversaries.  O Allah, I seek refuge in Thee with Thine Power; there is no god but Thou, lest Thou  leadest me astray. Thou art ever-living that dieth not, while the Jinn and mankind die.”             REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
