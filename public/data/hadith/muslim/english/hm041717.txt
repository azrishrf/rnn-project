4|1717|  Chapter: Preference for observing Nafl prayer in the house and permissibility of  observing it in the mosque also.  ‘Aisha said: The Messenger of Allah (way peace be upon him) came to me when a  woman was sitting with me. He said: Who is she? I said: She is a woman who does not  sleep but prays. He said: Do such acts which you are capable of doing. By Allah, Allah  does not grow weary but you will grow weary. The religious act most pleasing to Him is  one the doer of which does it continuously. (And in the Hadith transmitted by Abu  Usama [the words are]: “She was a woman from Banu Asad.”)
