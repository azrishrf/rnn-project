19|4315|  Chapter: Desirability of praying for victory at the time of confrontation with the enemy.  It is narrated on the authority of Ibn Abu Aufa that the Messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) cursed the tribes (who had marched upon Medina with a combined force  in 5 H) and said: O Allah, Revealer of the Book, swift in (taking) account, put the tribes  to rout. O Lord, defeat them and shake them.
