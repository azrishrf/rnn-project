30|5779|  Chapter: Pertaining to the old age of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).  Ibn Sirin reported: Anas b. Malik was asked whether Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) dyed his hair. He said: He had not become old enough to have white hair. Ibn  Idris said that he had a few white hair. Abu Bakr and Umar, however, dyed hair with  hina’ (henna).         (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
