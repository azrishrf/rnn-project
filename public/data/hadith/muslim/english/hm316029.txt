31|6029|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud and that of his mother (Allah be pleased with them).  Anas is reported to have said: Four persons collected the Qur’an during the lifetime of  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and all of them were Ansar: Mu’adh b.  Jabal, Ubayy b. Ka’b, Zaid b. Thabit, Abu Zaid. Qatada said: Anas, who was Abu Zaid?  He said: He was one of my uncles.             HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
