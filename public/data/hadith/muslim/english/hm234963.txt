23|4963|  Chapter: Every intoxicant is Khamr and every Khamr is forbidden.  Ibn ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Every  intoxicant is Khamr and every intoxicant is forbidden. He who drinks wine in this world  and dies while he is addicted to it, not having repented, will not be given a drink in the  Hereafter.
