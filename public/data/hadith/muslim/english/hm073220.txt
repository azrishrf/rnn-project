7|3220|  Chapter: Do not undertake journey (purely for visit to the sacred places) but to three  mosques.  Abu Haraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Messenger (way peace be  upon him) as saying: One should undertake journey to three mosques: the mosque of  the Ka’ba, my mosque, and the mosque of Elia (Bait al-Maqdis).
