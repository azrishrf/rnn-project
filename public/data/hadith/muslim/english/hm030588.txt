3|588|  Chapter: The menstruating woman is permitted to wash the head of her husband, comb  his hair, and her left-over is clean, and one is permitted to recline in her lap and recite  the Qur’an.  ‘Aisha reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) ordered me that I  should get him the mat from the mosque. I said: I am menstruating. He (the Holy  Prophet) said: Do get me that, for menstruation is not in your hand.
