9|3542|  Chapter: It is obligatory to abstain from adornment during the ‘Idda period, but  mourning beyond three days is prohibited.  Humaid b. Nafi’ narrated two traditions from Umm Salama dealing with collyrium and  the other hadith from the wives of Allah’s Prophet (may peace be upon him) except with  this that no mention was made of Zainab.
