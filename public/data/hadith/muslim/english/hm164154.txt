16|4154|  Chapter: When it is permissible to take the life of a Muslim.  ‘Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) stood up  and said: By Him besides Whom there is no god but He, the blood of a Muslim who  bears the testimony that there is no god but Allah, and I am His Messenger, may be  lawfully shed only in case of three persons: the one who abandons Islam, and deserts  the community [Ahmad, one of the narrators, is doubtful whether the Holy Prophet (may  peace be upon him) used the word li’l-jama’ah or al-jama’ah), and the married adulterer,  and life for life.
