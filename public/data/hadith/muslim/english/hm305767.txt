30|5767|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) perspired when revelation descended upon  him.  ‘Ubida b. Samit reported that when wahi descended upon Allah’s Apostle (may peace  be upon him), he lowered his head and so lowered his Companions their heads, and  when (this state) was over, he raised his head.
