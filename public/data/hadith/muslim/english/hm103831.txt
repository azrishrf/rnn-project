10|3831|  Chapter: Conversion of currency and selling of gold for silver on the spot.  Rumaid reported that Anas b. Malik (Allah be pleased with him) has asked about the  earnings of a cupper. Then (the above-mentioned hadith was reported but with this  addition) that he said: The best treatment which you get is cupping or aloeswood and do  not torture your children by pressing their uvula.
