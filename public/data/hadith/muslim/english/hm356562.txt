35|6562|  Chapter: Refuge from the evil of what one has done, and what one has not done.  Abu Huraira reported that when Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) set out on  a journey in the morning, he used to say: ” A listener listened to our praising Allah (for)  His goodly trial of us. Our Lord! accompany us, guard us and bestow upon us Thy  grace. I am seeker of refuge in Allah from the Fire.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
