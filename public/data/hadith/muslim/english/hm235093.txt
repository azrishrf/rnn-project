23|5093|  Chapter: The merit of vinegar as a condiment.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah reported that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) asked his  family for condiment. They (the members of his household) said: We have nothing with  us but vinegar. He asked for it, he began to eat it, and then said: Vinegar is a good  condiment, vinegar is a good condiment.
