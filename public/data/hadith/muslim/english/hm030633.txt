3|633|  Chapter: The quantity of water that is desirable for a bath because of sexual  intercourse, bathing of the male and female with one vessel in the same condition and  washing of one of them with the left-over of the other.  Zainab bint Umm Salama (the wife of the Holy Prophet) reported that Umm Salama and  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) took a bath from the same vessel.
