8|3256|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of ‘Abd al-‘Aziz b ‘Umar with the same  chain of transmitters, and he said: I saw Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon him)  standing between the pillar and the gate (of the Ka’ba) and he was relating a hadith as  narrated by Ibn Numair.
