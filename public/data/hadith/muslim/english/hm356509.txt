35|6509|  Chapter: The merit of pronouncing Tablil, Tasbib and supplication.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: He who  recites in the morning and in the evening (these words): “Hallowed be Allah and all  praise is due to Him” one hundred times, he would not bring on the Day of Resurrection  anything excellent than this except one who utters these words or utters more than  these words.
