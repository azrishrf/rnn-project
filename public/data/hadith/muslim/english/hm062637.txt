6|2637|  Chapter: Observing of I’tikaf in the last ten days of Ramadhan.  Abdullah b. Umar (Allah be pleased with both of them) reported that the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) used to observe i’tikaf in the last ten days of Ramadan.  Nafi’ said: Abdullah (Allah be pleased with him) showed me the place in the mosque  where the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) used to observe i’tikaf.
