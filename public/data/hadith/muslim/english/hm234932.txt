23|4932|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  Sa’id b. Jubair reported: I bear testimony to the fact that Ibn ‘Umar and Ibn ‘Abbas  testified to the fact that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) forbade (the  preparation) of Nabidh in gourd in vessel besmeared with pitch and hollow stump.
