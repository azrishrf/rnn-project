7|2861|  Chapter: Permissibility of Umra during the mouth of Hajj.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) came along with his Companions when four days had passed out of ten days  (of Dhu’l-Hijja) and they were pronouncing Talbiya for Hajj, and he (the Holy Prophet)  commanded them to change (this Ihram) into that of ‘Umra.
