26|5517|  Chapter: Omen, divinations and that which is unlucky.  Abu Huraira reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  There is no divination but the best type is the good omen. It was said to Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him): What is good omen? Thereupon he said: A good  word which one of you hears.
