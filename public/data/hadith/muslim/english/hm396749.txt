39|6749|  Chapter: The believer is likened to the date-palm tree.  Mujahid said: (I have had the privilege) of accompanying Ibn ‘Umar up to Medina but I  did not hear him narrate anything from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  except one hadith. And he said: We were in the presence of Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) that there was brought to him the kernel of a date. The rest of the  hadith is the same.  PARADISE AND HELL
