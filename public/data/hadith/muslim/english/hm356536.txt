35|6536|  Chapter: Supplication for the safeguard of one’s self against the evil of the turmoil.  Anas b. Malik reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) used to say:  “O Allah, I seek refuge in Thee from incapacity, from indolence, from cowardice, from  senility, from miserliness, and I seek refuge in Thee from the torment of the grave and  from trial of the life and death.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
