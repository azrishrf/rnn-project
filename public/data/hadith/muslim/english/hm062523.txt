6|2523|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  Abu Musa reported that the people of Khaibar (most of them were Jews) observed fast  on the day of ‘Ashura and they treated it as ‘Id and gave their women ornaments and  beautiful dress to wear. The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: You  (only) observe fast on this day.
