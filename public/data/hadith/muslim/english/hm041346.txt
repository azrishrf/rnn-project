4|1346|  Chapter: Time for the night prayer and its delay.  ‘Aisha, the wife of the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him), reported: The  believing women observed the morning prayer with the Messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) wrapped in their mantles. They then went back to their houses and were  unrecognisable, because of the Messenger of Allah’s (may peace be upon him) praying  in the darkness before dawn.
