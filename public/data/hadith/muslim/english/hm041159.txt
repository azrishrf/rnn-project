4|1159|  Chapter: It is forbidden to cry out for finding out the lost thing in the mosque.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: When  any one of you stands up to pray, the devil comes to him and confuses him to that he  does not know how much he has prayed. If any one of you h” such an experience he  should perform two prostrations while sitting down (in qa’da).
