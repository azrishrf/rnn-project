4|1761|  Chapter: Excellence of the recitation of the Qur’an and that of surat al-Baqara.  ‘Abd al-Rahman b. Yazid reported: I met Abu Mas’ud near the House (Ka’ba) and said  to him: A Hadith has been conveyed to me on your authority about the two (concluding  verses of Surah al-Baqara. He said: Yes. The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) (in fact) said: Anyone who recites the two verses at the end of Surah al-Baqara at  night, they would suffice for him.
