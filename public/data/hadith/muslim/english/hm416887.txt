41|6887|  Chapter: The sinking of an army in the earth which would attack the House.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of ‘Abdullah b. Rufai, with the same  chain of transmitters (but with the addition of these words): “When I met Abu Ja’far I told  him that she (simply) meant the plain ground. Thereupon Abu Ja’far said: No, by God,  she meant the plain ground of Medina.
