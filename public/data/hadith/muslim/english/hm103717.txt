10|3717|  Chapter: Leasing out land.  Jabir b. Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported some of the Companions of  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had surplus of land. Thereupon Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: He, who has surplus land (in his  possession) should cultivate it, or he should lend it to his brother for benefit, but if he  refuses to accept it, he should retain it.
