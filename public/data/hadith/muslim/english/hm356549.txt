35|6549|  Chapter: Supplication at the time of sleeping and going to bed.  Al-Bara’ reported that whenever Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) went to  bed, he said: “O Allah, it is with Thine Name that I live and it is with Thine Name that I  die.” And when he got up he used to say: “Praise is due to Allah, Who gave us life after  our death (sleep) and unto Thee is resurrection.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
