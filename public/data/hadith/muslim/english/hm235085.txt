23|5085|  Chapter: Excellence of truffles and their use as a medicine for the eyes.  Sa’id b. Zaid reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Truffles are a kind of blessing and their juice is a medicine for the eyes. Shu’ba said:  When Hakam narrated this hadith to me, I did not deem it as a Munkar hadith because  of the narration of Abd al-Malik.
