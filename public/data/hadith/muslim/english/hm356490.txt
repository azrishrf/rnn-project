35|6490|  Chapter: He who loves to meet Allah, Allah also loves to meet him, and he who dislikes to meet  Allah. Allah also abhors to meet him.  ‘Aisha reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: He who loves to  meet Allah, Allah also loves to meet him, and who dislikes to meet Allah, Allah abhors to  meet him. There is death before (one is able to) meet Allah.  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
