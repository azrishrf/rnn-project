4|963|  Chapter: Moderation in the articles of prayers and their shortening and perfection.  (‘Abdullah b) Ibn Abi Aufa reported: When the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) raised his back from the ruku’ he pronounced: Allah listened to him who praised  Him. O Allah ! our Lord ! unto Thee be praise that would fill the heavens and the earth  and fill that which will please Thee besides them.
