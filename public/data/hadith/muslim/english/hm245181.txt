24|5181|  Chapter: Modesty in case of garments and preference for the coarse cloth for wearing  and the permissibility of wearing cloth made of (camel’s) hair.  Abu Burda reported: I visited ‘Aisha and she brought out for us the coarse lower  garfnent (of Allah’s Messenger) made in Yemen and clothes made out of Mulabbada  cloth, and she swore in the name of Allah that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) died in these two clothes.
