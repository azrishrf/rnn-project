41|6984|  Chapter: The Last Hour would not come until a person would pass by a grave and wish that he  should have been the occupant of that grave because of this calamity.  Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: The  Jews will fight against you and you will gain victory over them until the stone would say:  Muslim, here is a Jew behind me; kill him.
