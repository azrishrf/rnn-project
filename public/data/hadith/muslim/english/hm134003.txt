13|4003|  Chapter: The deceased is entitled to reward for the sadaqa given after his death.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that a man came to Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) and said: Allah’s Messenger, my mother died all of a sudden  without making any will. I think if (she could have the opportunity) to speak she would  have made a Sadaqa. Would there be any reward for her if I give charity on her behalf?  He (the Holy Prophet) said: Yes.
