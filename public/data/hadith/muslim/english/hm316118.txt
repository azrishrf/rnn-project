31|6118|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked blessings upon the tribes of Ghifar  and Aslam.  Ibn ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Allah has  granted pardon to the tribe of Ghifar and to the tribe of Aslam Allah has granted safety  and as for Usayya tribe, they disobeyed Allah and His Messenger.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
