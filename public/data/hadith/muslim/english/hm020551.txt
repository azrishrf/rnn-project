2|551|  Chapter: Instructions pertaining to the licking of a dog.  Ibn Mughaffal reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) ordered  killing of the dogs, and then said: What about them, i.e. about other dogs? and then  granted concession (to keep) the dog for hunting and the dog for (the security) of the  herd, and said: When the dog licks the utensil, wash it seven times, and rub it with earth  the eighth time.
