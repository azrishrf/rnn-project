31|5981|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her).  ‘Aisha reported that she used to play with dolls in the presence of Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) and when her playmates came to her they left (the house)  because they felt shy of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him), whereas Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) sent them to her.
