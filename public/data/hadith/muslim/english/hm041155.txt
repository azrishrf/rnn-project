4|1155|  Chapter: Forbiddance to eat garlic, onions and anything of offensive smell while coming  to the mosque.  Sulaiman b. Buraida narrated it on the authority of his father that a man cried out in the  mosque saying: Who had called out for the red camel? Upon this the Apostle of Allah  (may peace be upon him) said: May it not be restored to you! The mosques are built for  what they are meant.
