20|4695|  Chapter: Desirability of seeking martyrdom.  It has been reported on the authority of Sahl b. Aba Umama b. Sahl b. Hunaif who  learned the tradition from his father who (in turn) learned it from his grandfather-that the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Who sought martyrdom with  sincerity will be ranked by Allah among the martyrs even if he died on his bed. In his  version of the tradition Abd Tahir did not mention the words: “with sincerity”.
