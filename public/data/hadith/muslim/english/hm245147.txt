24|5147|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Ibn Umar through another chain of  transmitters but with a slight variation of wording (and the words are that the Holy  Prophet) said: I sent it to you so that you might derive benefit from it, but I did not send it  to you to wear it.
