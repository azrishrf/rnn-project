30|5799|  Chapter: How long did the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) stay in Medina and  Mecca.  ‘Amr reported: I said to ‘Urwa: How long did Allah’s Apostle -(may peace be upon him)  stay in Mecca? He said: For ten years. I said: Ibn ‘Abbas says (that he stayed in Mecca)  for thirteen years.           (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
