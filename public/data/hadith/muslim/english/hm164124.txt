16|4124|  Chapter: Al-Qasamah.  Bushair b. Yasar reported that a person from the Ansar belonging to the tribe of Banu  Haritha who was called ‘Abdullah b. Sahl b. Zaid set out and the son of his uncle called  Muhayyisa b. Mas’ud b. Zaid, the rest of the hadith is the same up to the words: “Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) paid the blood-wit himself.” Bushair b. Yasar  reported that Sahl b. Abu Hathma said: One camel amongst the camels paid as blood- wit kicked me while I was in the (camel) enclosure.  MURDERS, FIGHTING, REQUITAL
