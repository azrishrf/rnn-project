7|3195|  Chapter: Allah will punish those who intend to do any harm to the citizens of Medina.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: He who  intends to do harm to its people (he meant Medina), Allah would efface him as salt is  dissolved in water. Ibn Hatim (one of the narrators) substituted the word “harm” for  “mischief”.
