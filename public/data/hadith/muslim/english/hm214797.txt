21|4797|  Chapter: Pertaining to the flesh of the Lizard.  Abu Zubair reported that he heard Jabir b. ‘Abdullah saying that there was presented to  Allah’s Messenger (the flesh) of the lizard, but he refused to eat that, saying: I do not  know; perhaps it (lizard) might (be one of those natives of) the distant past whose  (forms) had beer, distorted.
