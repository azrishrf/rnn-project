24|5234|  Chapter: Command concerning the wrapping up of a garment completely leaving no  opening for the arms and sitting with single garment wrapped around one’s knees.  Jabir reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) forbade that a man  should eat with the left hand or walk with one sandal or wrap himself completely leaving  no opening for the arms (to draw out) or support himself when sitting with a single  garment wrapped round his knees which may expose his private parts.
