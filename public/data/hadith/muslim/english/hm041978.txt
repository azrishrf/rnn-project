4|1978|  Chapter: Mention of the torment of grave in eclipse prayer.  Asma’ said: I came to ‘Aisha when the people were standing (in prayer) and she was  also praying. I said: What is this excitement of the people for? And the rest of the Hadith  was narrated like one, (narrated above). ‘Urwa said: Do not say Kasafat-ush-Shamsu,  but say Khasafat-ush-Shamsu.
