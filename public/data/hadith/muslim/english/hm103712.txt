10|3712|  Chapter: Forbiddance of al-Mubiqala, and al-Muzabana, and al-Mukhabara and the sale of  fruits before their good condition is clear, and al-Mu’awama, i.e., the sale for some years.         Jabir b. Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) forbidding Muhaqala and Muzabana and Mu’awama and Mukhabara.  (One of the narrators)’said: Sale years ahead is Mu’awama, and making exceptional but  he made an exemption of arraya.
