10|3885|  Chapter: Of salaam sale.  Nu’man b. Bashir b. Sa’d, a Companion of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  was heard delivering a sermon at Hims and was saying: I heard Allah’s Messenger (way  peace be upon him) as saying: The lawful is evident and the unlawful is evident, the rest  of the hadith is the same as related by Zakariya.
