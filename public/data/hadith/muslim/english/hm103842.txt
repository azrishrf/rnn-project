10|3842|  Chapter: The sale of gold necklace studded with pearls.  Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported: This news reached ‘Umar that Samura  had sold wine, whereupon he said: May Allah destroy Samura; does he not know that         Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said:” Let there be the curse of Allah upon  the Jews that fat was declared forbidden for them, but they melted it and then sold it”?
