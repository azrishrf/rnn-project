4|1897|  Chapter: Shortness of prayer and surmon.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah reported that while Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) was  delivering the sermon on Friday a person came there, and the Apostle of Allah (may  peace be upon him) said to him: So and so, have you prayed (two rak’ahs)? He said:  No. He (the Holy Prophet) said: Then stand and pray.
