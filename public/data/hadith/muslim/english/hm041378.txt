4|1378|  Chapter: He who hears the call for prayer it to essential for him to come to the mosque.  Abu Sha’tha’ al-Muharibi reported on the authority of his father, who said: I heard it from  Abu Huraira that he saw a person getting out of the mosque after the call to prayer had  been announced. Upon this he remarked: This (man) disobeyed Abu’l-Qasim (may  peace be upon him).
