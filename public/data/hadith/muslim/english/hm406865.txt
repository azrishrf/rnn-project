40|6865|  Chapter: The dead would be shown his seat in Paradise and hell, and the affirmation of the  torment of the grave and seeking refuge from it.  Al-Bara’ b. ‘Azib reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: This  verse: “Allah grants steadfastness to those who believe with firm word” was revealed in  connection with the torment of the grave. It would be said to him: Who is your Lord?  And he would say: Allah is my Lord and Muhammad is my Apostle (may peace be upon  him), and that is (what is implied) by the words of Allah, the Exalted: “Allah keeps  steadfast those who believe with firm word in this world and in the Hereafter.”  INTIMATES
