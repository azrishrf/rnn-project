26|5508|  Chapter: There is no infection, no evil omen, no Hama, no safar, no star promising rain,  no ghoul and the ill should not go to those who are healthy.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: There is  no transitive disease, no evil omen, no safar, no hama. A desert Arab said: Allah’s  Messenger... The rest of the hadith is the same.
