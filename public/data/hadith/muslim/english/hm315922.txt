31|5922|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Ali b. Abi Talib (Allah be pleased with him).  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of Abu Hayyan but with this addition:  “The Book of Allah contains right guidance, the light, and whoever adheres to it and  holds it fast, he is upon right guidance and whosoever deviates from it goes astray.         HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
