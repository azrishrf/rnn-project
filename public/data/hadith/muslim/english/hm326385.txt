32|6385|  Chapter: A person would be along with him whom he loves.  ‘Abdullah reported that a person came to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  and said to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him): What is your opinion about  the person who loves the people but his (acts or deeds are not identical to theirs)?  Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: A person would be along  with one whom he loves.  OF RELATIONSHIP
