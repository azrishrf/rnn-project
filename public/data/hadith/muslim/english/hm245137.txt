24|5137|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.  Shu’ba reported from al-Hakam that he heard ‘Abd al-Rahmin (i.e. Ibn Abu Laila) as  saying: I personally saw Hudhaifa asking for water in Mada’in and a man giving it to him  in a silver vessel. The rest of the hadith is the same.
