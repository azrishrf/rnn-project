42|7061|  Chapter: Not known.         Mutarrif reported on the authority of his father: I came to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) as he was reciting: “Abundance diverts you” (cii. 1). He said: The son of  Adam claims: My wealth, my wealth. And he (the Holy Prophet) said: O son of Adam. is  there anything as your belonging except that which you consumed, which you utilised,  or which you wore and then it was worn out or you gave as charity and sent it forward?
