30|5698|  Chapter: The cistern of our Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its characteristics.  A hadith like this has been transmitted on the authority, of ‘Ubaidullah with this addition:  Ubaidullah was asked (about these two names, i.e. Jarba’ and Adhruh). He said: These  are the two towns of Syria and there is between them the distance which can be  covered in three nights, and the hadith transmitted on the authority of Ibn Bishr (the  words are). “Three days.”  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
