4|982|  Chapter: Following the Imam and acting after him.  ‘Aisha reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) before his death  recited often: Hallowed be Thou, and with Thy praise, I seek forgiveness from Thee and  return to Thee. She reported: I said: Messenger of Allah, what are these words that I  find you reciting? He said: There has been made a sign for me in my Ummah; when I  saw that, I uttered them (these words of glorification for Allah), and the sign is: “When  Allah’s help and victory... to the end of the surah.
