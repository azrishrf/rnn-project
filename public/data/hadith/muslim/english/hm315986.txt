31|5986|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her).  ‘Aisha reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) at the time of  breathing his last was reclining against her chest and she was leaning over him and  listening to him as he was saying: O Allah, grant me pardon, show mercy to me, enjoin  me to companions (on High).  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
