4|1763|  Chapter: Excellence of the recitation of the Qur’an and that of surat al-Baqara.  Abu Mas’ud reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: If anyone  recites the two verses at the end of Surah al-Baqara at night, they would suffice for him  ‘Abd al-Rahman said: I met Abu Mas’ud and he was circumambulating the House (of  Allah) and asked him about this (tradition) and he narrated it to me from the Apostle of  Allah (may peace be upon him).
