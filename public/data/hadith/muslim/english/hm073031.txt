7|3031|  Chapter: It is permissible to join seven persons in a cow or a camel.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) sacrificed (animals) on behalf of his wives, and in the Hadith  transmitted by Ibn Abu Bakr (the words are): “A cow on behalf of ‘Aisha on the occasion  of the Hajj.”
