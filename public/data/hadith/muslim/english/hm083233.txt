Masâ€™ud) (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger  (May peace be upon him) said to us: O young men, those among you who can support  a wife should marry, for it restrains eyes (from casting evil glances) and preserves one  from immorality; but he who cannot afford It should observe fast for it is a means of  controlling the sexual desire.
