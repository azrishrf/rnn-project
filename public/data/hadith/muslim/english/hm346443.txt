34|6443|  Chapter: Pertaining to a disputationist.  ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported: I went to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) in  the morning and he heard the voice of two persons who had an argumentation with  each other about a verse. Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) came to us (and)  the (signs) of anger could be seen on his face. He said: Verily, the (peoples) before you  were ruined because of their disputation in the Book.
