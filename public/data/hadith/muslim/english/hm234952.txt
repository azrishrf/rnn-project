23|4952|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  ‘Abdullah b. Buraida, on the authority of his father, reported Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) as saying: I had forbidden you from the preparation of Nabidh  except in a water-skin. But now you may drink in all vessels, but do not drink what is  intoxicant.
