26|5491|  Chapter: Talbina gives comfort to the patient.  ‘Aisha the wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said: When there was any  bereavement in her family the women gathered there for condolence and they departed  except the members of the family and some selected persons. She asked to prepare  talbina in a small cauldron and it was cooked and then tharid was prepared and it was  poured over talbina, then she said: Eat it, for I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: Talbina gives comfort to the aggrieved heart and it lessens grief.
