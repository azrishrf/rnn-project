35|6582|  Chapter: Supplication during the time of trouble.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) used to supplicate  during the time of trouble (in these words): “There is no god but Allah, the Great, the  Tolerant, there is no god but Allah, the Lord of the Magnificent Throne There is no god  but Allah, the Lord of the Heaven and the earth, the Lord of the Edifying Throne.”         REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
