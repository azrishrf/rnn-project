33|6441|  Chapter: Affairs pertaining to strength, and shedding off weakness, and seeking help from Allah  and trust in the measures set by Allah.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: A strong  believer is better and is more lovable to Allah than a weak believer, and there is good in  everyone, (but) cherish that which gives you benefit (in the Hereafter) and seek help  from Allah and do not lose heart, and if anything (in the form of trouble) comes to you,  don’t say: If I had not done that, it would not have happened so and so, but say: Allah  did that what He had ordained to do and your “if” opens the (gate) for the Satan.
