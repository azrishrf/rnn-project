31|6080|  Chapter: The merits of Hassan b. Thabit (Allah be pleased with him).  ‘Urwa reported on the same chain of transmitters that Hassan b. Thabit sought  permission from Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) to satirise against the  polytheists, but he did not mention Abu Sufyan. And instead of the word al-Khamir, the  word al-‘Ajin was used.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
