3|581|  Chapter: Lying with one in menstruation above the waist wrapper.  Umm Salama reported: While I was lying with the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) in a bed cover I menstruated, so I slipped away and I took up the clothes  (which I wore) in menses.
