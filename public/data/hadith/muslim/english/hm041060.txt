4|1060|  Chapter: Prayer in a single garment.         Hudhaifa reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: We have  been made to excel (other) people in three (things): Our rows have been made like the  rows of the angels and the whole earth has been made a mosque for us, and its dust  has been made a purifier for us in case water is not available.
