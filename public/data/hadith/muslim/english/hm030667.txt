3|667|  Chapter: It Is forbidden to see the private parts of someone else.  ‘Abd al-Rahman, the son of Abu Sa’id al-Khudri, reported from his father: The  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: A man should not see the private  parts of another man, and a woman should not see the private parts of another woman,  and a man should not lie with another man under one covering, and a woman should  not lie with another woman under one covering.
