8|3394|  Chapter: Permissibility of intercourse with a suckling lady and disapproval of ‘Azl.  Sa’d b. Abu Waqqas (Allah be pleased with him) reported that a person came to Allah’s  Messenger (May peace be upon him) and said: I do ‘azi with my wife. Thereupon Allah’s  Messenger (May peace be upon him) said: Why do you do that? The person said: I fear  harm to her child or her children. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon  him) said: If that were harmful it would harm the Persians and the Greeks.
