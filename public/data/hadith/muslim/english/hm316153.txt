31|6153|  Chapter: The merits of the companions of the Holy Prophet (may peace be upon him) and of  those who are next to them and then of those who are next to them.  ‘Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: The  best among people are of my generation, then those next to them. (The narrator said): I  do not know whether (he said) it three times or four times. Then there would fellow after  them such persons whose evidence would precede the oath, and in case of some  others, the oath (would precede) the evidence.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
