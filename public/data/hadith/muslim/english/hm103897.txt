10|3897|  Chapter: Prohibition of taking oath in business transactions.  Abu Rafi’, the freed slave of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him), said: Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) took as a loan (the rest of the hadith is the same),  but with this variation that he (the Holy Prophet) said: Good amongst the servants of  Allah is he who is best in paying off the debt.
