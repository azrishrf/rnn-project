3|625|  Chapter: The quantity of water that is desirable for a bath because of sexual  intercourse, bathing of the male and female with one vessel in the same condition and  washing of one of them with the left-over of the other.  ‘Aisha reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) took a bath from the  vessel (which contained seven to eight seers, i.e. fifteen to sixteen pounds) of water  And I and he (the Holy Prophet) took a bath from the same vessel. And in the Hadith  narrated by Sufyan the words are: “from one vessel”. Qutaiba said: Al-Faraq is three Sa’  (a cubic measuring of varying magnitude).
