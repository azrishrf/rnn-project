16|4135|  Chapter: Pertaining to the combatants and apostates.  Anas reported: There came to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) some  people from ‘Uraina. They embraced Islam and swore allegiance to him and there had  spread at that time pleurisy. The rest of the hadith is the same (but with this addition):  “There were by his (the Prophet’s) side about twenty young men of the Ansar; he sent         them (behind) them (culprits), and he also sent along with them one expert in following  the track so that he might trace their footprints.”  MURDERS, FIGHTING, REQUITAL
