26|5535|  Chapter: Kahanat (Divination) is prohibited and it is forbidden to go to Kahin.  ‘Aisha reported: I said: Allah’s Messenger, the kahins used to tell us about things  (unseen) and we found them to be true. Thereupon he said: That is a word pertaining to  truth which a jinn snatches and throws into the ear of his friend, and makes an addition  of one hundred lies to it.
