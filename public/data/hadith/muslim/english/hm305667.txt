30|5667|  Chapter: The reliance of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) on Allah the Exalted, and  Allah’s protecting him against the people.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah reported: We went along with Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and  as we reached the place Dhat-ur-Riqa’; the rest of the hadith is the same, but there is no mention  of the word that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) did not harm him.
