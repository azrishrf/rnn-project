32|6289|  Chapter: He upon whom Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked curse  whereas he in fact did not deserve it, it would be a source of reward and mercy for him.  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of A’mash and in the hadith  transmitted on the authority of ‘Isa the words are: Make it a source of reward, and in the  hadith transmitted on the authority of Abu Huraira (the words are): “Make it a source of  mercy.”  OF RELATIONSHIP
