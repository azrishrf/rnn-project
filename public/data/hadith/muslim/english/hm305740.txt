30|5740|  Chapter: The utmost modesty of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).  Masruq reported: We went to Abdullah b. ‘Amr when Mu’dwiya came to Kufa, and he  made a mention of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and said: He was  never immoderate in his talk and he never reviled others. Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) also said: The best amongst you are those who are best in morals.  Uthman said: When he came to Kufa along with Mu’awiya... (The rest of the hadith is  the same).  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
