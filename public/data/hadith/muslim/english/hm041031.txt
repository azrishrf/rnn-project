4|1031|  Chapter: The excellence of the prayer and the way it is begun and the excellence of  Ruku’ and moderation in it and prostration and moderation in it, etc.  Yazid reported: Salama sought to say prayer near the pillar which was by that place  where copies of the Qur’an were kept. I said to him: Abu Muslim. I see you striving to  offer your prayer by this pillar. He said: I saw the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) seeking to pray by its side.
