7|3063|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: We feared that Safiyyah might have entered  the period of menses before performing Tawaf Ifada. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) came to us and said: Is Safiyyah going to detain us? Thereupon we said: She  has performed Tawaf Ifada. He (the Holy Prophet) said: Then there is no detention (for  us) now.
