7|3177|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Sahl b. Hunif reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) pointed with his  hands towards Medina and said: That is a sacred territory and a place of safety.
