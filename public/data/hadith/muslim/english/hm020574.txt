2|574|  Chapter: The Impurity of the blood of menses and its washing.  This tradition is narrated by Abu Kuraib, Ibn Numair, Abu Tahir, Ibn Wahb, Yahya b.  ‘Abdullah b. Salim, Malik b. Anas, ‘Amr b. Harith on the authority of Hisham b. ‘Urwa,  with the same chain of transmitters like one transmitted by Yahya b. Sa’id like the  above-mentioned.
