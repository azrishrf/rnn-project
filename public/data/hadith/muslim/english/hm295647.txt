29|5647|  Chapter: The vision of the Holy Prophet (may peace be upon him).  Anas b. Malik reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: I saw  during the night that which a person sees during the sleep as if we are in the house of  ‘Uqba b. Rafi’ that there was brought to us the fresh dates of Ibn Tab. I interpreted it as  the sublimity for us in the world and good ending in the Hereafter and that our religion is  good.
