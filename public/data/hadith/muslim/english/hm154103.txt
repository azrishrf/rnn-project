15|4103|  Chapter: He who emancipates his share in the slave.  Ibn ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: He who  emancipates his share in the slave, it is his responsibility to secure full freedom for him  provided he (the slave) has enough money to pay the (remaining) price, but it he has  not so much money he would be emancipated to the extent that the first man  emancipated.
