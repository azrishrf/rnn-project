10|3777|  Chapter: Prohibition of the sale of excess water in the barren lands, and preventing people to  use it, and hiring a camel to cover a she-camel.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported that in the time of Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) a man suffered loss in fruits he had bought and  his debt increased; so Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) told (the people) to  give him charity and they gave him charity, but that was not enough to pay the debt in  full, whereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to his creditors:” Take  what you find, you will have nothing but alms.
