36|6604|  Chapter: The majority in paradise would consist of the poor, pious persons and the majority of  the denizens of hell would consist of women, and the trial by means of women.         Usama b. Zaid b. Harith and Sa’id b. Zaid b. ‘Amr b. Naufal both reported Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: I have not left after me turmoil for the  people but the harm done to men by women.
