Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said that Allah, the  Exalted and Glorious, said: I have prepared for My pious servants which the eye has  seen not, and the ear has heard not and no human heart has ever perceived such  bounties leaving aside those about which Allah has informed you. He then recited: “No  soul knows what comfort has been hidden for them”.  INTIMATES
