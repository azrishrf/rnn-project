40|6861|  Chapter: The dead would be shown his seat in Paradise and hell, and the affirmation of the  torment of the grave and seeking refuge from it.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Abu Ayyub through some other chains  of transmitters (and the words are): “Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) went  out after the sun had set and he heard some sound and said: It is the Jews who are  being tormented in their graves.
