7|2648|  Chapter: What is permitted for the Muhrim (wearer of Ihram) in Hajj and Umra, what is  not permissible and forbiddance of the use of perfume.  Salim reported on the authority of his father (‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar) that the Apostle of  Allah (may peace be upon him) was asked what a Muhrim should wear, whereupon he  said: A Muhrim should not wear a shirt, or a turban, or a cap, or trousers, or a cloth  touched with wars or with saffron, nor (should he wear) stockings, but in case he does  not find shoes, but (before wearing stockings) be should trim them (in such a way) that  these should become lower than the ankles.
