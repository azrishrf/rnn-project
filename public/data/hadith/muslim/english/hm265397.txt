26|5397|  Chapter: Permissibility of women going out in the fields for easing themselves.  ‘Aisha reported that the wives of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) used to  go out in the cover of night when they went to open fields (in the outskirts of Medina) for  easing themselves. ‘Umar b Khattab used to say: Allah’s Messenger, ask your ladies to  observe veil, but Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) did not do that. So there  went out Sauda, daughter of Zarn’a, the wife of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him), during one of the nights when it was dark. She was a tall statured lady. ‘Umar  called her saying: Sauda, we recognise you. (He did this with the hope that the verses  pertaining to veil would be revealed). ‘Aisha said: Allah, the Exalted and Glorious, then  revealed the verses pertaining to veil.
