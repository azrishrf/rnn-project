2|537|  Chapter: Time limit for wiping over the shoes.  Shuraih b. Hani said: I came to ‘Aisha to ask her about wiping over the socks. She said:  You better ask (‘Ali) son of Abu Talib for he used to travel with Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him). We asked him and he said: The Messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) stipulated (the upper limit) of three days and three nights for a traveller  and one day and one night for the resident.
