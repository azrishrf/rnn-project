1|321|  Chapter: Night journey of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) to Heaven  and the prayer made obligatory.  It is narrated on the authority of Jabir that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) said: There appeared before me the apostles, and Moses was among men as if he  was one of the people of Shanu’a, and I saw Jesus son of Mary (peace be upon him)  and I saw nearest in resemblance with him was ‘Urwa b. Mas’ud, and I saw Ibrahim  (blessings of Allah be upon him) and I see your companions much in resemblance with  him, i.e. his personality, and I saw Gabriel (peace be upon him) and I saw Dihya nearest  in resemblance to him; but in the narration of Ibn Rumh it is Dihya b. Khalifa.
