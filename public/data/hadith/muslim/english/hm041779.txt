4|1779|  Chapter: Excellence of the recitation of the two suras concerning the taking of refuge.  ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  There should be no envy but only in case of two persons: one having been endowed  with wealth and power to spend it in the cause of Truth, and (the other) who has been  endowed with wisdom and he decides cases with the help of it and teaches it (to  others).
