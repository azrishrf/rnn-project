10|3863|  Chapter: The selling of the camel and stipulation of riding on it.  Fadala b. Ubaid al-Ansari reported: A necklace having gold and gems in it was brought  to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) in Khaibar and it was one of the spoils  of war and was put to sale. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: The gold  used in it should be separated, and then Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  further said: (Sell) gold for gold with equal weight.
