4|1037|  Chapter: The excellence of the prayer and the way it is begun and the excellence of  Ruku’ and moderation in it and prostration and moderation in it, etc.  ‘Urwa b. Zubair reported: ‘Aisha asked: What disrupts the prayer? We said: The woman  and the ass. Upon this she remarked: Is the woman an ugly animal? I lay in front of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) like the bier of a corpse and he said  prayer.
