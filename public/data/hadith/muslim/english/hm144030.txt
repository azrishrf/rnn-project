14|4030|  Chapter: He who took the vow that he would go on foot to the Ka’ba.         Abu Huraira reported: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) found an old man  walking between his two sons supported by them, whereupon Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) said: What is the matter with him? He (the narrator) said: Allah’s  Messenger, they are his sons and there is upon him the (fulfilment) of the vow,  whereupon Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said: Ride, old man, for Allah is  not in need of you and your vow.
