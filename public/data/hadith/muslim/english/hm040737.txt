4|737|  Chapter: Lying with one in menstruation above the waist wrapper.  Anas b. Malik reported: They (the Companions) discussed that they should know the  timings of prayer by means of something recognized by all. Some of them said that fire  should be lighted or a bell should be rung. But Bilal was ordered to repeat the phrases  twice in Adhan, and once in Iqama.
