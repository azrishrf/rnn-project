20|4711|  Chapter: The merit of archery and inducing others to learn it and denunciation of one  who learnt the art and then neglected it.  It has been narrated on the authority of Ibn Amir who said: I heard the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) say-and he was delivering a sermon from the pulpit:  Prepare to meet them with as much strength as you can afford. Beware, strength  consists in archery. Beware, strength consists in archery. Beware, strength consists in  archery.
