4|1587|  Chapter: The virtue of Sunan prayers before and after the Fard Rak’ahs and their number.  ‘Abdullah b. Shaqiq al-‘Uqaili reported: I asked ‘Aisha about the prayer of the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) during the night (i.e. Tahajjud prayer) She  replied: He used to pray for a long time standing and for a long time sitting in the night,  and when he recited the Qur’an while standing, he would bow himself from the standing  position, and when he recited while sitting, he would bow from the sitting position.
