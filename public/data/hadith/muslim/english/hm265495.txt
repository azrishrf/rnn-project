26|5495|  Chapter: Plague, ill omen and magic.  Usama reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Plague is a  calamity which was inflicted on those who were before you, or upon Bani Isra’il. So  when it has broken out in a land, don’t run out of it, and when it has spread in a land,  then don’t enter it.
