4|1634|  Chapter: Forenoon prayer should be offered when it is sufficiently hot.  ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported: A man stood up and said: Messenger of Allah, how is the  night prayer? The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: The night prayer  consists of pair, but if you apprehend the rise of dawn, make it odd number by  observing one rak’ah.
