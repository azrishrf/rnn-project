4|1092|  Chapter: The virtue of building the mosques and exhortation to it.  Mus’ab b. Sa’d b. Abu Waqqas reported: I said prayer by the side of my father. When I  bowed I intertwined my fingers and placed them between my knees. He struck my  hands. When he completed the prayer he said: We used to do that but then were  commanded to lift (our palms) to the knees.
