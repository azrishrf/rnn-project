1|56|  Chapter: Concerning the branches of Iman.  It is narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may peace  and blessings be upon him) said: Faith has over seventy branches or over sixty  branches, the most excellent of which is the declaration that there is no god but Allah,  and the humblest of which is the, removal of what is injurious from the path: and  modesty is the branch of faith.
