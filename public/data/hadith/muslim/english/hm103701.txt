10|3701|  Chapter: Pertaining to one who sells date-palm tree with dates hanging on Its  branches.  Nafi reported on the authority of Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) that the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Whichever tree is bought with its  roots, and if it is fecundated its fruit would belong to one who has grafted it except when  the provision is laid down by the buyer.
