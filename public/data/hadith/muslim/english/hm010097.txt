1|97|  Chapter: Concerning the fact that no one will enter Paradise except believers, that love  for believers is (a characteristic (feature of) Faith and giving currency to (the practice of  paying salutation by, saying) As-Salimu ‘Alaikum (peace be upon you) is the means to  achieve it (Faith).  Zuhair b. Harb said: Jarir reported on the authority of A’mash with this chain of  transmitters that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed: By him in  Whose hand is my life, you shall not enter Paradise unless you believe. The rest of the  hadith is the same as narrated by Abd Mu’awiya and Waki’.
