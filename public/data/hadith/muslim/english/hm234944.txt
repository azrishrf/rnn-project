23|4944|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  Jabalah reported: I heard Ibn ‘Umar narrating that Allah’s messenger (may peace be  upon him) had forbidden (the preparation of Nabidh) in the pitcher besmeared with  pitch. I said to him: What is Huntama? He said: It is a pitcher (besmeared with pitch).
