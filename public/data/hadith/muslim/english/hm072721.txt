7|2721|  Chapter: Which animal the Muhrim and the non-Muhrim are permitted to kill in the state  of Ihram at the conclusion of it (within the precincts of the Ka’ba).  ‘Aisha reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) having said this: Five are  the vicious and harmful things which should be killed even within the precincts of  Haram: rat, scorpion, crow, kite and voracious dog.
