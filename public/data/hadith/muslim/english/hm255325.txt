25|5325|  Chapter: Forbiddance in calling one (other than Allah’s Apostle) with the Kunya of Abdu’l- Qasim and the fact pertaining to good names.  Abu Huraira reported that Abu’l-Qasim (may peace be upon him) said: Give name (to  your children) after my name but do not give the kunya (of Abu’l-Qasim) after my kunya.  ‘Amr reported from Abu Huraira that he did not say that he had heard it directly from  Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).
