21|4780|  Chapter: Pertaining to the eating of the flesh of the horse.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah is reported to have said: We ate during the time of Khaibar the (flesh)  of horses and of wild asses, but Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) prohibited  us (to eat) the flesh of domestic asses. This hadith has hen transmitted on the authority  of Ibn Juraij.
