6|2589|  Chapter: Forbiddance to observe perpetual fast and excellence of observing fast on alternate  days.  ‘Abdullah b. ‘Amr (Allah be pleased with them) reported: The Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) said to me: Recite the whole of the Qur’an during every month. I  said: I find power (to recite it) in a shorter period. He said: Then recite it in twenty nights.  I said: I find power (to recite it in a shorter period even than this), whereupon he said:  Then recite it in seven (nights) and do not exceed beyond it.
