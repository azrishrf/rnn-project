10|3708|  Chapter: Forbiddance of al-Mubiqala, and al-Muzabana, and al-Mukhabara and the  sale of fruits before their good condition is clear, and al-Mu’awama, i.e., the sale for  some years.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) forbade the types of sales as described before.
