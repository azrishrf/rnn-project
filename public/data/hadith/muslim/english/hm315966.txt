31|5966|  Chapter: The merits of Hadrat Khadija, mother of the faithful (Allah be pleased with her).  Abu Musa reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: There are  many persons amongst men who are quite perfect but there are not perfect amongst  women except Mary, daughter of ‘Imran, Asiya wife of Pharaoh, and the excellence of  ‘Aisha as compared to women is that of Tharid over all other foods.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
