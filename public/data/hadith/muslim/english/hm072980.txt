7|2980|  Chapter: What is the excellent time for throwing pebbles (at the Jamras).  Jabir (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) flung pebbles at jamra on the Day of Nahr after sunrise, and after that (i.e. on the  11th, 12th and 13th of Dhu’l-Hijja when the sun had declined.
