1|152|  Chapter: Belief in Allah is the best of deeds.         ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud reported: I said: Messenger of Allah, which of the deeds (takes one)  nearer to Paradise? He (the Holy Prophet) replied: Prayer at its proper time, I said:  What next, Messenger of Allah? He replied: Kindness to the parents. I said: What next?  He replied: Jihad in the cause of Allah.
