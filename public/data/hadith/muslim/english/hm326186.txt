32|6186|  Chapter: Politeness towards parents and their right to it.  Yazid b. Abu Habib reported that Na’im, the freed slave of Umm Salama, reported to  him that ‘Abdullah b. ‘Amr b. ‘As said: There came to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be  upon him) a person and said: I owe allegiance to you for migration and Jihad seeking  reward only from Allah. He (the Holy Prophet) said: Is one from amongst your parents  living? He said: Yes, of course, both are living. He further asked: Do you want to seek  reward from Allah? He said: Yes. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) said: Go back to your parents and accord them benevolent treatment.               OF RELATIONSHIP
