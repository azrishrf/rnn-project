4|2070|  Chapter: Merit of the funeral prayer and following the bier.  This Hadith has been narrated by Qatada with the same chain of transmitters. And in  the Hadith transmitted by Sa’id and Hisham, (the words are): “The Apostle of Allah (may  peace be upon him) was asked about qirat, and he said: It is equivalent to Uhud.”
