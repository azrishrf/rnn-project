20|4633|  Chapter: The merit of Jihad and campaigning in the way of Allah.  Another version of the tradition narrated through a different chain of transmitters on the  authority of Abu Huraira has the same wording as the previous tradition: “Allah takes  care of one who goes out in the way of Allah” but ends in the words: “I would not lag  behind any expedition which is undertaken to fight in the way of Allah, the Exalted.”
