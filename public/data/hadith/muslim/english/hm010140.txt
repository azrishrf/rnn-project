1|140|  Chapter: The love of Ansar (helpers) and ‘All (may Allah be pleased with all of them) is  (an Ingredient) of Iman and (one of) its signs, and hatred against them is a sign of  dissemblance.  It is narrated on the authority of Abu Sa’id Khudri that the Messenger of Allah observed:  The person who believes in Allah and the Last Day never nurses a grudge against the  Ansar.
