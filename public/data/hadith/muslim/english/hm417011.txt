41|7011|  Chapter: Account of the Dajjal and his features and what would be along with.  Hudhaifa reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: the Dajjal  would have with him water and fire and his fire would bays the effect of cold water and  his water would have the effect of fire, so don’t put yourself to ruin. Abu Mas’ud  reported: I also heard it from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him).
