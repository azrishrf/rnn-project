4|1315|  Chapter: Preference for saying the ‘Asr prayer at the commencement of the prescribed time.  ‘Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported that the polytheists detained the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) from observing the afternoon prayer till the sun became red  or it became yellow. Upon this the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said:  They have diverted us from (offering) the middle prayer i.e. the ‘Asr prayer. May Allah fill  their bellies and their graves with fire, or he said: May Allah stuff their bellies and their  graves with fire.
