6|2405|  Chapter: The timing of fast begins with the dawn.  This Hadith has been narrated by Sulaiman al-Taimi with the same chain of transmitters  (but with a slight variation of words) that he (the Holy Prophet) said: The dawn is not like  it as it is said; he then gathered his fingers and lowered them. But he said, it is like this  (and he placed the index finger upon the other one and spread his hand).
