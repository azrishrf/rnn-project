9|3596|  Chapter: It is forbidden to sell Al-Wala’ (the right of inheritance of a slave) and its  conferring on others.  Ibn Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace he  upon him) forbade the selling and making a gift of the right of inheritance of a slave.  Imam Muslim said: All the persons depend upon Abdullah b. Dinar in regard to this  hadith.
