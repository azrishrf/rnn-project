41|7046|  Chapter: Approach of the Last Hour.  Shu’ba reported: I heard Qatada and Abu Tayyab narrating that both of them heard  Anas as narrating that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: I and the Last  Hour have been sent like this, and Shu’ba drew his forefinger and middle finger near  each other while narrating it.
