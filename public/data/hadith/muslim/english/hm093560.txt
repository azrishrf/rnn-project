9|3560|  Chapter: Li’an (Invoking curse).  Sa’id b. Jubair reported that Mus’ab b. Zubair did not affect separation between the  Mutala’inain (invokers of curses). Sa’id said: It was mentioned to ‘Abdullah b. Umar  (Allah be pleased with them) and he said: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him)  effected separation between the two members of Banu al-‘Ajlan.
