30|5711|  Chapter: The cistern of our Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its characteristics.  Jabir b. Samura reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Behold, I shall be present ahead of you on the Cistern, and the distance between its  different sides would be like that between Sana’ and Aila, and its jugs would be like  stars in the sky.
