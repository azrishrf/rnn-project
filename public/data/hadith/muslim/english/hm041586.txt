4|1586|  Chapter: The virtue of Sunan prayers before and after the Fard Rak’ahs and their number.  ‘Abdullah b. Shaqiq reported: I fell ill in Persia and therefore, prayed in a sitting posture,  and I asked ‘Aisha about it and she said: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) prayed for a long time in the night sitting.
