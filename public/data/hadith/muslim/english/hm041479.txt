4|1479|  Chapter: Compensation of the missed prayer and excellence of observing it promptly.  Ibn ‘Umar reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said two rak’ahs  at Mina, and Abu Bakr after him, and ‘Umar after Abu Bakr, and ‘Uthman at the  beginning of his caliphate; then ‘Uthman observed four rak’ahs, and when Ibn ‘Umar  prayed with the Imam, he said four rak’ahs, but when he observed prayer alone, he said  two rak’ahs.
