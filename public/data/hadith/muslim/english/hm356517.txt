35|6517|  Chapter: The merit of pronouncing Tablil, Tasbib and supplication.  Mus’ab b. Sa’d reported that his father told him that he had been in the company of  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) that he said: Is one amongst you  powerless to get one thousand virtues every day. Amongst those who had been sitting  there, one asked: How one amongst us can get one thousand virtues every day? He  said: Recite: “Hallowed be Allah” one hundred times for (by reciting them) one thousand  virtues are recorded (to your credit) and one thousand vices are blotted out.
