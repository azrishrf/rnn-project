12|3962|  Chapter: Disapproval of giving preference to tome children while making donation.  Nu’man b. Bashir reported: My father brought me to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) and said: I have donated this slave to my son. whereupon he said: Have you  made (such) donation to every one or your sons? He said: No. Thereupon he (the-Holy  Prophet) said: Then take him back.
