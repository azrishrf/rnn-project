30|5743|  Chapter: The mercy and compassion of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) for women  and his command to the camel-driver to drive the mount slowly on which they ride.  Anas reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had in one of his  journeys his black slave who was called Anjasha along with him. He goaded by singing  the songs of camel-driver. Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  said: Anjasha, drive slowly as you are driving (the mounts who are carrying) glass  vessels  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
