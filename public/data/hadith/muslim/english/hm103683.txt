10|3683|  Chapter: It is forbidden to sell fresh dates against dry dates, except in case of al-Araya.  Zaid b Thabit (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) gave concession in case of al-‘ariyya transactions (for exchanging dates)  for dates with measure. Yahya said: ‘Ariyya implies that a person should buy fresh  dates on the tree for his family to eat against a measure of dry dates.
