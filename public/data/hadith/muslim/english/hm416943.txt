41|6943|  Chapter: The Last Hour would not come until the fire emits from the earth of Hijaz.  Ibn Fudail reported on the authority of his father that he heard Salim b. ‘Abdullah b.  ‘Umar as saying: O people of Iraq, how strange it is that you ask about the minor sins  but commit major sins? I heard from my father ‘Abdullah b. ‘Umar, narrating that he  heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying while pointing his hand  towards the east: Verily. the turmoil would come from this side, from where appear the  horns of Satan and you would strike the necks of one another; and Moses killed a  person from among the people of Pharaoh unintentionally and Allah, the Exalted and  Glorious, said: “You killed a person but We relieved you from the grief and tried you with  (many a) trial” (xx. 40). Ahmad b. Umar reported this hadith from Salim, but he did not  make a mention of the words: “I heard”.
