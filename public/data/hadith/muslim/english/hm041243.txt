4|1243|  Chapter: Desirability of seeking refuge from the torment of the grave.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: If anyone  extols Allah after every prayer thirty-three times, and praises Allah thirty-three times,  and declares His Greatness thirty-three times, ninety-nine times in all, and says to  complete a hundred: “There is no god but Allah, having no partner with Him, to Him  belongs sovereignty and to Him is praise due, and He is Potent over everything, ” his  sins will be forgiven even If these are as abundant as the foam of the sea.
