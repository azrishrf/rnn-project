4|1516|  Chapter: Permissibility of combining two prayers on a journey.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed the  noon and afternoon prayers together in Medina without being in a state of fear or in a  state of journey. (Abu Zubair said: I asked Sa’id [one of the narrators] why he did that.  He said: I asked Ibn ‘Abbas as you have asked me, and he replied that he [the Holy  Prophet] wanted that no one among his Ummah should be put to [unnecessary]  hardship.)
