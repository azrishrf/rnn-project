30|5812|  Chapter: Pertaining to the name of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of Ma’mar (and the words are): I said  to Zuhri: What does (the word) al-‘Aqib imply? He said: One after whom there is no  Prophet, and in the hadith transmitted on the authority of Ma’mar and ‘Uqail there is a  slight variation of wording.  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
