6|2466|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing the fast or not observing it in the month of Ramadan for a  traveller.  Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) went out during the month of Ramadan in the year of Victory (when  Mecca was conquered) and was fasting till he reached Kadid (a canal situated at a  distance of forty-two miles from Mecca) and he then broke the fast. And it was the habit  of the Companions of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) to follow him in  every new thing (or act). So they followed him also (in this matter).
