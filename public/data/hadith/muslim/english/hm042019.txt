4|2019|  Chapter: The dead body is punished for the lamentation of his family.  Abu Burda narrated on the authority of his father that when ‘Umar was wounded Suhaib  uttered (loudly in lamentation): O brother! Upon this ‘Umar said: Suhaib, did you not  know that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: “The dead is punished  because of the lamentation of the living”?
