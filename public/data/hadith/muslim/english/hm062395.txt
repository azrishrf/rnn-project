6|2395|  Chapter: The months of Id are not incomplete.  ‘Abd Ar-Rahman b. Abu Bakra reported on the authority of Abu Bakra that the Apostle  of Allah (may peace be upon him) had said: The months of ‘Id are not incomplete. And  in the Hadith narrated by Khalid (the words are): “The months, of ‘Id are Ramadan and  Dhu’l-Hijja.”
