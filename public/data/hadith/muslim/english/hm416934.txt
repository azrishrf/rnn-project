41|6934|  Chapter: The signs before the approach of the Last Hour.  Abu Sariha reported: We were discussing the Last Hour) that Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) looked towards us. The rest of the hadith is the same and the tenth  (sign) was the descent of Jesus Christ son of Mary, and Shu’ba said: ‘Abd al-‘Aziz did  not trace it directly to Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).
