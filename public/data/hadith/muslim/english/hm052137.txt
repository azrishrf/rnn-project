5|2137|  Chapter: No Zakat is due from a Muslim or his slave or his horse.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Sadaqa (zakat) is payable on less than five wasqs of (dates or grains), on less than five  camel-heads, and on less than five uqiyas (of silver).
