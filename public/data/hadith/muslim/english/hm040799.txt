4|799|  Chapter: The placing of the right hand over the left hand after the first Takbir in prayer (Takbir-i- Tahrima) below the chest and above the navel and then placing them opposite the shoulders in  prostration.  Tawus narrated it on the authority of Ibn ‘Abbas that he said: The Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) used to teach us tashahhud as he would teach us a Sura of  the Qur’an.
