10|3860|  Chapter: Accepting that which is lawful and abandoning that which is doubtful.  Habib reported that he heard Abu Minhal as saying: I asked al-Bara’ b. Azib about the  exchange of (gold for silver or vice versa), whereupon he said: you better ask Zaid b.  Arqam for he knows more than I. So I asked Zaid but he said: You better ask al-Bara’  for he knows more than I. Then both of them said: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) forbade the sale of silver for gold when payment is to be made in future.
