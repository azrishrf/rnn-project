8|3376|  Chapter: Al-Azl (incomplete sexual intercourse): Coitus Interruptus.  Abu Sa’id al-Khudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Apostle (May  peace be upon him) was asked about ‘azl, whereupon he said: There is no harm if you  do not do that, for it (the birth of the child) is something ordained. Muhammad (one of  the narrators) said: (The words) La ‘alaykum (there is no harm) implies its Prohibition.
