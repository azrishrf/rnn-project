1|242|  Chapter: Pertaining to evil suggestion or prompting in faith and what should be said  when it occurs to the mind of a man.  It is narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) said: Men will continue to question one another till this is propounded: Allah  created all things but who created Allah? He who found himself confronted with such a  situation should say: I affirm my faith in Allah.
