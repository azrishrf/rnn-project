26|5442|  Chapter: The excellence of curing (with the help of incantation) the influence of the evil eye and  small pustules and the poison (especially of snake and scorpion).  ‘Abd al-Rahman b. al-Aswad reported on the authority of his father: I asked ‘Aisha about  incantation. She said: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had granted its  sanction to the members of a family of the Ansir for incantation in curing every type of  poison.
