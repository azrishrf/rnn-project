7|2973|  Chapter: Stoning at Jamrat al-‘Aqaba from the heart of the valley in the state that  Mecca is on the left side and pronouncing of takbir while throwing every pebble.  Abd al-Rahman b. Yazid reported that he performed Hajj along with ‘Abdullah (Allah be  pleased with him) and he flung seven pebbles at al-Jamra (from a position) that the  House was on his left and Mina was on his right and said: That is the place (of flinging  pebbles of one) upon whom Surah al-Baqara was revealed.
