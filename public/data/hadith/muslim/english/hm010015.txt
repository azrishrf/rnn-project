1|15|  Chapter: Concerning Iman by which a person would enter Heaven.  It is narrated on the authority of Jabir that Nu’man b. Qaufal came to the Holy Prophet  (may peace be upon him) and said: Would I enter Paradise if I say the obligatory  prayers and deny myself that which is forbidden and treat that as lawful what has been  made permissible (by the Shari’ah)? The Holy Prophet (may peace be upon him) replied  in the affirmative.
