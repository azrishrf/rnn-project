4|1899|  Chapter: Shortness of prayer and surmon.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah said that a person entered the Mosque while the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) was delivering the sermon on Friday. Upon this he (the Holy  Prophet) said: Have you observed prayer? He said: No. He (the Holy Prophet) said:  Stand and offer the two rak’ahs. According to Qutaiba, the words are: “He said: Offer  two rak’ahs.”
