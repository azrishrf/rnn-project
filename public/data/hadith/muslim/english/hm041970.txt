4|1970|  Chapter: Prayer at the time of solar eclipse.  ‘Aisha reported that the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) recited loudly in the  eclipse prayer, and he observed four rak’ahs in the form of two rak’ahs and four  prostrations. Zuhri said: Kathir b. ‘Abbas narrated on the authority of Ibn ‘Abbas that the  Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed four rak’ahs and four prostrations in  two rak’ahs.
