1|115|  Chapter: Concerning the peculiarities of a hypocrite.  It is reported on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may peace  and blessings of Allah be upon him) made observations like them embodied in the  hadith narrated by Yahya b. Muhammad on the authority of ‘Ala’, and added to it: even if  he observed fast and prayed and asserted that he was a Muslim.
