12|3980|  Chapter: Al-‘Umra (Life grant).  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) having said: Keep your property to yourselves and do not squander it, for  he who conferred a life grant upon another that property will belong to him upon whom it  is conferred whether he lives or dies, and (would pass on) to his successors (as  heritage).
