4|802|  Chapter: The placing of the right hand over the left hand after the first Takbir in prayer (Takbir-i- Tahrima) below the chest and above the navel and then placing them opposite the shoulders in  prostration.  This Hadith has been transmitted by Qatida with the same chain of transmitters (and the  words are): “Allah, the Exalted and the Glorious, commanded it through the tongue of  His Apostle (may peace be upon-him): Allah listens to him who praises Him.”
