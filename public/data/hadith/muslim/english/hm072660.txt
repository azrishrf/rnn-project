7|2660|  Chapter: The place where the pilgrims enter upon the state of Ihram.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) specified Dhu’l-Hulaifa for the people of Medina; Juhfa for the  people of Syria, Qarn al-Manazil for the people of Najd, Yalamlam for the people of  Yemen (as their respective Mawaqit), and he also said: These are (Mawaqit) of them  too (who live there) and everyone who comes from outside (through) their (directions)  for the sake of Hajj and ‘Umra and for those who live within (those bounds their Miqat is  that) from which they commenced (their journey), and for the people of Mecca, Mecca  itself is (the Miqat).
