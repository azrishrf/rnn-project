30|5759|  Chapter: The fragrance of the sacred body of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its  agility and softness and the blessings of its touch.  Anas reported: I never smelt ambergris or musk as fragrant as the fragrance of the body  of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and I never touched brocade or silk and  found it as soft as the body of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him).
