7|2939|  Chapter: Pronouncing of Talbiya and takbir while going from Mina to ‘Arafat on the day  of ‘Arafa.  Muhammad b. Abu Bakr al-Thaqafi asked Anas b. Malik (Allah be pleased with him),  while on their way from Mina to ‘Arafa in the morning: What did you do on this day in the  company of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)? Thereupon he said: One of  us pronounced Tahlil, and he met with no disapproval, and one of us pronounced  Takbir, and he also met with no disapproval.
