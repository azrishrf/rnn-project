35|6511|  Chapter: The merit of pronouncing Tablil, Tasbib and supplication.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Two are  the expressions which are light on the tongue, but heavy in scale, dear to the  Compassionate One: “Hallowed be Allah and praise is due to Him”; “Hallowed be Allah,  the Great.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
