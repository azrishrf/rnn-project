1|331|  Chapter: Concerning Sidrat-ul-Muntabi (remotest Lote tree).  Al-Shaibani narrated on the authority of Zirr who narrated it on this authority of Abdullah  that the (words of Allah): “The heart belied not what he saw” (al Qur’an, liii. 11) imply  that he saw Gabriel (peace be upon him) and he had six hundred wings.
