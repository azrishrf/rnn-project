2|516|  Chapter: Easing is forbidden in the streets and under the shade.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Be on  your guard against two things which provoke cursing. They (the companions present  there) said: Messenger of Allah, what are those things which provoke cursing? He said:  Easing on the thoroughfares or under the shades (where they take shelter and rest).
