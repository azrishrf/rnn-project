7|3081|  Chapter: The demolishing of the ka’ba and its reconstruction.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her), wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him),  heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: If your people, had not  been recent converts to Islam, I would have spent the treasure of the Ka’ba in the way  of Allah and would have constructed its door just on the level of the ground and would  have encompassed in it the space of Hijr.
