26|5443|  Chapter: The excellence of curing (with the help of incantation) the influence of the evil eye and  small pustules and the poison (especially of snake and scorpion).  ‘Aisha reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) granted sanction to  the members of a family of the Ansir for incantation (for removing the effects) of the  poison of the scorpion.
