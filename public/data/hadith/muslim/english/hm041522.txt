4|1522|  Chapter: Permissibility of combining two prayers on a journey.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) observed in  Medina seven (rak’ahs) and eight (rak’ahs), i.e. (be combined) the noon and afternoon  prayers (eight rak’ahs) and the sunset and ‘Isha prayers (seven rak’ahs).
