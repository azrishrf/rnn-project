43|7181|  Chapter: Compel not your slave-girls to prostitution.         Jabir reported that ‘Abdullah b. Ubayy b. Salul had two slave-girls; one was called  Musaika and the other one was called Umaima and he compelled them to prostitution  (for which ‘Abdullah b. Ubayy b. Salul compelled them). They made a complaint about  this to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and it was upon this that this verse  was revealed: “And compel not your slave-girls to prostitute” up to the words: “Allah is  Forgiving, Merciful.”
