6|2502|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) had ordered to observe fast (on ‘Ashura) before the fasting in Ramadan was  made obligatory. But when it became obligatory, then he who wished fasted on the day  of Ashura, and he who wished did not observe it (on that day).
