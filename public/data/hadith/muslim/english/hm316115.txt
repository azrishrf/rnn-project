31|6115|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked blessings upon the tribes of Ghifar  and Aslam.  This hadith has been narrated through other chains of transmitters but all of them  narrated on the authority of Jabir that Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said: To  the tribe of Aslam Allah has granted safety and to the tribe of Ghifar Allah has granted  pardon.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
