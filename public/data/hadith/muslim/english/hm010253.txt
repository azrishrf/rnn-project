1|253|  Chapter: Warning for one appropriating the right of a Muslim by taking a false oath: the  fire (of Hell) is his chastisement.  This hadith has been transmitted by another chain of narrators: Abu Bakr b. Abi Shaiba,  Ishaq b. Ibrahim, Harun b. Abdullah, Abi Usama, Walid b. Kathir, Muhammad b. Ka’b,  his brother Abdullah b. Ka’b and Abi Usama.
