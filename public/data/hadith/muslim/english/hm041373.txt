4|1373|  Chapter: Disapproval of delaying the prayer from the prescribes time; what one who is led in  prayer should do when the Imam delays it ?.  ‘Abdullah reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying about people  who are absent from Jumu’a prayer: I intend that I should command a person to lead  people in prayer, and then burn those persons who absent themselves from Jumu’a  prayer in their houses.
