20|4701|  Chapter: The merit of joining a naval expedition for jihad.  It has been reported on the authority of Umm Haram daughter of Milhan (through  another chain of transmitters). She said: One day the Messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) slept (at a place) near me. He woke up smiling. She said: Messenger of  Allah, what made thee laugh? He said: A people from my followers were presented to  me. They were sailing on the surface of this green sea... (here follows the tradition that  has gone before).
