7|2910|  Chapter: Excellence of touching the two corners (Rukunain al- Yamaniyain) in Tawaf.  Nafi’ (Allah be pleased with him) reported: I saw’ Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them)  touching the Stone with his hand and then kissing his hand and he said: I have never  abandoned it since I saw Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon him) doing It.
