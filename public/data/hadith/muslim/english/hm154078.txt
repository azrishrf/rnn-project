15|4078|  Chapter: How should the masters treat their slaves and expiation if they show high- handedness.  Zadhan Abl Umar reported: I came to Ibn ‘Umar as he had granted freedom to a stave.  He (the narrator further) said: He took hold of a wood or something like it from the earth  and said: It (freedom of a slave) has not the reward ever equal to it, but the fact that I  heard Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon him) say: He who slaps his slave or beats  him, the expiation for it is that he should set him free.
