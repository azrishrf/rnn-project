6|2512|  Chapter: Fasting on the day of ‘Ashura (1Oth of Muharram).  Qais b Sakan reported that al-Ash’ath b. Qais went to ‘Abdullah on the day of ‘Ashura  while he was eating. He said: Abu Muhammad, come near and dine. Upon this he said:  I am fasting. Thereupon he said: We used to observe fast and then (this practice) was  abandoned.
