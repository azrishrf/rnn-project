20|4610|  Chapter: Race between horses and their training for the competition.  It has been narrated on the authority of Ibn ‘Umar that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) had a race of the horses which had been especially prepared for  the purpose from Hafya’ to Thaniyyat al-Wada’ (the latter being the winning post), and  of those which had not been trained from Thaniyya to the mosque of Banu Zuraiq, and  Ibn Umar was among those who took part in this race.
