10|3834|  Chapter: Conversion of currency and selling of gold for silver on the spot.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported: The slave of Banu Bayada cupped  Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) and he gave him his wages, and talked to his         master and he reduced the charges, and if this earning was unlawful Allah’s Apostle  (may peace be upon him) would not have given it.
