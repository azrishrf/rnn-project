4|1494|  Chapter: The prayer of travellers and its shortening.  ‘Abdullah b. Harith reported that Ibn ‘Abbas commanded the Mu’adhdhin to (summon  the people to prayer on Friday and make announcement to say prayer in their houses)         when it was rainy, and the rest of the Hadith is the same (except this) that he said: I do  not like you should walk in muddy slippery place.
