4|967|  Chapter: Moderation in the articles of prayers and their shortening and perfection.  Abu Saâ€™id al-Khudri reported: When the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  raised his head after bowing, he said: O Allah! our Lord, to Thee be the praise that  would fill all the heavens and the earth, and all that it pleases Thee besides (them). O,  thou art worthy of praise and glory, most worthy of what a servant says, and we all are  Thy servants, no one can withhold what Thou givest or give what Thou withholdest, and  riches cannot avail a wealthy person against Thee.
