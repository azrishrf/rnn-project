4|1914|  Chapter: Hadith pertaining to giving instruction in Kbutba.  Abu Huraira reported that the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) used to recite  in the dawn prayer on Friday: “Alif-Lam-Mim, Tanzil” in the first rak’ah, and in the  second one: “Surely there came over the man a time when he was nothing that could be  mentioned.”
