7|2652|  Chapter: What is permitted for the Muhrim (wearer of Ihram) in Hajj and Umra, what is  not permissible and forbiddance of the use of perfume.  This Hadith has been narrated on the authority of ‘Amr b. Dinar with the same chain of  transmitters, but none of them (the narrators) made a mention that he (the Holy  Prophet) was delivering address at ‘Arafia except Sbu’ba.
