37|6647|  Chapter: Allah’s sense of honour, and His prohibition of the abominable acts.  ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud reported it directly from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) that he said: None is more self-respecting than Allah and it is because of this that  He has prohibited abominable acts-both visible and invisible and nothing is loved by  Allah more than the praise of His Ownself and it is because of this that He has praised  Himself.
