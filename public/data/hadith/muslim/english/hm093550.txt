9|3550|  Chapter: It is obligatory to abstain from adornment during the ‘Idda period, but  mourning beyond three days is prohibited.  Umm ‘Atiyya (Allah be pleased with her) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) had said: A woman must not observe mourning for one who had died for  more than three (days) except for four months and ten days in the case of her husband  and she must not wear a dyed garment except one of the types made of dyed yarn, or  apply collyrium, or touch perfume except a little perfume or incense, when she has been  purified after her courses.
