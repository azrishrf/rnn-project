4|1678|  Chapter: Encouragement to observe prayers during Ramadhan and that is Tarawih.         Salama said: I met Kuraib and he reported Ibn ‘Abbas as saying: I was with my mother’s  sister Maimuna that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) came there, and  then he narrated the rest of the Hadith as was narrated by Ghundar and said these  words: “Make me light,” beyond any doubt.
