3|713|  Chapter: Purification of the skins of the dead animals by tanning them.  Ibn Wa’la al-Saba’i reported: I asked ‘Abdullah b. ‘Abbas saying: We are the inhabitants  of the western regions. The Magians come to us with skins full of water and fat. He said:  Drink. I said to him: Is it your own opinion? Ibn Abbas said: I heard the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) say: Tanning purifies it (the skin).
