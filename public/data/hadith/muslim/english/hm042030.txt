4|2030|  Chapter: The dead body is punished for the lamentation of his family.  ‘Ali b. Rabi’a reported that the first one who was lamented upon in Kufa was Qaraza b.  Ka’b. Mughira b. Shu’ba said: I heard the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  saying: He who is lamented upon would be punished because of the lamentation for him  on the Day of judgment.
