4|1811|  Chapter: That which concerns recitation.  Uqba b. ‘Amir said: There were the times at which Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) forbade us to pray, or bury our dead: When the sun begins to rise till it is fully         up, when the sun is at its height at midday till it passes over the meridian, and when the  sun draws near to setting till it sets.
