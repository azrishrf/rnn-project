16|4156|  Chapter: He who sheds the blood first of all bears the burden of all subsequent murders.  ‘Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) having said:  No person who is killed unjustly, but the share of (this offence of his also) falls upon the  first son of Adam, for he was the first to introduce killing.
