31|6121|  Chapter: The merits of the tribes of Ghifir, Ashia’, Muzaina, Tamim, Daws and Tayyi.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Quraish,  Ansar, Muzaina, Juhaina and Ghifar, they are my friends and there is no friend of theirs  besides Allah and His Messenger.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
