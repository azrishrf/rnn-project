4|760|  Chapter: The excellence of Adhin and running away of the Satan on hearing it.  This Hadith has been transmitted with the same chain of transmitters by al. Zuhri as  narrated by Ibn Juraij (who) said: When the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) stood up for prayer, he raised hands (to the height) apposite the shoulders and  then recited takbir.
