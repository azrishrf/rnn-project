40|6849|  Chapter: The description of the Day of Resurrection (May Allah save us from its terrors).  Ibn ‘Umar reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: When the  people would stand before Allah, the Lord of the worlds, each one of them would stand  submerged into perspiration up to half of his ears, and there is no mention of the “day”  in the hadith transmitted on the authority of Ibn Muthanni.
