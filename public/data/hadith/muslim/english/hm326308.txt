32|6308|  Chapter: The evil of a lie and the goodness of truth and its merit.  ‘Abdullah b. Mas’ud reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Telling of truth is a virtue and virtue leads to Paradise and the servant who endeavours  to tell the truth is recorded as truthful, and lie is obscenity and obscenity leads to Hell- Fire, and the servant who endeavours to tell a lie is recorded as a liar. Ibn Abu Shaiba  reported this from Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).  OF RELATIONSHIP
