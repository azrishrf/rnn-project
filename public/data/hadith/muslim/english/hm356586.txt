35|6586|  Chapter: The merit of Subhan Allah Wa Bi Hamdihi.  Abu Dharr reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) was asked as to  which words were the best. He said: Those for which Allah made a choice for His  Angels and His servants (and the words are): “Hallowed be Allah and praise is due to  Him.”               REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
