26|5506|  Chapter: Plague, ill omen and magic.  ‘Amir b. Rabi’ah reported: ‘Umar went to Syria and as he came to Sargh, information  was given to him that an epidemic had broken out in Syria. ‘Abd al-Rahman b. ‘Auf  narrated to him that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had said: When you  hear of its presence in a land, don’t move towards it, and when it breaks out in a land  and you are therein, then don’t run away from it. So ‘Umar b. Khattab came back from  Sargh. Salim b. ‘Abdullah reported that ‘Umar went back, along with people on hearing  the hadith reported on the authority of ‘Abd al-Rahman b. ‘Auf.
