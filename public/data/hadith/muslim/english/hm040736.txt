4|736|  Chapter: Lying with one in menstruation above the waist wrapper.  Anas reported: Bilal was commanded (by the Apostle of Allah) to repeat (the phrases of)  Adhan twice and once in Iqama. The narrator said: I made a mention of it before Ayyub  who said: Except for saying: Qamat-is-Salat [the time for prayer has come].
