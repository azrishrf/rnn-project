This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of al-Bara’ b. ‘Azib with a slight  variation of wording and there is this addition in the hadith transmitted on the authority  of Husain: “In case you get up in the morning, you will get up with bliss.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
