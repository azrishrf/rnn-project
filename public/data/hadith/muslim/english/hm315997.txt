31|5997|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her).  ‘Aisha, the wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him), reported that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: ‘Aisha, here is Gabriel offering you  greetings. She said: I made a reply: Let there be peace and blessings of Allah upon  him, and added: He sees what I do not see.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
