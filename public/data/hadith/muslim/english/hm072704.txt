7|2704|  Chapter: Hunting is forbidden for one who is in the state of Ihram.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported that al-Sa’b b. Jaththama presented to  the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) a wild ass as he was in a state of Ihram,  and he returned it to him saying: If we were not in a state of Ihram, we would have  accepted it from you.
