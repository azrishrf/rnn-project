4|1027|  Chapter: The excellence of the prayer and the way it is begun and the excellence of  Ruku’ and moderation in it and prostration and moderation in it, etc.  Busr b Sa’id reported that Zaid b Khalid al-Juhani sent him to Abu Juhaim in order to  ask him what he had heard from the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) with  regard to the passer in front of the worshipper. Abu Juhaim reported that the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: If anyone who passes in front of a man who is  praying knew the responsibility he incurs, he would stand still forty (years) rather than to  pass in front of him Abu Nadr said: I do not know whether he said forty days or months  or years.
