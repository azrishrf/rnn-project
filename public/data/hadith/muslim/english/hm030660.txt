3|660|  Chapter: It is obligatory for menstruating woman to complete the abandoned fats, but  not the abandoned prayers.  Mu’adha reported: A woman asked ‘Aisha: Should one amongst us complete prayers  abandoned during the period of menses? ‘Aisha said: Are you a Haruriya? When any  one of us during the time of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) was in  her menses (and abandoned prayer) she was not required to complete them.
