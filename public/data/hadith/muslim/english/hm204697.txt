20|4697|  Chapter: Beware of those who could not join a jihad expedition on account of illness or  some other excuse.  It has been narrated on the authority of Jabir who said: We were with the Holy Prophet  (may peace be upon him) on an expedition. He said: There are some people in Medina.  They are with you whenever you cover a distance or cross a valley. They have been  detained by illness.
