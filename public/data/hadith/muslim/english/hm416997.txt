41|6997|  Chapter: Story of Ibn Sayyid.  This hadith has transmitted on the authority of Abu Sa’id that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) asked Ibn Sa’id about the earth of Paradise. Thereupon he said:  Abu’l-Qasim, It is like a fine white musk, whereupon he (the Holy Prophet) said: ‘You  have told the truth.
