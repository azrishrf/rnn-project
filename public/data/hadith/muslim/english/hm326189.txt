32|6189|  Chapter: The preference of benevolent treatment to parents over voluntary prayers, etc.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as saying: Let him be  humbled into dust; let him be humbled into dust. It was said: Allah’s Messenger, who is         he? He said: He who sees either of his parents during their old age or he sees both of  them, but he does not enter Paradise.  OF RELATIONSHIP
