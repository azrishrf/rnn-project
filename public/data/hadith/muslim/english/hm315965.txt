31|5965|  Chapter: The merits of Hadrat Khadija, mother of the faithful (Allah be pleased with her).  Abdullah b. Ja’far reported that he heard ‘All say in Kulfa that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) said: The best of the women of her time was Mary, daughter of  ‘Imran, and the best of the women of her time was Khadija, daughter of khuwailid. Abu  Kuraib said that Wakil pointed towards the sky and the earth           HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
