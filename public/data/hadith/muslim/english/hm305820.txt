30|5820|  Chapter: Respect of Allah’s Messenger and abandoning of too many questions especially those  for which there is no genuine need.  This hadith has been narrated by Abu Huraira through a different chain of transmitters  (and the words are) that he reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  having said: Abandon that which I have asked you to abandon, for the people before  you went to their doom (for asking too many questions).
