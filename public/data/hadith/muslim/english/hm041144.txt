4|1144|  Chapter: When food is brought before man and he is inclined to take it, he should not  say prayer before eating it and undesirability of prayer while feeling the call of nature.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: He who  eats of this plant (garlic) should not approach our mosque and should not harm us with  the odour of garlic.
