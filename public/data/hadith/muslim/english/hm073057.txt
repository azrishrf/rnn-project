7|3057|  Chapter: It is obligatory to perform farewell circumambulation, while a menstruating  woman i., exempted from it.         Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported that the people used to return through  every path, whereupon Allah’s Messenger (way peace be upon him) said: None  amongst you should depart until he performs the last circumambulation round the  House. Zuhair said (the words are): [Arabic: Yabswarifuwn Kulla Wajh] and the word  [Arabic: Fiy] was not mentioned.
