4|1841|  Chapter: The essentiality of taking bath on Friday.  ‘Abd al-Rahman son of Abd Sa’id al-Khudri reported on the authority of his father that  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: Bathing on Friday for every  adult, using of Miswak and applying some perfume, that is available-these are essential.  So far as the perfume is concerned, it may be that used by a lady.
