6|2607|  Chapter: Fasting in the month of Sha’ban.  ‘Imran b. Husain (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace  he upon him) having said to him or to someone else: Did you fast in the middle of  Sha’ban? He said: No. Thereupon he (the Holy Prophet) said: If you did not observe  fast, then you should observe fast for two days.
