14|4032|  Chapter: He who took the vow that he would go on foot to the Ka’ba.  ‘Uqba b. Amir reported: My sister took a vow that she would walk bare foot to the house  of Allah (Ka’ba). She asked me to inquire from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) about it. I sought his decision and he said: She should walk on foot and ride also.
