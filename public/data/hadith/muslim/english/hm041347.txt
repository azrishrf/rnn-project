4|1347|  Chapter: Time for the night prayer and its delay.  ‘Aisha reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) used to observe the  morning prayer, and the women would go back wrapped in their mantles being  unrecognisable because of the darkness before dawn. (Ishaq b. Musa) al-Ansari (one of  the transmitters in this chain of narration) narrated “wrapped” (only) in his narration. (No  mention was made of mantles.)
