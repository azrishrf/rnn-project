39|6744|  Chapter: The similitude of a believer and a non-believer.  Ka’b reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said that the similitude  of a believer is that of a standing crop in a field which is shaken by wind and then it  comes to its original position but it stands at its roots. The similitude of a non-believer is  that of a cypress tree which stands on its roots and nothing shakes it but it is uprooted  (with) one (violent stroke).  PARADISE AND HELL
