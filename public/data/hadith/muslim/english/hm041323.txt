4|1323|  Chapter: The severity (of punishment) in missing the ‘Asr prayer.  Waki’ reported (this Hadith) with the same chain of transmitters (that the Holy Prophet)  said: You will be soon presented before your Lord, and you will see Him as you are  seeing this moon, and then recited (the above-mentioned verse). But (in this Hadith) no  mention is made of Jarir.
