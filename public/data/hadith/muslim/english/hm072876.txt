7|2876|  Chapter: Talbiya of the Apostle (may peace be upon him) and his sacrifice.  Anas (Allah be pleased with him) reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) pronouncing Talbiya for both simultaneously, Talbiya for ‘Umra and Hajj.  Talbiya for Uwra and Hajj (he performed both Hajj and Umra as a Qarin). In another  version words are: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) pronouncing  Talbiya for Umra and Hajj (simultaneously).”
