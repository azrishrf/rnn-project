1|126|  Chapter: Don’t become unbelievers after me by striking the necks of one another.  It is narrated on the authority of Abdullah b. Umar that the Apostle(may peace and  blessings be upon him) observed on the occasion of the Farewell Pilgrimage Woe unto  you distress unto you! Don’t turn back as unbelievers after me by striking the necks of  one another.
