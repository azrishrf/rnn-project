8|3306|  Chapter: Seeking consent of al-Thayyib in marriage in words and of a virgin in (meaningful)  silence.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Apostle (May peace be upon  him) as saying: A woman without a husband has wore right to her person than her  guardian, and a virgin’s consent must be asked from her, and her silence implies her  consent.
