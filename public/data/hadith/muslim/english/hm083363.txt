8|3363|  Chapter: Permissibility of having sexual intercourse with one’s wife from the front or from  behind avoiding the anus.  Jabir (Allah be pleased with him) declared that the Jews used to say: When a man has  intercourse with his wife through the vagina but being on her back, the child will have  squint, so the verse came down: “Your wives are your tilth; go then unto your tilth as  you may desire”(ii. 223)
