26|5434|  Chapter: The excellence of curing the patient with the help of incantation.  ‘Aisha reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: When Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) visited the sick he would say: Lord of the people,  remove the disease, cure him, for Thou art the great Curer, there is no cure but through  Thine healing Power, which leaves nothing of the disease.
