7|3013|  Chapter: Excellence of making a halt at al Muha’ab, on the day of Nahr, and observing  prayer there.         Abu Huraira (Allah be pleated with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) said: God willing, we will get down tomorrow, at Khaif of Banu Kinanah, the  place where they had taken an oath on unbelief.
