35|6573|  Chapter: Refuge from the evil of what one has done, and what one has not done.  ‘Ali reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to him: Say, “O  Allah, direct me to the right path and make me adhere to the straight path,” and when  you make a mention of right guidance, keep in mind the right path and when you  consider of the straight (path), keep in mind the straightness of the arrow.
