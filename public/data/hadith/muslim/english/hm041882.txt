4|1882|  Chapter: Concerning the words of the Qur’an: “And when they see merchandise or  sport, they break away to it, and leave thee standing’.  Abdullah b. Umar and Abu Huraira said that they heard Allah’s Messenger (way peace  be upon him) say on the planks of his pulpit: People most cease to neglect the Friday  prayer or Allah will seal their hearts and then they will be among the negligent.
