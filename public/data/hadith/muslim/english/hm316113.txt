31|6113|  Chapter: Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) invoked blessings upon the tribes of Ghifar  and Aslam.  Abu Dharr reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: To the tribe  of Aslam Allah has granted safety and to the tribe of Ghifar Allah has granted pardon.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
