10|3666|  Chapter: Prohibition of the sale of fruits until they are clearly in good condition.  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) forbade the sale of palm-trees (i.e. their trusts) until the dates began to ripen,  and ears of corn until they were white and were safe from blight. He forbade the seller  and the buyer.
