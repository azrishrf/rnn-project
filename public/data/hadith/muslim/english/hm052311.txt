5|2311|  Chapter: The Khwarij and their characteristics.  This Hadith has been narrated by Sa’id b. Masruq with the same chain of transmitters  (with the words): “The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) distributed the spoils  of Hunain, and he (the Holy Prophet) gave one hundred camels to Abu Sufyan b. Harb.  The rest of the Hadith is the same, but with this addition: “He bestowed upon “Alqama b.  ‘Ulatha one hundred (camels).”
