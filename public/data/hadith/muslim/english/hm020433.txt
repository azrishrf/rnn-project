2|433|  Chapter: Purification is essential for prayer.  Mus’ab b. Sa’d reported: ‘Abdullah son of Umar came to Ibn’Amir in order to inquire  after his health as he was ailing. He said Ibn ‘Umar, why don’t you pray to Allah for me?  He said: I heard of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) say: Neither the prayer  is accepted without purification nor is charity accepted out of the ill-gotten (wealth), and  thou wert the (governor) of Basra.
