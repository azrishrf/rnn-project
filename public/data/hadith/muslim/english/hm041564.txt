4|1564|  Chapter: Excellence of the forenoon prayer, two are its minimum Rak’ahs and eight are its  maximum Rak’ahs and average Rak’ahs are four or six, and exhortation for the observance of  the prayer.  Hafsa reported that when it was dawn, the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) did not observe (any other prayers) but two short rak’ahs.
