7|3037|  Chapter: It is meritorious for one who does intend to go himself to send the sacrificial  animal to al-Haram.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) sent the sacrificial animals and I wove garlands for them with my own ‘hands, and  he did not refrain from doing anything which he did not avoid in the state of non-Muhrim.
