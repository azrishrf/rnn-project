4|991|  Chapter: The excellence of prostration and exhortation to observe it.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: The Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) had been  commanded that he should prostrate on the seven (bones) and he was forbidden to fold         back the hair and clothing. And in the narration transmitted by Abu Rabi’ (the words  are): “on the seven bones and I was forbidden to fold back the hair and clothing”.  According to Abu’l-Rabi’ (the seven bones are): The hands, the knees, and the  (extremities) of the feet and the forehead.
