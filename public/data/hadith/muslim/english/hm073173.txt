7|3173|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Abu Sa’id al-Kbudri (Allah be pleased with him) reported that Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) said: O Allah, bless us in our sa’ and mud and shower with its  blessings two other blessings (multiply blessings showered upon it).
