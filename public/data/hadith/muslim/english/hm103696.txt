10|3696|  Chapter: It is forbidden to sell fresh dates against dry dates, except in case of al-Araya.  Ibn ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) having forbidden Muzabana, and Muzabana is the selling of dry dates by  measure for fresh dates and the selling of raisins by measure for grapes and selling of  all Ports of fruits on the basis of calculation.
