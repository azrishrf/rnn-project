3|727|  Chapter: It is permissible to eat without ablution and there is no abhorrence in it and  performing of ablution immediately (after that) is not essential.         Ibn ‘Abbas reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) went to the  privy and when he came back, he was presented with food. It was said to him;  Messenger of Allah, wouldn’t you perform ablution. He said: Why, am I to say prayer?
