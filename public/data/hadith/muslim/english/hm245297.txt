24|5297|  Chapter: It Is forbidden to add false hair to one’s head or to pluck the eyebrow or to  separate the teeth.  Asma’, daughter of Abu Bakr, reported that a woman came to Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) and said: I have married my daughter (whose) hair of head have  fallen. Her spouse likes them (the long hair). Allah’s Messenger(may add false hair to  her head? He forbade her to do this.
