7|3097|  Chapter: Travelling of a woman with her Mahram for Hajj, etc.  This Hadith has been narrated on the same authority by Ubaidullah. And in the narration  of Abu Bakr (the words are): “More than three (days).” Ibn Numair narrated on the  authority of his father, (and the words are): “Three (days) except (when) she has a  Mahram with her.”
