26|5567|  Chapter: Prohibition of killing the ants.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: An ant  had bitten a Prophet (one amongst the earlier Prophets) and he ordered that the colony  of the ants should be burnt. And Allah revealed to him: “Because of an ant’s bite you  have burnt a community from amongst the communities which sings My glory.”
