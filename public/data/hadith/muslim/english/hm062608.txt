6|2608|  Chapter: Fasting in the month of Sha’ban.  Imran b. Husain (Allah be pleased with them) reported that Allah’s Apostle (peace be  upon him) said to a person: Did you observe any fast in the middle of this month  (Sha’ban)? He said: No. Thereupon the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  said: Fast for two days instead of (one fast) when you have completed (fasts of)  Ramadan.
