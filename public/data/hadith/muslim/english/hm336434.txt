33|6434|  Chapter: Everyone is born according to his true nature and the command pertaining to the  demise of the children of the infidels and of the children of the Muslims.  Ubayy b. Ka’b reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: The  young man whom Khadir killed was a non-believer by his very nature and had he  survived he would have involved his parents in defiance and unbelief.
