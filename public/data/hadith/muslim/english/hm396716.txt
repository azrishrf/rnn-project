39|6716|  Chapter: The question of a Jew from Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) about soul  and his words: “They ask thee about the soul”.  This hadith has been narrated on the authority of Khabbib through another chain of  transmitters and the words are. I in the pre-Islamic days used to work as an iron-smith. I  did some work for ‘As b. Wa’il and came to him for getting the remuneration of my  wages.  PARADISE AND HELL
