8|3246|  Chapter: Temporary marriage and its prohibition for all times to come.  Jabir b. ‘Abdullah and Salama b. al-Akwa’ said: There came to us the proclaimer of  Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon him) and said: Allah’s Messenger (May peace  be upon him) has granted you permission to benefit yourselves, i.e. to contract  temporary marriage with women.
