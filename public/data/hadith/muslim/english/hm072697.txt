7|2697|  Chapter: Applying of perfume to the body before entering upon the state of Ihram.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported: I used to perfume the Apostle of Allah (may  peace be upon him) with a perfume containing musk before entering upon the state of  Ihram and on the day of sacrifice (10th of Dhu’l-Hijja) and (at the conclusion of Ihram)  before circumambulating the House (for Tawaf-i-Ifada).
