4|791|  Chapter: Argument of those who say that he (the Holy Prophet) did not recite Bismillah (in the  name of Allah) loudly.  Mukhtar b. Fulful reported that he had heard Anas b. Malik say that the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) dozed off, and the rest of the Hadith is the same as  transmitted by Mus-hir except for the words that he (the Holy Prophet) said: It (Kauthar)  is a canal which my Lord the Exalted and the Glorious has promised me in Paradise.  There is a tank over it, but he made no mention of the tumblers like the number of the  stars.
