30|5653|  Chapter: Intercession  by  the  Apostle  of  Allah  (may  peace  be  upon  him)  and  the  paying  of  salutations by a stone to him before his advent as a Prophet.  Wathila  b.  al-Asqa’ reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Verily Allah granted eminence to Kinana from amongst the descendants of Isma’il and he granted  eminence to the Quraish amongst Kinana and he granted eminence to the Quraish amongst Banu  Hashim and he granted me eminences from the tribe of Banu Hashim.
