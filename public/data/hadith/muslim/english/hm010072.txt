1|72|  Chapter: Concerning the fact that it is one of the characteristics of Iman that one should  like the same thing for one’s brother-in-Islam as one likes for one’s self.  It is arrested on the authority of Anas b. Malik that the Prophet (may peace and  blessings be upon him) observed: one amongst you believes (truly) till one likes for his  brother or for his neighbour that which he loves for himself.
