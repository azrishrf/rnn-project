7|3003|  Chapter: Regarding one who shaves before offering the sacrifice or offers sacrifice  before throwing (the stones at Jamra).  Ibn Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) reported that it was said to Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) about sacrificing of animals, shaving of one’s head, throwing of  pebbles, and (the order of) precedence and succession, and he said: There is no harm  in it.
