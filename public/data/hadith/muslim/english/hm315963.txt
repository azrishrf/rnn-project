31|5963|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Abdullah b. Ja’far (Allah be pleased with him).  Abdullah b. Ja’a’far reported that when Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  came back from a journey he met us. Once he met me, Hasan or Husain, and he  mounted one of us before him and the other one behind him until we entered Medina.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
