4|1157|  Chapter: Forbiddance to eat garlic, onions and anything of offensive smell while coming  to the mosque.  Ibn Buraida narrated it on the authority of his father that a Bedouin came when the  Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) had completed the morning prayer. He thrust  his head in the door of the mosque, and then the Hadith (as narrated above) was  narrated.
