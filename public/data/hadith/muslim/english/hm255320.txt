25|5320|  Chapter: Forbiddance in calling one (other than Allah’s Apostle) with the Kunya of Abdu’l- Qasim and the fact pertaining to good names.  This hadith has been reported on the authority of A’mash with the same chain of  transmitters but there is a slight variation (of wording) that, instead of the word Bu’i that  (I have been sent), the word ju’ilat (I have been made) has been used.
