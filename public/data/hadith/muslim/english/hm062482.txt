6|2482|  Chapter: Permissibility of observing the fast or not observing it in the month of Ramadan for a  traveller.  Humaid reported that Anas (Allah be pleased with him) was asked about fasting during  Ramadan while travelling. He said: We travelled with the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) during the month of Ramadan, but neither the observer of the fast  found fault with the breaker of the fast, nor the breaker of the fast found fault with the  observer of the fast.
