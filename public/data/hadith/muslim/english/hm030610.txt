3|610|  Chapter: Bathing is obligatory for a woman after experiencing orgasm in dream.  Umm Salama reported: Umm Sulaim went to the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon  him) and said: Apostle of Allah, Allah is not ashamed of the truth. Is bathing necessary  for a woman when she has a sexual dream? Upon this the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) said: Yes, when she sees the liquid (vaginal secretion). Umm  Salama said: Messenger of Allah, does a woman have sexual dream? He (the Holy  Prophet) said: Let your hand be covered with dust, in what way does her child resemble  her?
