3|628|  Chapter: The quantity of water that is desirable for a bath because of sexual  intercourse, bathing of the male and female with one vessel in the same condition and  washing of one of them with the left-over of the other.  Hafsa, daughter of ‘Abd al-Rahman b. Abu Bakr, reported that ‘Aisha narrated to her  that she and the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) took a bath from the same  vessel which contained water equal to three Mudds or thereabout.
