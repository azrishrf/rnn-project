3|661|  Chapter: It is obligatory for menstruating woman to complete the abandoned fats, but  not the abandoned prayers.  It is reported from Mu’adha that she asked ‘Aisha: Should a menstruating woman  complete the prayer (abandoned during the menstrual period)? ‘Aisha said: Are you a  Hurariya? The wives of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) have had their  monthly courses, (but) did he order them to make compensation (for the abandoned  prayers)? Muhammad b. Ja’far said: (Compensation) denotes their completion.
