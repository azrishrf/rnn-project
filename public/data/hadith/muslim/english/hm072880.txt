7|2880|  Chapter: Concerning the Umras performed by Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon  him) and their respective times.  Qatida said: I asked Anas (Allah be pleased with him) as to bow many Pilgrimages had  been performed by Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him), and he replied: One  Hajj and four ‘Umras were performed by him. The rest of the Hadith is the same.
