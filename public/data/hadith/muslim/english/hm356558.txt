35|6558|  Chapter: Refuge from the evil of what one has done, and what one has not done.  Farwa’ b. Naufal reported: I asked ‘Aisha about the supplication that Allah’s Messenger  (may peace be upon him) made. She said that he used to say: “O Allah, I seek refuge in  Thee from the evil of what I have done and from the evil of what I have not done.”  REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
