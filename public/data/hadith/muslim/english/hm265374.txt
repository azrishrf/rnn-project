26|5374|  Chapter: The rider should first greet the pedestrian and the small number should greet the large  number of persons.  Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: The rider  should first greet the pedestrian, and the pedestrian the one who is seated and a small  group should greet a larger group (with as-Salam-u-‘Alaikum).
