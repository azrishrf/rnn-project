30|5713|  Chapter: The angels fought along with Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) in the Battle of  Uhud.  Sa’d reported that on the Day of Ubud I saw on the right side of Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) and on his left side two persons dressed in white clothes and whom  I did not see before nor after that, and they were Gabriel and Michael (Allah be pleased  with both of them).
