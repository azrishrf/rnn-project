7|3151|  Chapter: Eminence of Medina, Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) showered  blessings upon it. Its sacredness and unlawfulness of hunting and lopping of trees and  demarcation its precincts.  Rafi’ b. Khadij reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Ibrahim  declared Mecca as sacred and I declare sacred the area between its two stony grounds  (lava lands by which he meant Medina).
