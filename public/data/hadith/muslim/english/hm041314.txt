4|1314|  Chapter: Preference for saying the ‘Asr prayer at the commencement of the prescribed time.  ‘Ali reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said on the day (of the  Battle) of Ahzab: They diverted us from saying the middle prayer, i.e. the ‘Asr prayer.  May Allah fill their houses and graves with fire; he then observed this prayer between  the evening prayer and the night prayer.
