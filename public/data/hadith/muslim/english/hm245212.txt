24|5212|  Chapter: Wearing of silver ring by Allah’s Apostle (may peace he upon him) in which  were engraved the words (Muhammad the Messenger of Allah) and after him his  successors wore it.         Ibn Umar reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had made for  himself a ring of silver, and he (wore it in his finger), then it was in Abu Bakr’s finger,  then it was in ‘Umar’s finger, then it was in ‘Uthman’s finger, until it fell into the well of  Aris and it had these words engraved upon it (Muhammad, Messenger of Allah). Ibn  Numair narrated it with a slight variation of words.
