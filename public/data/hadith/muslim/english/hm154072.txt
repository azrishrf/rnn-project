15|4072|  Chapter: What a non-believer should do with his vow when he embraces Islam.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that Umar (b. Khattab) said: Messenger of Allah, I had taken a vow  during the days of Ignorance (Jahiliyya) that I would observe I’tikaf for a night in the  Sacred Mosque. He (the Holy Prophet) said: Fulfil your vow.
