31|6159|  Chapter: The merits of the companions of the Holy Prophet (may peace be upon him) and of  those who are next to them and then of those who are next to them.  ‘Aisha reported that a person asked Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) as to who  amongst the people were the best. He said: Of the generation to which I belong, then of  the second generation (generation adjacent to my generation), then of the third  generation (generation adjacent to the second generation).  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
