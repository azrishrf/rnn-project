35|6532|  Chapter: Excellence of remembering Allah in a low voice.  Abu Musa Ash’ari reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to  him: Should I not direct you to the words from the treasures of Paradise, or he said: Like  a treasure from the treasures of Paradise? I said: Of course, do that. Thereupon he  said: “There is no might and no power but that of Allah.”             REPENTANCE AND SEEKING FORGIVENESS
