4|1524|  Chapter: Permissibility of combining two prayers on a journey.  ‘Abdullah b. Shaqiq al-‘Uqaili reported: A person said to Ibn ‘Abbas (as he delayed the  prayer): Prayer. He kept silence. He again said: Prayer. He again kept silence, and he  again cried: Prayer. He again kept silence and said: May you be deprived of your  mother, do you teach us about prayer? We used to combine two prayers during the life  of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him).
