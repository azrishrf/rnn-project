39|6764|  Chapter: None would attain salvation because of his deeds but it is through Lord’s Mercy (that  he would attain that).         Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: None  amongst you can get. into Paradise by virtue of his deeds alone. They said: Allah’s  Messenger, not even you? Thereupon he said: Not even I, but that Allah should wrap  me in His Grace and Mercy.  PARADISE AND HELL
