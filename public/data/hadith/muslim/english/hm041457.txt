4|1457|  Chapter: The excellence of Qunut in all the prayers when any calamity befalls the Muslims.  Qatada reported it on the authority of Anas b. Malik that the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) said: When any one of you omits the prayer due to sleep or he  forgets it, he should observe it when he remembers it, for Allah has said: “Observe  prayer for remembrance of Me.”
