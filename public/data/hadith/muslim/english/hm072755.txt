7|2755|  Chapter: What is to be done with a Muhrim when he dies.  Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with him) reported that there was a person in the company  of Allah’s Messenger (may peace’ be upon him) whose camel broke his neck and he  died; thereupon Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) said: Wash him, but do not         apply perfume and do not cover his face, for he would be raised (on the Day of  Resurrection) pronouncing Talbiya.
