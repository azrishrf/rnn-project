4|1087|  Chapter: The virtue of building the mosques and exhortation to it.  This Hadith is narrated on the authority of Alqama and Aswad by another chain of  transmitters and in the Hadith transmitted by Ibn Mus-hir and Jabir the words are: “I  perceive as if I am seeing the gap between the fingers of the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) as he was bowing.”
