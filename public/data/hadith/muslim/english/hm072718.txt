7|2718|  Chapter: Which animal the Muhrim and the non-Muhrim are permitted to kill in the state  of Ihram at the conclusion of it (within the precincts of the Ka’ba).         ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported Allah’* Apostle (may peace be upon him) as  saying: Five are the harmful things which should be killed in the state of Ihram or  otherwise: snake, speckled crow, rat, voracious dog, and kite.
