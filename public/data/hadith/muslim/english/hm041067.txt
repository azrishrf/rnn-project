4|1067|  Chapter: Prayer in a single garment.  Hammam b. Munabbih reported: That is what Abu Huraira reported to us from the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) and he narrated (some) ahadith one of  which is that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said: I have been helped  by terror (in the hearts of enemies) and I have been given words which are concise but  comprehensive in meaning.
