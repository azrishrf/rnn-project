5|2151|  Chapter: Taking out of Sadaqat-ul-Fitr before observing Fitr prayer.  Ibn ‘Umar said that the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) prescribed the  Sadaqa of Ramadan (Sadaqat-al-Fitr) one sa’ of dates or one sa’ of barley for every  free man or a slave, male or female, and then the people equalised (one sa’ of dates or  barley) with half a sa’ of wheat.
