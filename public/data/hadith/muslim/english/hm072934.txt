7|2934|  Chapter: The pilgrim should continue to pronounce Talbiya until the stoning of Jamrat  al-‘Aqaba on the day of sacrifice (10th of Dhu’l-Hajja).         ‘Abdullah narrated to us as we had gathered (at Muzdalifa): I have heard from one upon  whom Surah al-Baqara was revealed (the Holy Prophet) pronouncing Talbiya at this  place.
