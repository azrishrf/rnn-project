30|5773|  Chapter: Concerning the characteristic features of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him),  and he was the most handsome of the people.  Qatada reported: I asked Anas b. Malik: How was the hair of Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him)? Thereupon he said: His hair was neither very curly nor very  straight, and they hung over his shoulders and earlobes.
