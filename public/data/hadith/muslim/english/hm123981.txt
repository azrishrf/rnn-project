12|3981|  Chapter: Al-‘Umra (Life grant).  This hadith is narrated on the authority of Jabir through other chains of transmitters, but  (with this addition of words) that thehadith transmitted on the authority of Ayyub (these  words are found): “The Helpers (Ansar) conferred the benefit of ‘Umra, upon the  Emigrants (Muhajirin), whereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said:  Keep your property to yourselves.
