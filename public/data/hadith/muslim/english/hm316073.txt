31|6073|  Chapter: The merits of Hassan b. Thabit (Allah be pleased with him).  ‘Abd al-Rahman reported that he heard Hassin b. Thabit al-Ansari call Abu Huraira to  bear witness by saying: I adjure you by Allah if you had not heard Allah’s Apostle (may  peace be upon him) saying: Hassin, give a reply on behalf of the Messenger of Allah. O  Allah, help him with Ruh-ul-Qudus. Abu Huraira said: Yes, it is so.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
