19|4324|  Chapter: Justification for cutting down the trees and burning them.  It is narrated on the authority of ‘Abdullah that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) ordered the date-palms of Banu Nadir to be burnt and cut. These palms were  at Buwaira. Qutaibah and Ibn Rumh in their versions of the tradition have added: So  Allah, the Glorious and Exalted, revealed the verse: “Whatever trees you have cut down  or left standing on their trunks, it was with the permission of Allah so that He may  disgrace the evil-doers” (lix. 5).
