1|286|  Chapter: The necessity of affirming the Prophethood of our Apostle Muhammad (may  peace be upon him) who is the Apostle sent to the whole of humanity. and the  abrogation of other religions with his religion.  This hadith has been narrated by another chain of transmitters like Abu Bakr b. Abi  Shaiba, ‘Abda b. Sulaiman Ibn Abi ‘Umar Sufyan, ‘Ubaidullah b. Mu’adh, Shu’ba; all of  them heard it from Salih b. Salih.
