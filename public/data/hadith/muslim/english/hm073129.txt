7|3129|  Chapter: The merit of Hajj and Umra and the day of ‘Arafa (the 9th of Dhu’l-Hijja).  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying. He who came to this House (Ka’ba) (with the intention of  performing Pilgrimage), and neither spoke indecently nor did he act wickedly, would  return (free from sin) as on the (very first day) his mother bore him.
