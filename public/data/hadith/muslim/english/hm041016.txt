4|1016|  Chapter: The excellence of the prayer and the way it is begun and the excellence of  Ruku’ and moderation in it and prostration and moderation in it, etc.  ‘Aun b. Abu Juhaifa narrated from the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) on the  authority of his father a Hadith like that of Sufyan, and ‘Umar b. Abu Za’ida made this  addition: Some of them tried to excel the others (in obtaining water), and in the Hadith  transmitted by Malik b. Mighwal (the words are): When it was noon, Bilal came out and  summoned (people) to (noon) prayer.
