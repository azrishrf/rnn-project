6|2595|  Chapter: Forbiddance to observe perpetual fast and excellence of observing fast on alternate  days.  ‘Abdullah b. ‘Amr (Allah be pleased with both of them) reported Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) as saying: With Allah the best fasting is that of David and the best  prayer is that of David (peace be upon him) for he slept half of the night and stood for  prayer for the third of it and (then) slept the sixth part of it and he observed fast one day  and broke on the other.
