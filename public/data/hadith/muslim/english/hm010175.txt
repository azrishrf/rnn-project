1|175|  Chapter: Prohibition of killing of an infidel after he say’s: “There is no god but Allah”.  It is narrated by Miqdad, and he was an ally of B. Zuhra and was of those who  participated in the Battle of Badr along with the Messenger of Allah, that he said:  Messenger of Allah, here is a point: If I happened to encounter a person amongst the  infidels (in the battle). Then he narrated a hadith similar to the one transmitted by Laith.
