41|6939|  Chapter: The Last Hour would not come until the fire emits from the earth of Hijaz.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) stood by the door  (of the apartment of) gafsa and, pointing towards the east, he said: The turmoil would  appear from this side, viz. where the horns of Satan would appear, and he uttered these  words twice or thrice and Ubaidullah b. Sa’ld in his narration said. The Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) had been standing by the door of ‘Aisha.
