25|5369|  Chapter: It is forbidden to peep into the house of another person.  Anas b. Malik reported that a person peeped in some of the holes (in the doors) of  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) (and he found him) standing up (lifting) an  arrow or some arrows. The narrator said: I perceived as if Allah’s Messenger (may  peace be upon him) was going to pierce (his eyes).
