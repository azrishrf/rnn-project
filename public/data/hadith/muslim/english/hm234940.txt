23|4940|  Chapter: It is forbidden to prepare Nabidh is varnished jar, gourd, green pitcher, and hollow  stumps.  Ibrahim b. Maisarah reported that he heard Tawus as saying: I was sitting with Ibn  ‘Umar when a man came to him, and said: Did Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) forbid the preparation of Nabidh in a green pitcher (besmeared with pitch), in  varnished jar and in gourd? Thereupon he said: Yes.
