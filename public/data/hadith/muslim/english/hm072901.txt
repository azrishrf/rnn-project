7|2901|  Chapter: Excellence of walking at a quick pace in Tawaf and Umra and on the occasion  of first Tawaf in Hajj.  This Hadith has been narrated on the authority of jurairi with the same chain of  transmitters but with a slight variation of words (and this is) that he (the narrator) did not  say: “They felt jealous of him but said: The people of Mecca, were jealous people.”
