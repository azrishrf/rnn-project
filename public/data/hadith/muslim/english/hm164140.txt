16|4140|  Chapter: The justification of Qisas (retribution) when one is killed with stone or any other heavy  thing and killing of a male for the murder of a female.  Anas reported that a Jew killed a girl of the Ansar for her ornaments and then threw her  in a well and smashed her head with a stone. He was caught and brought to the  Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him), and he commanded that he should be  stoned to death. So he was stoned until he died.  MURDERS, FIGHTING, REQUITAL
