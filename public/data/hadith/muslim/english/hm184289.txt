18|4289|  Chapter: Entertainment of the Guest.  ‘Uqba b. Amir reported: We said to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him): You  send us out and we come to the people who do not give us hospitality, so what is your  opinion? Thereupon Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: If you come to  the people who order for you what is befitting a guest, accept it; but if they do not take  from them what befits them to give to a guest.
