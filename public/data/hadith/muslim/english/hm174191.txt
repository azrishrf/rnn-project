17|4191|  Chapter: Prescribed punishment for an adulterer and an adulteress.  ‘Ubada b. as-Samit reported: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying:  Receive (teaching) from me, receive (teaching) from me. Allah has ordained a way for  those (women). When an unmarried male commits adultery with an unmarried female  (they should receive) one hundred lashes and banishment for one year. And in case of  married male committing adultery with a married female, they shall receive one hundred  lashes and be stoned to death.
