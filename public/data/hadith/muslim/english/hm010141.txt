1|141|  Chapter: The love of Ansar (helpers) and ‘All (may Allah be pleased with all of them) is  (an Ingredient) of Iman and (one of) its signs, and hatred against them is a sign of  dissemblance.         Zirr reported: ‘Ali observed: By Him Who split up the seed and created something living,  the Apostle(may peace and blessings be upon him) gave me a promise that no one but  a believer would love me, and none but a hypocrite would nurse grudge against me.
