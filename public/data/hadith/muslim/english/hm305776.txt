30|5776|  Chapter: Pertaining to the characteristic features of the face of Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) and his eyes and his heels.  Jabir b. Samura reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had a broad  face with reddish (wide) eyes, and lean heels. Shu’ba reported: I said to Simak: What  does this dali-ul-fam mean? And he said: This means broad face. I said: What does this  ashkal mean? He said: Long in the slit of the eye. I said: What is this manhus-ul- aqibain? He said: It implies little flesh at the heels.
