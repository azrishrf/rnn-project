9|3549|  Chapter: It is obligatory to abstain from adornment during the ‘Idda period, but  mourning beyond three days is prohibited.  ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  as saying: It is not permissible for a woman believing in Allah and the Hereafter to  observe mourning on the dead for more than three (days), except in case of her  husband.
