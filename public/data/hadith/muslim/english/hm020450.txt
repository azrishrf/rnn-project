2|450|  Chapter: The merit of Wudu and that of prayer after it.  Abu Huraira reported: Verily the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said:  Five (daily) prayers and from one Friday prayer to the (next) Friday prayer, and from  Ramadhan to Ramadhan are expiatious for the (sins) committed in between (their  intervals) provided one shuns the major sins.
