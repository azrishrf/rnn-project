5|2171|  Chapter: Exhortation to give Sadaqa.  Abu Dbarr reported: I went to the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him) and he was  sitting under the shade of the Ka’ba, and the rest of the Hadith it the same but for this  that he (the Holy Prophet) said: “By Allah, in Whose hand is my life, no person on earth  who dies and leaves behind camels, or cattle or goat and sheep, and does not pay  Zakat (would be spared the punishment).”
