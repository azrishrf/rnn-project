5|2218|  Chapter: The Labourer should give charity out of his wages and it is severely forbidden to  belittle one who contributes less.  Adi b. Hatim reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) made a  mention of the Fire and sought refuge (with Allah against it). He turned aside his face  three times and then said: Protect yourselves against Fire even if with half a date. But if  you fail to find it (then protect yourselves against Fire) with the help of a pleasant word).
