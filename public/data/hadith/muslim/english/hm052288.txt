5|2288|  Chapter: Excellence of abstaining from begging and that of endurance.  Abu Saâ€™id al-Khudri reported that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)  stood up and addressed the people thus: O people, by Allah, I do not entertain fear  about you in regard to anything else than that which Allah would bring forth for you in  the form of adornment of the world. A person said: Messenger of Allah, does good  produce evil? The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) remained silent for a  while and he then said: What did you say? He replied: Messenger of Allah, I said: Does  good produce evil? The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him)said to him: The  good does not produce but good. but among the plants the spring rain produces There  some which kill with a tremor or nearly kill all but the animal which feeds on vegetation.  It eats and when its flanks are distended, it faces the can, then when it has donged or  urinated and chewed it returns and eats. He who accepts wealth rightly, Allah confers  blessing on it for him and he who takes wealth without any right, he is like one who eats  and is not satisfied.
