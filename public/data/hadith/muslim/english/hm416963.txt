41|6963|  Chapter: The Last Hour would not come until a person would pass by a grave and wish that he  should have been the occupant of that grave because of this calamity.  Abu Sa’id reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace he upon him) I said: There  would be amongest your caliphs a caliph who would give handfuls of wealth to the, but  wbuld not count it. In. the narration transmitted on the authority of Ibn Hujr, there is a  slight variation of wording.
