41|7051|  Chapter: Approach of the Last Hour.  Anas reported that a person asked Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as to  when the Last Hour would come. He had in his presence a young boy of the Ansar who  was called Mahammad, Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said: If this young  boy lives, he may not grow very old till (he would see) the Last Hour coming to you.
