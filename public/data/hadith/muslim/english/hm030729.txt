3|729|  Chapter: What should be uttered while entering the privy ?.  Anas reported: When the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) entered the  privy, and in the Hadith transmitted by Hushaim (the words are): When the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) entered the lavatory, be used to say: O Allah, I seek  refuge in Thee from wicked and noxious things.
