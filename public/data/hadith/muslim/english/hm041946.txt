4|1946|  Chapter: Permission for sport on ‘Id days in which there in no sin.  Abu Huraira reported: While the Abyssinians were busy playing with their arms in the  presence of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) ‘Umar b. Khattab came  there. He bent down to take up pebbles to throw at them (in order to make them go off).  The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said to him: ‘Umar, leave them alone.
