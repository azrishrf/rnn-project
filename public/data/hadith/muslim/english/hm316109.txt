31|6109|  Chapter: The merits of the settlements of the Ansar).  Abu Usaid Ansari reported that he heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him)  as saying: The worthiest of the Ansar or the worthiest of the settlements and the clans  of Ansar; the rest of the hadith is the same, but there is no mention of the story of Sa’d  b. ‘Ubida (Allah be pleased with him).         HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
