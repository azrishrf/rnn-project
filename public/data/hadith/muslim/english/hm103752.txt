10|3752|  Chapter: Remission in the payment of yield stricken by calamity.  Abdullah b. al-Sa’ib reported: We visited ‘Abdullah b. Ma’qil and asked him about  sharing of crops, whereupon he said: Thabit alleged that Allah’s Messenger (may peace  be upon him) forbade Muzara’a and commanded leasing it out on rent (for money) and  said: There is no harm in it.
