6|2635|  Chapter: Excellence of Lailat-ul Qadar and exhortation to find it.  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported: We were talking about Lailat-ul-Qadr  in the presence of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) and he said: He         who amongst you remembers (the night) when the moon arose and it was like a piece  of plate (at the fag end of the month in a state of waning).
