26|5400|  Chapter: It is prohibited to sit with a strange lady in privacy or to enter her house when she is  alone.         ‘Uqba b. Amir reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Beware  of getting, into the houses and meeting women (in seclusion). A person from the Ansir  said: Allah’s Messenger, what about husband’s brother, whereupon he said: Husband’s  brother is like death.
