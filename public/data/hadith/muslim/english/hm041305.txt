4|1305|  Chapter: Desirability of observing the noon prayer at the earlier hour (of times prescribed for it)  when there is no intense heat.         This Hadith has been reported by ‘Auza’i with the same chain of transmitters: We used  to slaughter the camel during the lifetime of the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) after the ‘Asr prayer, but he made no mention of: “We used to pray along with  him.”
