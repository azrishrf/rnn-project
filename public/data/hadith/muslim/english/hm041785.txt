4|1785|  Chapter: Excellence of one who acts upon (the teachings of the) Qur’an and one who  teaches it.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: Gabriel  taught me to recite in one style. I replied to him and kept asking him to give more  (styles), till he reached seven modes (of recitation). Ibn Shibab said: It has reached me  that these seven styles are essentially one, not differing about what is permitted and  what is forbidden.
