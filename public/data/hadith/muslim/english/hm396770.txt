39|6770|  Chapter: None would attain salvation because of his deeds but it is through Lord’s Mercy (that  he would attain that).  ‘Aisha, the wife of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him), reported that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) used to say: Observe moderation (in doing  deeds), and if you fail to observe it perfectly, try to do as much as you can do (to live up  to this ideal of moderation) and be happy for none would be able to get into Paradise  because of his deeds alone. They (the Companions of the Holy Prophet) said: Allah’s  Messenger, not even thou? Thereupon he said: Not even I, but that Allah wraps me in  His Mercy, and bear this in mind that the deed loved most by Allah is one which is done  constantly even though it is insignificant.
