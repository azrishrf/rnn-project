20|4601|  Chapter: No migration after the conquest of Mecca but allegiance could be sworn (to a  rightful leader) on serving the cause of Islam, on fighting in the way of Allah and on  following the path of virtue.  This tradition has been handed down through a different chain of transmitter with the  addition of the following words at the end: “Do you milk them on the day they arrive at  the water? He replied: Yes.”
