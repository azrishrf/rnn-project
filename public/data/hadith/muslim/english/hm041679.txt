4|1679|  Chapter: Encouragement to observe prayers during Ramadhan and that is Tarawih.  Ibn ‘Abbas reported: I spent a night in the house of my mother’s sister, Maimuna, and  then narrated (the rest of the) Hadith, but he made no mention of the washing of his  face and two hands but he only said: He then came to the water-skin and loosened its  straps and performed ablution between the two extremes, and then came to his bed and  slept. He then got up for the second time and came to the water skin and loosened its  straps and then performed ablution which was in fact an ablution (it was performed  well), and implored (the Lord) thus: “Give me abundant light,” and he made no retention  of: “Make me light.”
