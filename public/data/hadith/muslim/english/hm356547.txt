35|6547|  Chapter: Supplication at the time of sleeping and going to bed.  This hadith has been transmitted on the authority of al-Bara’ b. ‘Azib that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to a person: O, so and so, as you go to your  bed; the rest of the hadith is the same but with this variation of wording that he said:  “Thine Apostle whom Thou sent.” If you die that night you would die on Fitra and if you  get up in the morning you would get up with a bliss.
