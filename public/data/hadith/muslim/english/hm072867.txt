7|2867|  Chapter: Saying of people to Ibn ‘Abbas: What to this religious verdict of yours that has  engaged the attention of the people?  Abu Hassan al-A’raj reported that a person from Bani Hujaim said to Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah  be pleased with them): What is this religious verdict of yours which has engaged the  attention of the people or which has become a matter of dispute among them that he  who circumambulated the House can be free from Ihram? Thereupon he said: That is  the Sunnah of your Apostle (may peace be upon him), even though you may not  approve of it.
