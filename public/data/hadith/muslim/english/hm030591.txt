3|591|  Chapter: The menstruating woman is permitted to wash the head of her husband, comb  his hair, and her left-over is clean, and one is permitted to recline in her lap and recite  the Qur’an.  ‘Aisha reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) would recline in my  lap when I was menstruating, and recite the Qur’an.
