4|1487|  Chapter: The prayer of travellers and its shortening.  Ibn ‘Umar announced Adhan for prayer on a cold, windy night. Then added: Pray in your  dwellings; and then said: When it was a cold, rainy night, the Messenger of Allah (may  peace be upon him) used to command the Mu’adhdhin to say “Pray in your dwellings.”
