4|1093|  Chapter: Concerning the command of placing one’s hands on the knees while in Ruku’  and abrogation of al-Tatbiq.         Tawus reported: We asked Ibn Abbas about sitting on one’s buttocks (in prayer). (ala  alqad mein)He said: It is sunnah. We said to him: We find it a sort of cruelty to the foot.  Ibn ‘Abbas said: It is the sunnah of your Apostle (may peace be upon him).
