10|3635|  Chapter: Command pertaining to the selling of animal whose udder is tied up.  Abu Huraira (Allah be pleased with him) reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) as saying: He who buys a goat with its udder tied up has the option to retain  the goat if he so desires or return it within three days, and in case he returns it he  should do so along with a sa’ of dates.
