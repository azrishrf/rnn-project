1|30|  Chapter: Command for fighting against the people so long as they do not profess that  there is no god but Allah and Muhammad is His Messenger.  It is reported on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah said: I have  been commanded to fight against people so long as they do not declare that there is no  god but Allah, and he who professed it was guaranteed the protection of his property  and life on my behalf except for the right affairs rest with Allah.
