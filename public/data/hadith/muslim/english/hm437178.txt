43|7178|  Chapter: Pertaining to the Verse: “Has not the time yet come for the Believers that their  Hearts should be humble for the Remembrance of Allah? ” (lvii. 16).  Ibn Mas’ud said: Since our acceptance of Islam and the revelation of this verse in which  Allah has shown annoyance to us: “Has not the time yet come for the believers that their  hearts should be humble for the remembrance of Allah?” (lvii. 16), there was a gap of  four years.
