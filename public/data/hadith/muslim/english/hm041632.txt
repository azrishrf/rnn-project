4|1632|  Chapter: Forenoon prayer should be offered when it is sufficiently hot.  Ibn ‘Umar reported that a person asked the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon  him) about the night prayer. The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said:  Prayer during the night should consist of pairs of rak’ahs, but if one of you fears morning  is near, he should pray one rak’ah which will make his prayer an odd number for him.
