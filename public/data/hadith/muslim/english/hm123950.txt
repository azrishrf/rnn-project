12|3950|  Chapter: Disapproval of buying the donated property by the donor himself.  Zaid b. Aslam reported on the authority of his father that ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with  him) donated a horse in the path of Allah. He found that it had languished in the hand of  its possessor, and he was a man of meagre resources He (Hadrat ‘Umar) intended to  buy it. He came to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and made a mention of  that to him, whereupon he said: Don’t buy that even if you get it for a dirham for he who  gets back the charity is like a dog which swallows its vomit.
