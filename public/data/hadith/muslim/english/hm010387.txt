1|387|  Chapter: Pertaining to the words of the Apostle of Allah (may peace be upon him): “I  would be the first among people to Intercede in the Paradise and among the Apostles I  would have the largest following”.  ‘Amr b. Abu Sufyan transmitted a hadith like this from Abu Huraira who narrated it from  the Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him).
