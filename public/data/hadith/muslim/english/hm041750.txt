4|1750|  Chapter: Excellence of the reciting of the Qur’an by one who is more skilled and  proficient before one who is inferior to him.  ‘Abdullah (b. Mas’ud) reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon (him)  asked me to recite the Qur’an. He said: Messenger of Allah, (how) should I recite to you  whereas it has been sent down to you? He (the Holy Prophet) said: I desire to hear it  from someone else. So I recited Surat Al-Nisa’ till I reached the verse: How then shall it  be when We shall bring from every people a witness and bring you against them as a  witness?” (verse 41). I lifted my head or a person touched me in my side, and so I lifted  my head and saw his tears falling (from the Holy Prophet’s eyes).
