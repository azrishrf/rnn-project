26|5469|  Chapter: There is a remedy for every malady and its excellence to get treatment.  Jabir reported that Umm Salama sought permission from Allah’s messenger (may  Allah’s peace upon him) for getting herself cupped. The Apostle of Allah (may peace be  upon him) asked Abu Taiba to cup her. He (Jabir) said: I think he (Abu Taiba) was her  faster brother or a young boy before entering upon the adolescent period.
