9|3493|  Chapter: Pronouncement of three divorces.  Abu al-Sahba’ said to Ibn ‘Abbas: Enlighten us with your information whether the three  divorces (pronounced at one and the same time) were not treated as one during the  lifetime of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) and Abu Bakr. He said: It was in  fact so, but when during the caliphate of ‘Umar (Allah be pleased with him) people  began to pronounce divorce frequently, he allowed them to do so (to treat  pronouncements of three divorces in a single breath as one).
