1|225|  Chapter: Pertaining to the virtuous act of a man before embracing Islam.         Hisham b. Urwa narrated it on the authority of his father: Hakim b. Hizam freed one  hundred slave and donated one hundred camels (for the sake of Allah) during the state  of ignorance. Then he freed one hundred slaves and donated one hundred camel(for  the sake of Allah) after) he had embraced Islam. He subsequently came to the Apostle  (may peace be upon him). The rest of the hadith is the same as narrated above.
