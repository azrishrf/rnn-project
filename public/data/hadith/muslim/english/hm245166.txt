24|5166|  Chapter: The use of gold and silver vessels to forbidden to men and women and it is  also prohibited to drink in them.         Uqba b. ‘Amir said: A silk go vn was presented to Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) and he wore it and observed prayer in it and then returned and put it off so  violently as if he despised it. He then said: It does not befit the God-fearing persons.
