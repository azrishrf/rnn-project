4|2044|  Chapter: Washing of the dead body.  A Hadith like this has been transmitted by Hafsa on the authority of Umm ‘Atiyya with  the exception (of these words that the Holy Prophet asked them to wash her dead  body): “three times, five times, seven times, or more than that, if you deem fit:” Hafsa  (further) said on the authority of Umm ‘Atiyya: We braided (the hair) of her head in three  plaits.
