4|1536|  Chapter: Excellence to be on the right side of the Imam.  Ibn Buhaina reported: The dawn prayer had commenced when the Messenger of Allah  (may peace be upon him) saw a person observing prayer, whereas the Mu’adhdhin had  pronounced the Iqama. Upon this he (the Holy Prophet) remarked: Do you say four  (rak’ahs) of Fard in the dawn prayer?
