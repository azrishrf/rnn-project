8|3280|  Chapter: Mubrim (one who is in the state of Ihram) is forbidden to marry and the disapproval of  giving the proposal (in this state).  Uthman b. ‘Affan reported that Allah’s Messenger (May peace be upon him) had said: A  Muhrim should neither marry himself, nor should he be got married to anyone, nor  should he make the proposal of marriage.
