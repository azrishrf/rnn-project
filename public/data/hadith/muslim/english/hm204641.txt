20|4641|  Chapter: Merit of leaving for Jihad in the morning and evening.  It has been narrated on the authority of Sahl b. Sa’d as-Sa’idi that the Messenger of  Allah (may peace be upon him) said: A journey undertaken in the morning or evening  (fond Jihad) in the way of Allah (will merit a reward) better than the world and all that is  in it.
