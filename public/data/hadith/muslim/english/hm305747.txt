30|5747|  Chapter: The mercy and compassion of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) for women  and his command to the camel-driver to drive the mount slowly on which they ride.  Anas reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) had a camel-driver who  had a very melodious voice. Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) said to him:  Anjasha, drive slowly; do not break the vessels of glass, meaning the weak women.  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
