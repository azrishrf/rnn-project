1|248|  Chapter: Pertaining to evil suggestion or prompting in faith and what should be said  when it occurs to the mind of a man.  Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger of Allah (may peace be upon him) said to me:  they (the people) till constantly ask you, Abu Huraira, (about different things pertaining  to religion) the they would say: Well, there is Allah, but after all who created Allah? He  (Abu Huraira) narrated: Once we were in the mosque that some of the Bedouins came  there and said: Well, there is Allah, but who created Allah? He (the narrator) said: I took  hold of the pebbles in my fist and flung at them and remarked: Stand up, stand up (go  away) my friend (the Holy Prophet) told the truth.
