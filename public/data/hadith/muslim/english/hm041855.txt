4|1855|  Chapter: Observing of silence in sermon on Friday.  Abu Burda b. Abu Musa al-Ash’ari reported: ‘Abdullah b. Umar said to me: Did you hear  anything from your father narrating something from the messenger of Allah (may peace  be upon him) about the time on Friday? I said: Yes, I heard him say from the Messenger  of Allah (may peace be upon him) (these words): “It is between the time when the Imam  sits down and the end of the prayer.”
