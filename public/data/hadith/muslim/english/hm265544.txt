26|5544|  Chapter: Killing of snakes.         Salim, on the authority of his father, reported Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him)  as saying: Kill the snakes having stripes over them and short-tailed snakes, for these  two types cause miscarriage (of a pregnant woman) and they affect the eyesight  adversely. So Ibn ‘Umar used to kill every snake that he found. Abu Lubaba b. ‘Abd al- Mundhir and Zaid b. Khattab saw him pursuing a snake, whereupon he said: They were  forbidden (to kill) those snakes who live in houses.
