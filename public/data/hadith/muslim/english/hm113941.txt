11|3941|  Chapter: The last verse revealed was that pertaining to Kalala.  Abu Ishaq said that he heard al-Bara’ b. ‘Azib (Allah be pleased with him)say: The last  complete sura revealed (in the Holy Qur’an) is Sura Tauba (i e. al-Bara’at, ix.), and the  last verse revealed is that pertaining to Kalala.
