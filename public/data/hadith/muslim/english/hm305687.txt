30|5687|  Chapter: The cistern of our Apostle (may peace be upon him) and its characteristics.  Umm Salama reported that she heard Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him) saying this as he  was sitting on the pulpit and she was getting her hair combed. (He uttered these words): “O  people.” And she said to one who was combing:  Leave  my  head;  the  rest  of  the  hadith  is  the  same.  (PBUH) AND HIS COMPANIONS
