32|6230|  Chapter: Merit of visiting the sick.  Thauban, the freed slave of Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him), reported  Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) as saying: He who visits the sick is  supposed to remain in the fruit garden of Paradise. It was said: Allah’s Messenger, what  is this Khurfat-ul-jannah? He said: It is a place abounding in fruits.         OF RELATIONSHIP
