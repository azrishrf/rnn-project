7|2824|  Chapter: Permissibility of the Tamattu’ (form of pilgrimage).  Mutarrif reported: ‘Imran b. Husain said to me: Should I not narrate to you a Hadith  today by which Allah will benefit you subsequently-and bear in mind that Allah’s  Messenger (may peace be upon him) made some members of his family perform ‘Umra  within ten days of Dhu’l-Hijja. No verse was revealed to abrogate that, and he (the Holy  Prophet) did not refrain from doing it till he died. So after him everyone said as he liked,  (but it would be his personal opinion and not the verdict of the Shari’ah).
