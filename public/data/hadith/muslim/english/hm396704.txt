39|6704|  Chapter: Description of the day of judgement, paradise and hell.  Abdullah b. ‘Umar reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) saying: Allah,  the Exalted and Glorious, would fold the Heavens on the Day of Judgment and then He  would place them on His tight hand and say: I am the Lord; where are the haughty and  where are the proud (today)? He would fold the’ earth (placing it) on the left hand and  say: I am the Lord; where are the haughty and where are the proud (today)?         PARADISE AND HELL
