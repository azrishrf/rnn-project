32|6349|  Chapter: The prohibition of pride and vanity.  Abu Sa’id Khudri and Abu Huraira reported Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) as saying: Allah, the Exalted and Glorious, said: Glory is His lower garment and  Majesty is His cloak and (Allah says,) He who contends with Me in regard to them I shall  torment him.
