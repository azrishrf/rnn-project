35|6541|  Chapter: Seeking of refuge in Allah from the evil of destiny and from the severity of calamity.  Khaula bint Hakim Sulamiyya reported: I heard Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon  him) as saying: When anyone lands at a place, and then says: “I seek refuge in the  Perfect Word of Allah from the evil of what He has created,” nothing would harm him  until he marches from that stopping place.
