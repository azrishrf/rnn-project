30|5780|  Chapter: Pertaining to the old age of Allah’s Apostle (may peace be upon him).  Ibn Sirin reported: I asked Anas b. Malik whether Allah’s Messenger (may peace be  upon him) dyed his hair. He said: He had not reached the stage when (he needed)  dyeing (of his white hair). He had a few white hair in his beard. I said to him: Did Abu  Bakr dye his hair? He said: Yes, with hina’ (henna).
