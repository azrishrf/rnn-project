31|5985|  Chapter: The merits of ‘Aisha (Allah be pleased with her).  ‘Aisha reported that Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) during his last illness)  inquired: Where I would be tomorrow, where I would be tomorrow (thinking, that the turn  of ‘Aisha was not very near) and when it was my turn, Allah called him to his Heavenly  Home and his head was between my neck and chest.  HOLY PROPHET (PBUH)
