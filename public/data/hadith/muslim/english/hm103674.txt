10|3674|  Chapter: Prohibition of the sale of fruits until they are clearly in good condition.         Abu Bakhtari reported: I asked Ibn ‘Abbas (Allah be pleased with them) about the sale  of dates. He said: Allah’s Messenger (may peace be upon him) forbade the sale of  dates of the trees until one eats them or they are eaten (i.e. they are fit to be eaten) or  until they are weighed (or measured). I said: What does it imply: “Until it is weighed”?  Thereupon a person who was with him (Ibn Abbas) said: Until he is able to keep it with  him (after plucking them).
