1|270|  Chapter: Islam was initiated as something strange, and it would revert to its (old  position) of being strange, and it would concentrate between the two mosques.  It is narrated on the authority of Abu Huraira that the Messenger of Allah (may peace be  upon him) said: Islam initiated as something strange, and it would revert to its (old  position) of being strange, so good tidings for the stranger.
