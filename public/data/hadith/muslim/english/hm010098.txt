1|98|  Chapter: Din is sincerity and well-wishing.  It is narrated on the authority of Tamim ad-Dari that the Apostle of Allah (may peace  and blessings be upon him) observed: Al-Din is a name of sincerity and well wishing.  Upon this we said: For whom? He replied: For Allah, His Book, His Messenger and for  the leaders and the general Muslims.
